故事

開場：0.97 AUROC 之後的一封信

情境

🚨 「為什麼我兒子的血氧 88% 還被分到非緊急？」

一支機器學習團隊在一家美國大型醫院開發了 sepsis 預測模型，內部 AUROC 0.97。論文發表在頂級期刊，被三家醫院引進部署。六個月後，一封信寄到開發者：「我兒子 19 歲，黑人，血氧 88%、心率 132，他在急診被你的模型評為『非緊急』，等了 4 小時才見到醫師。他現在 ICU 第 11 天，雙肺炎合併敗血症。為什麼？」

團隊調出資料：模型訓練資料 85% 是白人；對黑人病人的 sensitivity 0.61，對白人 0.78；vital sign baseline 對種族未做調整（黑人 baseline heart rate 略高被視為「正常」）；training 時 LOS 是 feature → outcome leakage；race 雖未被當 feature，但 ZIP code 與保險型態實質代表了種族（fairness through unawareness 失敗）。他們重新訓練、加入 fairness constraint、設置 monitoring、發 retraction、改 Model Card 公開所有限制。但一條生命已被改寫。

這個故事不是虛構——它的原型是 2019 Obermeyer et al. Science 揭露的 Optum 健康風險演算法事件，影響全美 2 億人。本章的所有規則、指引、技術，最後都要回到這個問題：當你的模型錯了，誰會付出代價？

A team built a sepsis predictor at a large US hospital, internal AUROC 0.97. The paper hit a top journal; three hospitals deployed it. Six months later: "My 19-year-old Black son had SpO₂ 88%, HR 132. Your model rated him 'non-urgent.' He waited 4 hours. He's now Day 11 in the ICU with bilateral pneumonia and sepsis. Why?"

The team digs in: training data was 85% White; sensitivity 0.61 for Black patients vs 0.78 for White; vital-sign baselines weren't race-adjusted (Black baseline HR slightly higher counted as "normal"); LOS was a feature → outcome leakage; race was dropped but ZIP code + insurance encoded it anyway (fairness-through-unawareness fails). They retrain, add fairness constraints, set monitoring, post a retraction, publish a Model Card disclosing all limits. But a life is already rewritten.

This isn't fiction — its template is Obermeyer et al. Science 2019, the Optum risk-score audit affecting 200M Americans. Every rule, guideline, and technique in this chapter ultimately answers one question: when your model is wrong, who pays?

術語

術語小卡：HIPAA 到 Model Card

HIPAA Privacy Rule
健康資訊隱私規則
美國的「醫療資料保密法」，1996 通過。
The US "medical data secrecy law," passed 1996.
45 CFR Parts 160 + 164；定義 PHI、covered entities、permitted uses；Safe Harbor (18 identifiers) 或 Expert Determination 兩種 de-identification 路徑。
45 CFR Parts 160 + 164; defines PHI, covered entities, permitted uses; two de-identification routes: Safe Harbor (18 identifiers) or Expert Determination.
Safe Harbor / 18 Identifiers
安全港 / 18 項識別碼
把姓名、生日、地址、社安號等 18 種標籤全拿掉就算「去識別化」。
Remove the 18 obvious labels (name, DOB, address, SSN…) and the data counts as "de-identified."
含 ZIP (3-digit only, >20k pop)、age < 90、所有 dates 只到年；剩餘 quasi-identifiers 仍可能 re-identifiable (Sweeney 2000)。
Includes ZIP (3-digit only, >20k pop), age < 90, dates to year only; residual quasi-identifiers can still re-identify (Sweeney 2000).
GDPR Art. 9 / Art. 22
歐盟資料保護規則
健康資料屬「特殊類別」，原則上禁止；自動化決策必須允許「找人類審查」。
Health data is a "special category" — prohibited unless excepted; automated decisions must allow human review.
GDPR 2016/679；Art. 9 禁止健康/種族/性取向/生物特徵處理除非明確同意或公共利益；Art. 22 right to human review；Art. 35 DPIA 評估。
GDPR 2016/679; Art. 9 prohibits health/race/sexual-orientation/biometric processing except with explicit consent or public-interest exception; Art. 22 right to human review; Art. 35 DPIA.
Differential Privacy (DP)
差分隱私
在資料的答案上加一點亂數，讓壞人猜不出單一個人。
Add a calibrated bit of random noise so an attacker can't pin down any individual.
Dwork 2006；ε-DP 保證 P(M(D)∈S) ≤ e^ε · P(M(D')∈S)；Laplace / Gaussian mechanism；ε ≤ 1 為強隱私、ε ≥ 10 弱。
Dwork 2006; ε-DP guarantees P(M(D)∈S) ≤ e^ε · P(M(D')∈S); Laplace / Gaussian mechanisms; ε ≤ 1 strong, ε ≥ 10 weak.
k-anonymity / l-diversity
k-匿名 / l-多樣性
每筆資料至少和其他 k-1 筆「長得一樣」，無法被獨自挑出。
Each record looks like at least k-1 others on quasi-identifiers, so it can't be singled out.
Sweeney 2002 k-anonymity；Machanavajjhala 2007 l-diversity（每 k-group 至少 l 個 sensitive value）；t-closeness 2007 進一步要求 distribution 接近 overall。
Sweeney 2002 k-anonymity; Machanavajjhala 2007 l-diversity (≥ l sensitive values per k-group); t-closeness 2007 requires distribution close to overall.
Demographic Parity
人口統計均等
不同族群被預測為「陽性」的比例相同。
Different groups have the same rate of being predicted "positive."
P(Ŷ=1|A=a) = P(Ŷ=1|A=b)；不條件於 y_true；可能與 accuracy 衝突 (base rate disparity)。
P(Ŷ=1|A=a) = P(Ŷ=1|A=b); unconditional on y_true; can conflict with accuracy under base-rate disparity.
Equalized Odds / Equal Opportunity
機率均等 / 機會均等
真實有病的人，不分族群都該以同樣機率被找到。
Truly sick patients should be caught at the same rate across groups.
Hardt 2016 NeurIPS；Equalized Odds 要求各組 TPR 與 FPR 都相等；Equal Opportunity 只要求 TPR (弱化版)。
Hardt 2016 NeurIPS; Equalized Odds requires equal TPR + FPR per group; Equal Opportunity only TPR (weaker).
Disparate Impact / 4/5 Rule
差別影響 / 4/5 法則
弱勢族群的「陽性率 ÷ 優勢族群陽性率」< 0.8 就算差別影響。
If unprivileged-group positive-rate ÷ privileged-group positive-rate < 0.8, it's "disparate impact."
US EEOC 1978 Uniform Guidelines；DI = P(Ŷ=1|A=u)/P(Ŷ=1|A=p)；0.8 ≤ DI ≤ 1.25 為合規區間。
US EEOC 1978 Uniform Guidelines; DI = P(Ŷ=1|A=u)/P(Ŷ=1|A=p); compliant zone 0.8 ≤ DI ≤ 1.25.
SHAP / LIME
模型解釋方法
告訴你每個特徵對最終預測的貢獻有多少。
Tell you how much each feature contributed to the prediction.
Lundberg-Lee 2017 NeurIPS SHAP (Shapley value)；Ribeiro 2016 KDD LIME；皆為 post-hoc 解釋，與本質可解釋模型 (Rudin 2019) 觀點對立。
Lundberg-Lee 2017 NeurIPS SHAP (Shapley values); Ribeiro 2016 KDD LIME; both post-hoc, contra Rudin 2019's "inherently interpretable" stance.
FAIR Principles
FAIR 資料原則
資料要能被找得到、拿得到、能對接、能再用。
Data must be Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, Reusable.
Wilkinson et al. 2016 Sci Data；FAIR Maturity Indicators 2019；NIH DMS 2023 Policy 要求公開 metadata + DOI。
Wilkinson et al. 2016 Sci Data; FAIR Maturity Indicators 2019; NIH DMS 2023 Policy mandates public metadata + DOI.
EU AI Act 2024/1689
歐盟人工智慧法
2024 通過，2026 起對通用 AI、2027 起對高風險 AI 全面適用。
Adopted 2024; 2026 enforces GPAI, 2027 enforces high-risk AI.
Risk-based：unacceptable / high / limited / minimal；Annex III 列高風險領域（含醫療）；GPAI 含 frontier-LLM 透明度報告與訓練摘要。
Risk-based: unacceptable / high / limited / minimal; Annex III lists high-risk domains (incl. medical); GPAI obligations cover frontier-LLM transparency reports and training-data summaries.
Model Card / Datasheet
模型卡 / 資料表
每個 AI 模型的「成分標示」——資料來源、限制、可預期錯誤模式。
An AI model's "nutrition label" — data source, limits, expected failure modes.
Mitchell 2019 ACM FAccT Model Cards；Gebru 2021 CACM Datasheets；2024 TRIPOD-AI BMJ + 2025 STARD-AI Nat Med 把這些升級為發表標準。
Mitchell 2019 ACM FAccT Model Cards; Gebru 2021 CACM Datasheets; 2024 TRIPOD-AI BMJ + 2025 STARD-AI Nat Med upgrade these into publication standards.

概覽

四個支柱：法遵 / 公平 / 可解釋 / 再現

一個合格的生醫資料探勘專案，至少要同時滿足四件事：(1) 法律合規（HIPAA、GDPR、EU AI Act、本地 IRB）；(2) 公平性（不對受保護族群造成系統性差別）；(3) 可解釋（醫療決策必須可被質問）；(4) 可再現（同樣資料 + 同樣程式 → 同樣結果）。

2026/8/2 起 EU AI Act 對 GPAI 模型生效；2027/8/2 對「高風險」AI（含多數醫療診斷模型）全面適用。 FDA 的 SaMD / PCCP（Predetermined Change Control Plan，2024 final）也已成為部署 AI 醫材的必經流程。

A defensible biomedical mining project satisfies four constraints simultaneously: (1) legal compliance (HIPAA, GDPR, EU AI Act, local IRB); (2) fairness (no systematic disparate impact on protected groups); (3) explainability (clinical decisions must be auditable); (4) reproducibility (same data + same code → same result).

EU AI Act: 2026-08-02 enforcement for GPAI; 2027-08-02 for "high-risk" AI (which covers most clinical diagnostic models). FDA SaMD / PCCP (Predetermined Change Control Plan, finalized 2024) is now mandatory for AI/ML-enabled medical devices.

比喻

隱私 = 上鎖的日記、公平 = 應徵面試

隱私像一本上鎖的日記：HIPAA Safe Harbor 是把封面標籤撕掉（名字、社安號）；差分隱私是再灑上墨水，讓即使有專家想拼湊也猜不出某一頁是誰的——ε 是允許的墨水濃度。公平像應徵面試：你想要「同樣能力的應徵者，不管種族性別都拿到同樣機會」——這是 equal opportunity；「整體錄取率不同族群相同」是 demographic parity；兩者數學上有時不可能同時滿足 (Chouldechova 2017)。

Privacy is a locked diary. HIPAA Safe Harbor tears off the obvious label (name, SSN); differential privacy adds calibrated ink so no expert can reconstruct any one page — ε is the ink concentration allowed. Fairness is a job interview: you want "equally qualified applicants get equal chances regardless of race/sex" — that's equal opportunity; "overall hire rate equal across groups" is demographic parity; mathematically the two can be incompatible (Chouldechova 2017).

公平性指標

二、四個常見公平性指標

⚖️

Demographic Parity

P(Ŷ=1|A=0) = P(Ŷ=1|A=1)。各族群的「被預測為陽性」比例相同；忽略真實 base rate。

P(Ŷ=1|A=0) = P(Ŷ=1|A=1). Equal positive-prediction rates across groups; ignores true base rate.

🎯

Equalized Odds

各族群的 TPR 與 FPR 都要相等；考慮真實標籤。

Equal TPR and FPR across groups; conditioned on the true label.

🪜

Equal Opportunity

只要求 TPR 相等（對真實陽性者公平），是 Equalized Odds 的弱化版。

Only TPR equal across groups (fair to true positives); weakened Equalized Odds.

📐

Disparate Impact

比值形式：DI = P(Ŷ=1|A=u)/P(Ŷ=1|A=p)，4/5 規則 ⇒ 0.8 ≤ DI ≤ 1.25。

Ratio form: DI = P(Ŷ=1|A=u)/P(Ŷ=1|A=p); four-fifths rule ⇒ 0.8 ≤ DI ≤ 1.25.

完整實例

稽核一個再住院模型的公平性

FA

從 0.82 accuracy 到 0.04 disparity 的 6 步

情境：上線前審查
Step 1
分層：把 hold-out 集按 race (Black / White / Asian / Hispanic) × sex × insurance (Medicare / Medicaid / Private / Uninsured) 切分。先檢查每組樣本數，避免某些組過小無法估計（rule of thumb: 每組 ≥ 100）。
Stratify: split the hold-out by race (Black / White / Asian / Hispanic) × sex × insurance (Medicare / Medicaid / Private / Uninsured). Check sample size per group (rule of thumb: ≥ 100 per cell).
Step 2
每組混淆矩陣：計算 TPR / FPR / PPV / NPV per subgroup；得到 TPR_White=0.78, TPR_Black=0.61, TPR_Hispanic=0.69, TPR_Asian=0.74。
Per-group confusion matrices: compute TPR / FPR / PPV / NPV; obtain TPR_White=0.78, TPR_Black=0.61, TPR_Hispanic=0.69, TPR_Asian=0.74.
Step 3
量化差距：Equal Opportunity violation: max-min TPR = 0.78 - 0.61 = Δ = 0.17（業界常以 ≤ 0.10 為合規）。DI = P(Ŷ=1|Black) / P(Ŷ=1|White) = 0.58 → 違反 4/5 rule。 用 fairlearn.metrics.MetricFrame 一次跑出所有指標。
Quantify gap: Equal Opportunity violation: max-min TPR = 0.78 - 0.61 = Δ = 0.17 (industry threshold ≤ 0.10). DI = P(Ŷ=1|Black) / P(Ŷ=1|White) = 0.58 → 4/5 rule violated. Use fairlearn.metrics.MetricFrame to compute all metrics at once.
Step 4
根因分析：(a) 訓練資料 race 分布：White 76% / Black 12% / Hispanic 8% / Asian 4% → severe imbalance；(b) ZIP code 與 insurance 與 race 高度相關 → 即使 drop race，模型仍能 indirectly proxy；(c) base rate 不同 (Black readmission ~22% vs White ~16%) → 部分差距是真實 base rate。
Root cause: (a) training race distribution White 76% / Black 12% / Hispanic 8% / Asian 4% — severe imbalance; (b) ZIP code + insurance highly correlated with race → dropping race doesn't help (proxies remain); (c) base rates differ (Black readmission ~22% vs White ~16%) — part of the gap is base-rate driven.
Step 5
加入公平性約束：用 fairlearn.reductions.ExponentiatedGradient(LogisticRegression(), constraints=EqualizedOdds()) 重訓練；或 Hardt 2016 post-processing thresholding 法（per-group threshold 調整）。
Add fairness constraint: retrain with fairlearn.reductions.ExponentiatedGradient(LogisticRegression(), constraints=EqualizedOdds()); or use Hardt 2016 post-processing thresholding (per-group threshold tuning).
Step 6
結果：準確率從 0.82 → 0.79 (-3%)，但 TPR Δ 從 0.17 → 0.04，DI 從 0.58 → 0.91。Stakeholder review (含 community advisory board) 決定接受這個 trade-off：「3% 整體準確 vs 較不漏掉黑人病人」。發 Model Card 公開所有限制與決策。
Result: accuracy 0.82 → 0.79 (-3%), but TPR Δ 0.17 → 0.04, DI 0.58 → 0.91. Stakeholder review (incl. community advisory board) accepts the trade-off: "3% accuracy for fewer missed Black patients." Publish a Model Card disclosing all limits and decisions.
🔬 進階：fairness-accuracy 數學不可能性

Chouldechova 2017 與 Kleinberg-Mullainathan-Raghavan 2017 證明：當 base rate 不同時，calibration、equalized odds、demographic parity 三者不可能同時滿足（除非完美預測或 base rate 相同）。這是個數學定理，不是技術問題。所以實務上要：(1) 選擇優先指標（通常 equal opportunity 或 EO）；(2) 透明告訴所有 stakeholder 你犧牲了哪一個；(3) 由 community advisory board 做最終決策。

Chouldechova 2017 and Kleinberg-Mullainathan-Raghavan 2017 prove: when base rates differ, calibration, equalized odds, and demographic parity cannot all hold simultaneously (except trivially). It's a theorem, not engineering. So in practice: (1) pick one priority (often equal opportunity or EO); (2) be transparent about what you sacrifice; (3) let a community advisory board make the final call.

⚕️
案例

2019 Obermeyer Science — 影響 2 億美國人的演算法偏差

Ziad Obermeyer (UC Berkeley) 與 Brian Powers (Brigham) 等人 2019 Science 〈Dissecting racial bias in an algorithm used to manage the health of populations〉揭露：美國一個被 200+ 大型健康系統使用、影響 2 億人的演算法（推測為 Optum/UnitedHealth）用「醫療花費」當作「醫療需求」的 proxy。但結構性歧視導致黑人病人在同樣健康程度下花費較少（缺乏保險、不信任系統、地理不便），因此演算法把這些「真正需要照護」的黑人排除在高需求 cohort 之外。

作者估計：若改用真實健康變項取代花費，高需求黑人病人比例從 17.7% 翻倍到 46.5%。修正後，模型被多家公司採用。這個案例是當代 medical AI fairness 的分水嶺，激發了 2020 之後一系列 fairness audit、Model Card 政策與 EU AI Act 高風險範圍的擴張。

Obermeyer (UC Berkeley) and Powers (Brigham) et al. 2019 Science "Dissecting racial bias in an algorithm used to manage the health of populations" exposed: an algorithm used by 200+ health systems and influencing 200M Americans (widely understood to be Optum/UnitedHealth) used healthcare cost as a proxy for healthcare need. But structural discrimination means Black patients with equal disease incur less cost (less insurance, distrust, access), so the algorithm under-identified Black patients as "high need."

By switching to direct health proxies, the share of Black patients flagged for high-need care doubled from 17.7% to 46.5%. The fix was adopted across multiple vendors. The paper is the watershed moment for medical-AI fairness, catalyzing fairness audits, Model Card policies, and the broader scope of high-risk AI in the EU AI Act.

爭論

三個正在燒的爭論

爭論 1

公平 vs 準確 — 必須二擇一嗎？

當 base rate 不同時，Chouldechova 2017 / Kleinberg 2017 證明多重 fairness 指標不可能同時滿足。但 Wick et al. 2019 指出許多現實案例「Pareto frontier」很平緩，實際上犧牲很少準確就能大幅改善公平（如 Step 4-6 的 3% vs 0.13 改善）。
When base rates differ, Chouldechova 2017 / Kleinberg 2017 prove multiple fairness metrics can't hold simultaneously. But Wick et al. 2019 argue many real cases have a flat Pareto frontier — tiny accuracy loss buys large fairness gain (cf. our Step 4-6: 3% for 0.13).
實務建議：(1) 量化 Pareto frontier；(2) 與 stakeholder + community advisory board 商討 trade-off；(3) Model Card 公開所有取捨。
Practical: (1) quantify the Pareto frontier; (2) discuss trade-offs with stakeholders + community advisory boards; (3) disclose all trade-offs in the Model Card.
爭論 2

差分隱私在醫療 AI 實用嗎？

DP 是數學上最強的隱私保證，US Census 2020 已採用 (ε ≈ 19.61)。但醫療 AI 訓練常需 ε < 5 才有意義保護；在這 ε 下 accuracy 嚴重劣化 (Bagdasaryan 2019 證實 DP-SGD 對 minority class 影響更大)。Google Med-PaLM 2 paper 2023 提到 DP 仍是「未解的研究問題」。
DP is the strongest mathematical privacy guarantee — US Census 2020 used ε ≈ 19.61. But medical AI typically needs ε < 5 to be meaningful, where accuracy drops sharply (Bagdasaryan 2019: DP-SGD disproportionately hurts minority classes). Google Med-PaLM 2 paper 2023 calls DP "an open problem."
2026 折衷：federated learning + secure aggregation + 部分 DP (high ε on noisy stats only) 為現實路線；嚴格 DP 留給 census-style 公開統計，不用於 model training。
2026 compromise: federated learning + secure aggregation + partial DP (high ε on noisy stats only); strict DP stays for census-style public statistics, not for model training.
爭論 3

EU AI Act 2026/2027 — 阻礙創新還是保障病人？

支持者：終於有可執行的 AI 法律，迫使廠商做 risk-management、技術文件、上市後監測——這是病人的勝利。反對者：小型新創無力負擔合規成本 (估計 €300k+/年)，創新會外流到美國 / 中國。Schaake 2024 Nature 評論認為「pull from US」是現實風險。
Pro: finally an enforceable AI law — forces risk-management, technical docs, post-market monitoring — a win for patients. Con: small startups can't afford compliance (~€300k+/year), so innovation flees to US/China. Schaake 2024 Nature commentary notes the "pull from US" risk.
實證觀察：2024 既有醫療 AI 公司已開始準備 ISO 13485 + EU AI Act 雙合規；歐洲新創比例下降已有跡象；2026 啟動後的真實影響仍待觀察。
Empirical signs: in 2024 existing medical-AI companies are aligning ISO 13485 + EU AI Act dual compliance; declining EU startup share is observable; the true 2026 impact remains to be seen.
陷阱

五個倫理 / 再現性的常見陷阱

「不知種族」就是公平？

很多人以為「不用 race 當特徵就是公平」。但 ZIP code、保險型態、語言、姓氏、看診科別實質上 proxy race。dropping race 反而讓 bias 不可見、更難稽核。正解：保留 race 用於 audit，但 carefully constrain 訓練。

Many think "not using race = fairness." But ZIP code, insurance, language, surname, specialty proxy race. Dropping race hides bias and blocks audit. Better: keep race for audits, carefully constrain training.

把 DI 當因果

DI=0.6 表示「比例不同」，不直接代表演算法歧視——可能反映真實 base rate 差異、或更上游的結構問題。要做 causal analysis (Plečko 2022 fairadapt)。

DI=0.6 means "rates differ" — not direct proof of algorithmic discrimination. It may reflect real base-rate differences or upstream structural issues. Use causal analysis (Plečko 2022 fairadapt).

沒人口資料就說「公平」

「我們沒蒐集 race，所以模型不可能有種族偏差」——這是錯的邏輯。沒蒐集 ≠ 沒偏差，反而是無法稽核的偏差。所有合規論文都必須提供 demographic table。

"We didn't collect race so no racial bias is possible" — logically wrong. No collection ≠ no bias; it's un-auditable bias. Every compliant paper must include a demographic table.

只有 Model Card 沒 Datasheet

Mitchell 2019 Model Card 描述模型；Gebru 2021 Datasheet 描述資料集。少了 Datasheet，讀者無法評估資料的代表性、收集動機、清理過程。兩者缺一不可。

Mitchell 2019 Model Card describes the model; Gebru 2021 Datasheet describes the dataset. Without the datasheet, readers can't judge representativeness, motivation, cleaning. Both required.

「可再現」沒 Docker

只 commit Python 程式碼到 git，沒鎖 numpy / pandas / sklearn 版本，沒 Dockerfile → 半年後跑不出來。reproducibility 需 git + lockfile (uv/pip-tools/renv) + container (Docker/Apptainer) + workflow (Snakemake/Nextflow) 四件套。

Commit Python to git without locking numpy / pandas / sklearn versions and no Dockerfile → it won't run six months later. Reproducibility needs git + lockfile (uv/pip-tools/renv) + container (Docker/Apptainer) + workflow (Snakemake/Nextflow).

忽略外部驗證

許多臨床 AI 論文只報內部 5-fold CV AUROC 0.95，但外部一家新醫院 0.65。TRIPOD-AI 2024 + STARD-AI 2025 都把外部驗證列為必要。

Many clinical-AI papers report internal 5-fold CV AUROC 0.95, but 0.65 at the next hospital. TRIPOD-AI 2024 + STARD-AI 2025 make external validation mandatory.

常見錯誤陷阱 / Common Pitfalls

症狀：模型輸入包含 race 欄位；做完訓練後仍出現顯著 disparate impact。修正：把 race 從預測特徵中拿出，但保留用於 fairness audit；race-blind 不等於 fair（proxy 仍存在）。

Symptom: race is in the feature set; significant disparate impact persists post-training. Fix: remove race from prediction features but keep it for fairness audits; race-blind is NOT fair (proxies persist).

症狀：觀察到「Black 病人 sensitivity 低 10%」就斷言「模型有種族歧視」；但 disparity 可能源自 sampling、label 偏差或實際 prevalence 差異。修正：causal fairness（counterfactual fairness, Kusner 2017）區分 association vs causation。

Symptom: observing "10% lower sensitivity for Black patients" → concluding "the model is racist"; but disparity can stem from sampling, label bias, or true prevalence differences. Fix: causal fairness (counterfactual fairness, Kusner 2017) separates association from causation.

症狀：「我們不放 race 進模型，所以模型 fair」——但 ZIP code、insurance type、preferred language 都是 race 的 proxy，bias 依舊。修正：用 equalized odds / equality of opportunity 等 outcome-based 公平指標分層檢測。

Symptom: "we don't put race in the model, so it's fair" — but ZIP code, insurance type, preferred language are race proxies; bias remains. Fix: apply outcome-based fairness metrics (equalized odds / equality of opportunity) and stratify the audit.

症狀：「我們連 race 都沒收」——但沒有 demographic data 就無法做 fairness audit；不能修補你看不見的問題。修正：主動收集 self-reported demographics（含 opt-out），用 audit-only 模式儲存於與訓練資料分離的安全層。

Symptom: "we don't even collect race" — but without demographic data you cannot audit fairness; you can't fix what you can't see. Fix: actively collect self-reported demographics (with opt-out), store in an audit-only secure layer separate from training data.

症狀：只發布 model card（描述模型行為），但沒有 datasheet（描述訓練資料來源、人口組成、限制）——外部使用者無法判斷模型是否適用其族群。修正：Mitchell 2019 Model Card + Gebru 2021 Datasheets for Datasets 兩者必須並行。

Symptom: only a model card is released (describing behavior) but no datasheet (describing training data provenance, demographics, limitations) — external users can't judge applicability. Fix: Mitchell 2019 Model Card and Gebru 2021 Datasheets for Datasets must travel together.

症狀：新模型部署一年後才發現 Hispanic 族群 FPR 高 2 倍；本應在部署前用 historical data 模擬。修正：FDA PCCP 要求 pre-deployment fairness audit；報告 stratified TPR / FPR / PPV by 各 sensitive subgroup。

Symptom: the new model is deployed; a year later you discover Hispanic FPR is 2× higher — it should have been simulated pre-deployment on historical data. Fix: FDA PCCP requires pre-deployment fairness audits; report stratified TPR / FPR / PPV per sensitive subgroup.

追求 fairness 是否必然犧牲 overall accuracy？

CASE STUDY

2019 年 Obermeyer 等在 Science 揭露：一個被全美 200 million 病人使用的 care-management 演算法，存在嚴重種族偏差。問題出在 label——演算法用「過去醫療花費」當作「未來醫療需求」的 proxy。但 Black 病人歷史上因 systemic bias，被花在他們身上的錢就比較少。

結果：演算法低估 Black 病人需求達 50%——也就是他們必須病得「更嚴重」才能被分配到同等的 care management。光在這一個演算法上，每年就有 數百萬 Black 病人被低估。這篇 Science 論文催生了整個 algorithmic fairness 研究領域，並影響了 FDA 對 SaMD 的 fairness audit 要求。

In 2019 Obermeyer et al. exposed in Science that a US care-management algorithm used by 200M patients was racially biased. The problem was the label — it used "past healthcare cost" as a proxy for "future healthcare need". But due to systemic bias, Black patients historically had LESS spent on them.

Result: the algorithm under-served Black patients by ~50% — they had to be "sicker" to qualify for the same care management. From this single algorithm, millions of Black patients were under-served each year. The Science paper birthed the entire field of algorithmic fairness and influenced FDA's SaMD fairness-audit requirements.

歷史小故事：2000 年 — 當時還在 Carnegie Mellon 念博士的 Latanya Sweeney 證明：只需要 ZIP code、生日、性別三個欄位，就能唯一辨識 87% 的美國人。她用 Massachusetts 州政府「去識別化」的員工醫療資料，實地重新識別了當時的州長 William Weld 的住院紀錄，並把紀錄寄到州長辦公室。這篇論文催生了 HIPAA Safe Harbor 的「18 個 identifier」清單。Sweeney 後來成為 Harvard Data Privacy Lab 主任——她的 87% 論文是隱私領域引用次數最多的單篇。額外一筆：1979 年 Belmont Report 由 7 位委員在 Maryland Belmont Conference Center 4 天閉門會議起草，當時為了「Justice」要不要列為第三原則激辯一整夜，最後 Beauchamp 與 Childress 投了關鍵票；差分隱私的「ε」則是 Dwork 在 2006 ICALP 論文隨手用的希臘字母（「小量」之意），意外成為現代隱私運算的通用符號。2000 — Latanya Sweeney, then a Carnegie Mellon PhD student, proved that just three fields (ZIP code, birthdate, sex) could uniquely re-identify 87% of Americans. She demonstrated this on Massachusetts' "de-identified" state-employee medical data by re-identifying Governor William Weld's hospital records and mailing them to his office. The paper birthed HIPAA Safe Harbor's "18-identifier" list. Sweeney went on to direct Harvard's Data Privacy Lab — her 87% paper is the single most-cited privacy paper ever. Footnote: the 1979 Belmont Report was drafted by 7 commissioners during a 4-day retreat at Maryland's Belmont Conference Center; they argued through the night over whether "Justice" should be the third principle — Beauchamp and Childress cast the deciding votes. Differential privacy's "ε" was Dwork's casual Greek-letter choice in the 2006 ICALP paper ("small"); it became the universal symbol of privacy computing.
