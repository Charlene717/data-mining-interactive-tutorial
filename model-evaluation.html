"How good is the model?" has no single answer. This chapter covers cross-validation, confusion matrix, ROC/PR, calibration, and the three search strategies — a complete evaluation framework.

開場故事
情境劇

2021 Duke + Michigan

2017 年，Epic Systems 推出一個內建在電子病歷的早期敗血症預測模組——Epic Sepsis Watch。內部驗證 AUROC=0.76，部署到全美約 30% 的醫院。各院內部報告都是 0.74–0.78，看起來 great。

2021 年 6 月，Michigan 大學的 Habib Wong 教授團隊在 JAMA Internal Medicine 做了第一個獨立外部驗證——用密西根大學自家的 27,697 位住院病人。結果：AUROC = 0.63。Recall 在臨床有意義的高 specificity 區域只有 33%。也就是說，模型錯失約 67% 真實敗血症病例，並產生大量誤警。

究竟發生了什麼？分析發現幾件事：(1) Epic 的訓練集包含了「醫師已經開了抗生素」這種事後特徵 → label leakage；(2) k-fold CV 在 patient 重複住院的資料上做隨機切，同一病人跨 fold 出現 → 變相 LOO；(3) 訓練 / 部署之間 6 年，疾病編碼、ICU 流程都變了。

從那天起，外部驗證、group-CV、校準這三件事成為任何臨床 ML 部署的硬性要求。本章從根本告訴你：怎麼設計一個誠實的評估流程。

In 2017, Epic Systems released an EHR-embedded early-sepsis prediction module — Epic Sepsis Watch. Internal AUROC=0.76, deployed across ~30% of US hospitals. Site-level reports all showed 0.74–0.78. Looks great.

June 2021. Habib Wong's team at University of Michigan published the first independent external validation in JAMA Internal Medicine on 27,697 of their own inpatients. Result: AUROC = 0.63. Recall at clinically meaningful specificity was just 33%. The model missed ~67% of real sepsis cases and produced massive false alarms.

What happened? Investigation found: (1) Epic's training set included "physician already ordered antibiotics" — post-hoc label leakage; (2) k-fold CV randomly split a dataset where the same patient appeared in multiple admissions → effectively LOO; (3) 6 years between training and deployment changed diagnosis codes and ICU workflows.

From that day forward, external validation, group-CV, and calibration became hard requirements for any clinical ML deployment. This chapter teaches you how to design an honest evaluation pipeline — from the ground up.

評估詞彙表 ×16

Hold-out
留出法
把資料切一刀：80% 訓練、20% 看模型有沒有偷學。
Cut the data once: 80% train, 20% to check.
一次性 train/test 切分；小資料下變異大，建議 stratified + shuffle + 多次重複。
Single train/test split; high variance on small data — use stratified + shuffle + multiple repeats.
K-Fold CV
K 折交叉驗證
輪流讓 5 組資料當「考試題」，平均分數最準。
Take turns making 5 groups the "exam questions", average the scores.
將資料切 K 等分，輪流以 K-1 訓練 / 1 驗證。Stone 1974 提出；K=5 或 10 是經驗最佳。
Split into K folds, take turns K-1 train / 1 val. Stone 1974; K=5 or 10 is empirically optimal.
Stratified CV
分層 CV
每 fold 中陽性陰性比例保持一致——不讓某 fold 沒有陽性。
Keep positive:negative ratio constant in each fold.
在 K-Fold 基礎上維持類別分佈；對 imbalanced 分類必備。
K-Fold preserving class distribution; mandatory for imbalanced classification.
Group-CV (Patient-Level)
分組 CV (病人層級)
同一個病人的所有資料一定要在同一 fold——不能偷看到「自己的未來」。
All visits from the same patient must stay in one fold — no peeking at your own future.
GroupKFold：按 patient_id 切。是醫療資料避免 leakage 的標準。
GroupKFold: split by patient_id. The standard to avoid leakage in medical data.
Nested CV
巢狀 CV
兩層 CV：內層調參、外層誠實估泛化。
Two layers: inner tunes, outer reports honestly.
外層估泛化，內層調 hyperparam。解決 "tune-and-evaluate on same data" bias (Cawley-Talbot 2010)。
Outer for generalization, inner for tuning. Cures "tune-and-evaluate on same data" bias (Cawley-Talbot 2010).
Confusion Matrix
混淆矩陣
2×2 表格：對的、錯的、漏的、誤的——四個格子說明一切。
2×2 grid: right, wrong, missed, mistaken — four boxes tell the whole story.
TP/FP/TN/FN；其他所有 metric 都從這四數推導。
TP/FP/TN/FN; every other metric derives from these four counts.
Precision / Recall
精確率 / 召回率
Precision：抓對多少？Recall：漏掉多少？
Precision: how many right? Recall: how many caught?
P=TP/(TP+FP)；R=TP/(TP+FN)。Recall=Sensitivity=TPR。對 imbalanced 比 Accuracy 誠實。
P=TP/(TP+FP); R=TP/(TP+FN). Recall=Sensitivity=TPR. More honest than Accuracy under imbalance.
F1 / F-β
F1 / F-β
P 和 R 的「和諧平均」——β 大就重 Recall。
Harmonic mean of P and R; β>1 weighs Recall more.
F1=2PR/(P+R); F-β=(1+β²)PR/(β²P+R)。β=2 (F2) 用於漏診成本高的場合。
F1=2PR/(P+R); F-β=(1+β²)PR/(β²P+R). β=2 (F2) for high miss-cost.
ROC-AUC
ROC 曲線下面積
所有可能門檻下，模型把陽性排在陰性前面的機率。
Probability the model ranks a positive above a negative, across all thresholds.
∫TPR d(FPR)；等於 Mann-Whitney U / (n+·n−)。Spackman 1950 軍用雷達起源。
∫TPR d(FPR); equivalent to Mann-Whitney U / (n+·n−). Origin: 1950 military radar (Spackman).
PR-AUC (Average Precision)
PR 曲線下面積
不平衡資料的真本事——只看陽性類別。
Real skill on imbalanced data — focuses on the positive class.
∫Precision d(Recall) = ΣΔR·P。Saito-Rehmsmeier 2015 PLoS ONE 證明 imbalance 時遠勝 ROC。
∫Precision d(Recall) = ΣΔR·P. Saito-Rehmsmeier 2015 PLoS ONE proved superiority under imbalance.
Threshold / Youden's J
判定門檻
機率超過多少才算「陽」？預設 0.5，但臨床上常常要調。
Above what probability do we call "positive"? Default 0.5 — clinical use often shifts.
Youden's J = TPR − FPR 的最大值點；sklearn 1.5+ 提供 FixedThresholdClassifier。
Youden's J = max(TPR − FPR). sklearn 1.5+ added FixedThresholdClassifier.
Calibration / Brier
校準 / Brier
模型說 80%，那 100 個案例中真的有 80 個是陽嗎？
Model says 80% — out of 100 such cases, are 80 truly positive?
Brier = (1/N) Σ(p̂i − yi)²；reliability diagram 視覺化。Niculescu-Mizil 2005。
Brier = (1/N) Σ(p̂i − yi)²; reliability diagram for visualisation. Niculescu-Mizil 2005.
Grid / Random / Bayesian Search
三種超參搜尋
窮舉 vs 隨機 vs 聰明地下一個試什麼。
Exhaustive vs random vs smart-next-pick.
Bergstra 2012：random 通常勝 grid；Snoek 2012 Bayesian (GP/TPE) 進一步用前次結果指引。
Bergstra 2012: random beats grid on average; Snoek 2012: Bayesian (GP/TPE) uses past results to guide.
Optuna / TPE
Optuna / TPE
Bayesian + 自動剪枝的超參搜尋工具。
Bayesian search + auto-pruning library.
Akiba et al, KDD 2019 (arXiv:1907.10902)；define-by-run + TPE + HyperbandPruner。
Akiba et al, KDD 2019 (arXiv:1907.10902); define-by-run + TPE + HyperbandPruner.
Hyperband / ASHA
Hyperband / ASHA
先給每個候選一點 resource，表現好的繼續多給。
Give each candidate a little resource; promote the good ones.
Li 2017 JMLR 提出 successive halving；ASHA (Li 2020) 平行化版本，配合 Ray-Tune 廣泛使用。
Li 2017 JMLR — successive halving; ASHA (Li 2020) — parallel version, used with Ray-Tune.

外部驗證

類比

ROC vs PR — 兩種誠實

ROC 像「醫院整體準確率」：考慮全部病人 (陽性 + 陰性) 的判斷力。PR 像「在我科室真的被標陽性的病人中，我講對了多少」——只盯陽性類別。對罕見病 (1% prevalence)，ROC 可以是 0.95 而 PR 只有 0.25——前者看似亮眼，後者揭穿真相：99% 警報其實是誤警。嚴重不平衡時，PR 是唯一誠實的指標。

ROC is like "hospital-wide accuracy" — judges across all patients (positives + negatives). PR is like "of the patients I flagged positive in my own ward, how many did I get right" — focused on positives. For rare disease (1% prevalence), ROC can be 0.95 while PR is just 0.25 — the first looks shiny, the second exposes the truth: 99% of alarms were false. Under heavy imbalance, PR is the only honest metric.

概覽

為什麼評估這麼難？

單一準確率 (accuracy) 在類別不平衡時極度誤導——若 99% 樣本為陰性，模型「全猜陰性」就 99% 準確率，但對任何陽性都毫無預測力。實務上需要從切資料的方式（CV）、選指標、看分布、做校準、調參五個面向整體評估。

下方表格展示同一個模型在不同指標下可能呈現完全不同樣貌，這就是為什麼「報告單一數字」是 ML 論文的常見錯誤。

A single accuracy number is wildly misleading under class imbalance — if 99% of samples are negative, "always predict negative" hits 99% accuracy with zero predictive power for positives. In practice you must combine splitting strategy (CV), metric choice, distribution, calibration, and tuning for honest evaluation.

四、要報告什麼指標？

🌳 指標選擇決策樹

Q1:
類別極度不平衡（陽性 <5%）？ → PR-AUC + Recall@k，避免單看 Accuracy / ROC-AUC
Q2:
FN 成本>>FP（漏診癌症）？ → Recall / F-β (β=2) 優先
Q3:
FP 成本>>FN（垃圾訊息）？ → Precision 優先
Q4:
機率本身會被下游用（風險分層、決策）？ → 報告 Brier + reliability diagram
Q5:
多元分類？ → macro-F1 (各類等權) 或 weighted-F1 (按樣本量)
Q6:
調超參時？ → 用 nested CV；外圈報泛化、內圈調 hyperparam
Q1:
Severe imbalance (positives <5%)? → PR-AUC + Recall@k; never Accuracy / ROC-AUC alone
Q2:
FN cost >> FP (missed cancer)? → Prioritize Recall / F-β (β=2)
Q3:
FP cost >> FN (spam)? → Prioritize Precision
Q4:
Downstream uses the probability itself? → Report Brier + reliability diagram
Q5:
Multiclass? → macro-F1 (equal weight) or weighted-F1 (sample-weighted)
Q6:
When tuning hyperparameters? → Use nested CV; outer = generalization, inner = tuning
🚨
調參偏誤：用同一份驗證集既調超參又報 metric → 分數一定偏樂觀。Nested CV 或留一個從未動過的 holdout 是必須。 Tuning bias: Using one validation set to both tune and report → metrics are guaranteed optimistic. Use nested CV or a true never-touched holdout.

手算範例

手算 5-fold CV 全套

4

XGBoost 誠實評估

完整
STEP 1
資料：1000 位住院病人，120 位敗血症 (12%)。每位有 50 個特徵 (vital signs + lab values)。切資料策略：採用 GroupKFold(n_splits=5, groups=patient_id)，因為同病人多次住院不能算獨立樣本。
Data: 1000 inpatients, 120 sepsis cases (12%). 50 features each (vitals + labs). Split strategy: GroupKFold(n_splits=5, groups=patient_id) — same patient with multiple admissions ≠ independent samples.
STEP 2
5-fold 迴圈：每 fold 訓練 800 / 驗證 200。記錄每 fold 的 ROC-AUC: 0.81, 0.78, 0.83, 0.79, 0.80。Mean ± SD = 0.802 ± 0.018。
5-fold loop: train 800 / val 200 each. ROC-AUC per fold: 0.81, 0.78, 0.83, 0.79, 0.80. Mean ± SD = 0.802 ± 0.018.
STEP 3
PR-AUC 同時記錄：每 fold 算 average_precision = 0.52, 0.48, 0.55, 0.50, 0.51 → mean ± SD = 0.512 ± 0.025。注意 PR-AUC 比 ROC-AUC 低很多——這就是 12% 不平衡讓 ROC 樂觀的後果。
PR-AUC simultaneously: 0.52, 0.48, 0.55, 0.50, 0.51 → mean ± SD = 0.512 ± 0.025. PR-AUC much lower than ROC-AUC — that's the 12% imbalance making ROC look generous.
STEP 4
計算 Brier：每 fold 取 P̂ 和 y，Brier=(1/N)Σ(p̂-y)²。5 fold mean Brier = 0.13。對 12% prevalence base rate (永遠輸 0.12) Brier = 0.106 → 0.13 比 base 差 → 模型校準有問題，要 Platt scaling。
Brier per fold: Brier=(1/N)Σ(p̂-y)². Mean Brier = 0.13. Base rate (always output 0.12) gives Brier = 0.106 → 0.13 is worse than base → model is mis-calibrated; apply Platt scaling.
STEP 5
Nested CV：外層 5-fold 估泛化、內層 5-fold 跑 Optuna 找最佳超參。每 outer fold 中，inner 用 GridSearchCV(scoring='average_precision') 找出 best params 再用 outer val 算 AUROC。最終得 nested ROC-AUC = 0.79 ± 0.02。比非 nested 的 0.80 略低，但這才是無偏估計。
Nested CV: outer 5-fold for generalization, inner 5-fold for Optuna. Each outer fold's inner uses GridSearchCV(scoring='average_precision') for best params, then computes outer val AUROC. Nested ROC-AUC = 0.79 ± 0.02. Slightly lower than non-nested 0.80 — but this is the unbiased estimate.
STEP 6
找最佳門檻：合併所有 5 fold 的 OOF predictions，計算 Youden's J = max(TPR − FPR) 找出 threshold τ* = 0.18 (不是預設 0.5！)。在 τ*：Recall = 0.74、Precision = 0.42、F1 = 0.54。
Find best threshold: stack all 5-fold OOF preds, compute Youden's J = max(TPR − FPR), get τ* = 0.18 (not the default 0.5!). At τ*: Recall = 0.74, Precision = 0.42, F1 = 0.54.
STEP 7
Bootstrap 95% CI：對 OOF preds 做 1000 次 bootstrap → ROC-AUC 95% CI = [0.75, 0.84]。報告：「ROC-AUC = 0.80 (95% CI 0.75–0.84)」。
Bootstrap 95% CI: 1000× bootstrap on OOF preds → ROC-AUC 95% CI = [0.75, 0.84]. Report: "ROC-AUC = 0.80 (95% CI 0.75–0.84)".
STEP 8
外部驗證：用另一家醫院的 500 病人 (從未訓練過) 跑同模型 → ROC-AUC = 0.71。內外差距 0.09 不小——需報告，並考慮 domain adaptation 或 fine-tuning。
External validation: 500 patients from another hospital (never seen) → ROC-AUC = 0.71. Gap 0.09 is non-trivial — must be reported; consider domain adaptation or fine-tuning.
STEP 9
最終報告 (TRIPOD+AI 規範)：「ROC-AUC 0.80 ± 0.02 (95% CI 0.75–0.84)，PR-AUC 0.51 ± 0.03，Brier 0.10 (post-calibration)，τ*=0.18，外部 AUROC 0.71」。沒有單一數字，而是完整證據鏈。

爭論題

四題爭論

爭論 1

ROC vs PR — 怎麼選？

觀點 A：類別嚴重不平衡 (陽性 <10%) 時只能信 PR-AUC。ROC-AUC 在不平衡下會被大量 TN 「灌水」——FPR 增加 0.01 對應 1000 個 FP，但分母 N− 很大 → 看不出來。Under severe imbalance (positives <10%), only PR-AUC. ROC-AUC gets "puffed up" by enormous TN counts — FPR rising 0.01 hides 1000 FPs because denominator N− is huge.
觀點 B：兩個一起報告。ROC 對比模型「對 全 population 的判別能力」更直觀；PR 對比「我的陽性預測有多可信」更實用。研究員的義務是兩者都呈現。Report both. ROC speaks to "across the population, how well does the model discriminate?"; PR speaks to "of my positive predictions, how trustworthy?" — both views are needed.
實務原則：prevalence < 10% → 主指標 PR-AUC；prevalence ≥ 10% → ROC-AUC 也可主。無論如何，外加 Brier 校準。Prevalence < 10% → headline PR-AUC; ≥ 10% → ROC-AUC fine as headline. Always add Brier for calibration.
爭論 2

Nested CV 值得嗎？

挺方：Cawley-Talbot 2010 JMLR 證明：同一份資料調參+評估會高估泛化 5–10%。對小資料 (n < 1000) 必須 nested。臨床部署誤估這幾 % 可能差別生死。Cawley-Talbot 2010 JMLR proved: tuning and evaluating on the same data inflates generalization by 5–10%. For small n (<1000) nested is required. In clinical deployment those few % can be life-and-death.
反方：大資料 (n > 10⁵) 簡單的 70/15/15 split 已經夠穩；nested 帶來的 ±0.5% 估計改善值不上 10× 計算。For big data (n > 10⁵), a 70/15/15 split is stable enough; the ±0.5% gain from nested doesn't justify 10× compute.
折衷：n < 5000 → nested 5×5；5000 ≤ n < 50000 → outer hold-out + inner 5-fold；n ≥ 50000 → train/val/test single split + early stopping。n < 5000 → nested 5×5; 5000 ≤ n < 50000 → outer hold-out + inner 5-fold; n ≥ 50000 → train/val/test single split + early stopping.
爭論 3

四種搜尋法怎麼選？

超參 ≤ 3 個：Grid Search 直觀、可重現、結果易解讀。Grid Search — intuitive, reproducible, easy to read.
超參 4–10：Random Search (Bergstra-Bengio 2012)：以同樣的 trials 數，random 通常擊敗 grid，因為「重要參數」會被多次抽到。Random Search (Bergstra-Bengio 2012): given same trial budget, random beats grid because the "important parameters" get sampled multiple times.
超參多 + 評估昂貴：Optuna (TPE) 或 Bayesian (GP)：用 surrogate model 引導下一試。但 trials < 30 時優勢不明顯。Optuna (TPE) or Bayesian (GP): surrogate model guides next trial. Advantage faint when trials < 30.
深度學習：ASHA / Hyperband：低成本探索很多 trials，successive halving 早停淘汰。配合 Ray-Tune。ASHA / Hyperband: explore many trials cheaply, successive halving kills bad ones early. Use with Ray-Tune.
爭論 4

怎麼報告 K 個 fold 分數？

挺 mean ± SD：簡潔、易引用、論文表格容納度高。但 K=5 樣本估的 SD 不穩、SD 不代表 95% CI。Concise, easy to cite, table-friendly. But SD from K=5 is unstable; SD ≠ 95% CI.
挺 boxplot：顯示分佈、極端值、是否雙峰 — 評審看一眼就知模型穩定度。Shows distribution, outliers, bimodality — reviewers see model stability at a glance.
2025 規範：TRIPOD+AI 要求同時報告 mean、SD、95% CI (bootstrap 1000×)；附 fold-level boxplot 為 SI 圖。TRIPOD+AI requires both: mean, SD, bootstrap 95% CI (1000×), with fold-level boxplot in SI.

實案聚光燈

Epic Sepsis Watch 崩盤

2021 年 6 月 JAMA Internal Medicine 發表 Wong et al. 對 Epic Sepsis Watch 的獨立驗證——這個被部署到全美 30% 醫院的模型，外部 AUROC 只有 0.63、Recall 33%，遠低於 Epic 內部宣稱的 0.76。

In June 2021, JAMA Internal Medicine published Wong et al.'s independent validation of Epic Sepsis Watch — the model deployed in 30% of US hospitals had an external AUROC of just 0.63 and Recall of 33%, far below Epic's claimed internal 0.76.

事後分析指出三大錯誤：(1) 訓練特徵含「已開抗生素」→ post-hoc leakage；(2) 同一病人多次住院在 random k-fold 被切到不同 fold → 偽獨立；(3) 模型訓練於 2013–2015 數據，部署到 2019–2021 流程已變。這個案例直接觸發了 FDA / EMA / NICE 對醫療 ML 的嚴格規範化。

Post-hoc analysis identified three errors: (1) training features included "antibiotic already ordered" → post-hoc leakage; (2) the same patient's multiple admissions split across folds in random k-fold → pseudo-independence; (3) model trained on 2013–2015 data, deployed in 2019–2021 with changed workflows. This single case triggered FDA / EMA / NICE to formalize regulation of medical ML.

陷阱 6 連發

評估 6 大陷阱

病人層級洩漏

同一病人多次住院隨機切入不同 fold → 模型偷學「自己的病史」。必用 GroupKFold(groups=patient_id)。

Same patient's admissions randomly split → model "peeks" at own history. Always GroupKFold(groups=patient_id).

不平衡只看 Accuracy

2% 陽性 → 全猜陰性 = 98% Accuracy、Recall=0。用 PR-AUC / Recall@k / F-β (β=2)。

2% positives → "all negative" gives 98% Accuracy, Recall=0. Use PR-AUC / Recall@k / F-β (β=2).

單一切分

n=200 一次切只得單點估計，變異大。至少 5×5 repeated CV 或 bootstrap 1000×。

n=200 single split gives a point estimate with huge variance. At least 5×5 repeated CV or bootstrap 1000×.

只優化單一指標

為了 ROC-AUC 0.95 而犧牲 Brier 0.4 (爛校準)，臨床上完全無法用。多目標 Optuna 或 Pareto front。

Chasing ROC-AUC 0.95 while Brier ends at 0.4 (terrible calibration) is clinically useless. Multi-objective Optuna or Pareto front.

忽略校準

XGBoost 預設輸出未校準 (overconfident at extremes)。必跑 CalibratedClassifierCV(method='isotonic', cv=5) 再報告 Brier + reliability diagram。

XGBoost default outputs are uncalibrated (overconfident at extremes). Always run CalibratedClassifierCV(method='isotonic', cv=5), then report Brier + reliability diagram.

在 test 上調門檻

如果你在 test 上找最佳 τ 再回報 metric → 即是 test leakage。τ 必在 train/val 上決定，test 只能用 τ*。

Tuning τ on test and reporting metrics = test leakage. τ must be decided on train/val; test only uses fixed τ*.

壞範例：「我們 AUROC = 0.97」

一個句子的論文：「我們的模型 ROC-AUC = 0.97」。沒提 split、沒提 CV、沒提 prevalence、沒提 Brier、沒外部驗證。資訊量為零，且高度可能存在 leakage。

A one-sentence paper: "Our model achieves ROC-AUC = 0.97." No mention of split, CV, prevalence, Brier, external validation. Zero information value; high probability of leakage.

進階研究員彩蛋

2024–2026 SOTA

Davis-Goadrich revisited (2024)：原始 Davis-Goadrich ICML 2006 證明 ROC 與 PR 的對應關係；2024 Sofaer et al. 在 Methods Ecol Evol 重新分析，指出 PR-AUC 在跨資料集比較時仍有 prevalence-dependent 陷阱——建議搭配 normalised PR-AUC (Boyd et al. 2013) 使用。

Davis-Goadrich revisited (2024): Davis-Goadrich ICML 2006 proved ROC↔PR correspondence; 2024 Sofaer et al. (Methods Ecol Evol) showed PR-AUC still has prevalence-dependent traps for cross-dataset comparison — pair it with normalised PR-AUC (Boyd et al. 2013).

🔍 Conformal Prediction

Conformal prediction (Vovk-Gammerman 2005, Angelopoulos-Bates 2023 review arXiv:2107.07511) 用一個 calibration set 對任何分類器產生「prediction set」，保證 P(true ∈ set) ≥ 1−α，沒有分布假設。2024 sklearn-conformal 0.3 + crepes 0.5 提供成熟 sklearn API。
用於評估：除了報告 AUROC、Brier，再報告「90% coverage 下平均 prediction set 大小」——越小代表模型越確定。

Conformal prediction (Vovk-Gammerman 2005, Angelopoulos-Bates 2023 review arXiv:2107.07511) wraps any classifier with a calibration set to produce a "prediction set" guaranteeing P(true ∈ set) ≥ 1−α, distribution-free. The 2024 sklearn-conformal 0.3 + crepes 0.5 give mature sklearn APIs.
For evaluation, beyond AUROC/Brier, also report "mean prediction-set size at 90% coverage" — smaller = more confident model.

🔍 Robust Bootstrap calibration

2025 年 Adigun et al. NeurIPS 提出 RoBus (Robust Bootstrap) calibration：對 small holdout sets 上的 Platt/isotonic estimates 做 1000× bootstrap，產生 calibration curve 的信賴帶。比單一 isotonic 更穩、能誠實估計校準的不確定度。GitHub: pip install robus-cal。

Adigun et al. NeurIPS 2025 introduced RoBus (Robust Bootstrap) calibration: 1000× bootstrap on small-holdout Platt/isotonic estimates, producing confidence bands on the calibration curve. More stable than vanilla isotonic; honest about calibration uncertainty. pip install robus-cal.

🔍 Survival evaluation

對時間-至-事件 (time-to-event) 預測，ROC-AUC 不適用。標準指標：Harrell's C-index (Harrell et al, 1982 JAMA) 衡量「兩位病人預測風險排序是否與真實事件順序一致」；time-dependent AUC (Heagerty 2000 Biometrics) 評估特定時間 t 時的判別力。Python: from sksurv.metrics import concordance_index_censored, cumulative_dynamic_auc。

For time-to-event prediction, ROC-AUC doesn't apply. Standards: Harrell's C-index (Harrell et al. 1982 JAMA) — "do two patients' risk rankings agree with their event ordering?"; time-dependent AUC (Heagerty 2000 Biometrics) — discrimination at specific time t. Python: from sksurv.metrics import concordance_index_censored, cumulative_dynamic_auc.

🔍 Multi-objective Optuna

2024 Optuna 4.0 加入 NSGA-II + TPE 多目標支援：可同時最佳化 PR-AUC ↑ + Brier ↓ + Latency ↓，得到 Pareto front。臨床部署時讓 panel 從 frontier 選最合適 trade-off。

API: study = optuna.create_study(directions=["maximize","minimize","minimize"])。

Optuna 4.0 (2024) added NSGA-II + TPE multi-objective: simultaneously maximise PR-AUC, minimise Brier, minimise latency → Pareto front. Clinical panel picks the trade-off from the frontier.

API: study = optuna.create_study(directions=["maximize","minimize","minimize"]).
