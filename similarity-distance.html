故事化導讀

🧒 兒童醫院的「最像我家小孩」演算法

波士頓兒童醫院的 Dr. Park 正在面對一個三歲女孩 Lily——罕見的代謝症候群，全球僅 47 個確診病例。她需要決定治療方案，但無法照「教科書」走——案例太少。她決定用「病人匹配 (patient matching)」：在資料庫的 47 個病例中，找出「最像 Lily」的 5 個，看看他們的治療反應。

她的資料科學家先用 Euclidean 距離跑——前 5 名是 A、B、C、D、E。改用 Cosine 距離——前 5 名變成 B、F、G、H、I。再改用 Mahalanobis（考慮 covariance）——前 5 名又變成 C、F、J、K、L。同一個 Lily，三種度量，三組完全不同的「鄰居」。

Dr. Park 看著螢幕沈默了很久。她意識到「距離」不是技術選擇——而是臨床推論的選擇。Cosine 忽略症狀的絕對強度（只看「模式」），Euclidean 把實驗室數值絕對化，Mahalanobis 校正了實驗室之間的相關。她最後問了一個問題：「對 Lily 來說，重要的是『症狀組合』還是『嚴重度』還是『獨立風險』？」這個問題決定了她該用哪一個度量——而度量決定了哪 5 個病人會影響 Lily 的治療。

At Boston Children's Hospital, Dr. Park is facing a 3-year-old girl, Lily, with a rare metabolic syndrome — only 47 documented cases worldwide. She has to choose a treatment, but the "textbook" doesn't fit — too few cases. So she turns to patient matching: from the database's 47 cases, find the 5 "most like Lily" and study their responses.

Her data scientist runs Euclidean distance first — top 5: A, B, C, D, E. Then Cosine — top 5: B, F, G, H, I. Then Mahalanobis (covariance-aware) — top 5: C, F, J, K, L. One Lily, three metrics, three completely different "neighbors."

Dr. Park stared at the screen for a long time. She realized "distance" is not a technical choice — it's a clinical reasoning choice. Cosine ignores absolute symptom intensity (only "pattern" matters); Euclidean takes lab values at face; Mahalanobis corrects for correlations among labs. Her final question: "For Lily, is the important thing the combination of symptoms, the severity, or the independent risk?" That question decided the metric — and the metric decided which 5 patients would shape Lily's treatment.

名詞速查

15 個關鍵詞：從直白到精確

Distance Metric
距離度量
兩個東西「差多少」的數字尺——必須滿足四個基本規則。
A numeric ruler for "how different are two things?" — must obey four basic rules.
滿足 (1) 非負 (2) 同一性 (3) 對稱 (4) 三角不等式的函式 d : X × X → ℝ⁺。
A function d : X × X → ℝ⁺ satisfying (1) non-negativity (2) identity (3) symmetry (4) triangle inequality.
Semi-metric
半度量
「幾乎是距離」——少了三角不等式那條。
"Almost a distance" — but missing the triangle inequality.
滿足三角不等式之外的所有公理；Cosine distance 是典型例（不嚴格滿足三角）。
Satisfies all axioms except the triangle inequality; Cosine distance is the canonical example.
Similarity
相似度
兩個東西「多像」——越大代表越像。
"How alike" two things are — bigger means more alike.
sim(x,y) ∈ [0,1] 或 [−1,1]；通常透過 d = 1 − sim 或 d = 1/sim 轉換為距離。
sim(x,y) ∈ [0,1] or [−1,1]; usually converted to distance via d = 1 − sim or d = 1/sim.
Euclidean (L2)
歐氏距離
就是「直線距離」——拿尺量兩點之間的直線長度。
"Straight-line distance" — a ruler between two points.
d(x,y) = √Σ(xᵢ−yᵢ)²；低維 + 同單位最佳；對 outlier 敏感。
d(x,y) = √Σ(xᵢ−yᵢ)²; best in low-D with consistent units; outlier-sensitive.
Manhattan (L1)
曼哈頓距離
在棋盤上只能走橫直，不能對角——加起來的格數。
On a chessboard you can only move H/V, never diagonal — sum of moves.
d(x,y) = Σ|xᵢ−yᵢ|；高維比 Euclidean 更穩；對 outlier 較寬容。
d(x,y) = Σ|xᵢ−yᵢ|; more stable in high-D than Euclidean; more outlier-tolerant.
Minkowski (Lp)
Minkowski 距離
L1、L2 的家族總稱——p 是調整旋鈕。
The family name for L1/L2 — p is the tuning knob.
d(x,y) = (Σ|xᵢ−yᵢ|ᵖ)^(1/p)；p=1 Manhattan、p=2 Euclidean、p→∞ Chebyshev。
d(x,y) = (Σ|xᵢ−yᵢ|ᵖ)^(1/p); p=1 Manhattan, p=2 Euclidean, p→∞ Chebyshev.
Chebyshev (L∞)
Chebyshev 距離
只看「差最多」的那個方向——其他都不重要。
Only the "biggest gap" direction matters — ignore the rest.
d(x,y) = max|xᵢ−yᵢ|；下棋國王的移動數；倉儲、物流定位常用。
d(x,y) = max|xᵢ−yᵢ|; the chess king's moves; useful for warehouse/logistics positioning.
Mahalanobis
馬氏距離
考慮特徵間的「拉扯」——當身高跟體重相關，要做矯正。
Accounts for "pulling" among features — if height and weight correlate, correct for it.
d² = (x−y)ᵀΣ⁻¹(x−y)；Σ 為樣本共變異矩陣；多變量異常值偵測金標。
d² = (x−y)ᵀΣ⁻¹(x−y); Σ is the sample covariance; the gold standard for multivariate anomaly detection.
Cosine
餘弦相似度
不在乎「多大」，只在乎「方向」——像看兩支箭頭指哪。
Ignores magnitude — only direction matters, like comparing arrow directions.
sim = (x·y)/(‖x‖‖y‖)；distance = 1−sim；文本 TF-IDF、基因表現方向相似性首選。
sim = (x·y)/(‖x‖‖y‖); distance = 1−sim; the go-to for TF-IDF text and gene expression direction.
Jaccard
Jaccard 相似度
兩個集合的「交集除以聯集」——共同朋友占所有朋友的比例。
"Intersection over union" — the share of mutual friends among all friends.
J(A,B) = |A∩B|/|A∪B|；二元 / 集合資料（symptoms、SNPs）。
J(A,B) = |A∩B|/|A∪B|; for binary/set data (symptoms, SNPs).
Tanimoto / Dice
Tanimoto / Dice
化學分子指紋的「兄弟距離」——Tanimoto 跟 Jaccard 公式幾乎一樣。
The chemistry sibling — Tanimoto's formula is essentially Jaccard.
Tanimoto = Jaccard 在二元情境；Dice = 2|A∩B|/(|A|+|B|)；化學 Morgan fingerprint 標準。
Tanimoto = Jaccard in binary contexts; Dice = 2|A∩B|/(|A|+|B|); standard for chemical Morgan fingerprints.
Gower
Gower 距離
「混合資料」的瑞士刀——一張 EHR 表有年齡、性別、用藥、實驗室數值，Gower 都能算。
The Swiss knife for "mixed data" — age, sex, meds, lab values all in one table, Gower handles it.
Gower 1971。每個欄位依類型分別計算 0–1 距離後加權平均；混合連續 + 類別 + 順序。
Gower 1971. Per-column 0–1 distance by type then weighted average; handles continuous + categorical + ordinal.
DTW
動態時間扭曲
兩段「節奏不同」的歌——DTW 會自動拉伸對齊。
Two tunes with different tempo — DTW stretches them into alignment.
Sakoe & Chiba 1978。動態規劃尋找最短對齊路徑；對長度不同的時間序列、語音、步態。
Sakoe & Chiba 1978. Dynamic programming for the shortest alignment path; for variable-length time series, speech, gait.
Edit / Levenshtein
編輯距離
「把 cat 改成 cut 要幾步？」插入、刪除、替換各算一步。
"How many steps to turn 'cat' into 'cut'?" Insertions, deletions, substitutions each count.
Levenshtein 1965。字串、DNA、蛋白質序列的標準距離；變形如 Needleman-Wunsch、Smith-Waterman。
Levenshtein 1965. Standard distance for strings, DNA, proteins; variants include Needleman-Wunsch, Smith-Waterman.
Curse of Dimensionality
維度詛咒
當你有 1000 個欄位，每個人跟每個人都「差不多遠」——KNN 失靈。
With 1000 columns, everyone is "about equally far" from everyone — KNN breaks.
Beyer/Aggarwal 1999/2001 證明：高維 Euclidean 下 (max−min)/min → 0；先 PCA / UMAP / Cosine 救援。
Beyer/Aggarwal 1999/2001 proved: high-D Euclidean gives (max−min)/min → 0; rescue with PCA / UMAP / Cosine.

概覽

距離度量的四個性質

嚴格意義的「距離度量 (metric)」必須滿足四個公理：(1) 非負 d(x,y)≥0、(2) 同一性 d(x,y)=0 ⇔ x=y、(3) 對稱 d(x,y)=d(y,x)、(4) 三角不等式 d(x,z) ≤ d(x,y)+d(y,z)。不滿足全部四項的稱為 半度量 或 相似度（如 Cosine 不是嚴格距離）。

距離選擇直接決定模型行為——KNN 預測、K-Means cluster 形狀、Hierarchical 樹結構都會因此改變。在生物醫學上更要小心：基因表現量用 Cosine / Pearson，影像 fingerprint 用 Tanimoto / Jaccard，混合型病人特徵用 Gower，ECG 時間序列用 DTW。

A strict metric satisfies four axioms: (1) non-negativity d(x,y)≥0, (2) identity d(x,y)=0 ⇔ x=y, (3) symmetry d(x,y)=d(y,x), (4) triangle inequality d(x,z) ≤ d(x,y)+d(y,z). Failing any of these makes it a semi-metric or just a similarity (e.g., Cosine is not a strict metric).

The choice of distance shapes model behavior — KNN predictions, K-Means cluster shapes, hierarchical trees all change. In biomedicine: gene expression uses Cosine / Pearson, chemical fingerprints use Tanimoto / Jaccard, mixed patient features use Gower, ECG time-series uses DTW.

生活比喻

距離度量就是「比較人」的方式

想像你要找一個「跟我最像的人」。你可以問：（1）身高跟我差幾公分？這是 Euclidean。（2）我喜歡的音樂類型跟他重疊幾種？這是 Jaccard。（3）我們在 Spotify 上的播放列表方向是否一致？這是 Cosine。（4）考慮到「身高跟體重相關」，我們真的有多獨立地不同？這是 Mahalanobis。每種度量問了完全不同的問題——所以同一個你，會得到不同的「最像我」。

Imagine you want to find "the person most like me." You could ask: (1) How many cm taller or shorter? That's Euclidean. (2) How many music genres do we share? That's Jaccard. (3) Are our Spotify playlists pointing the same direction? That's Cosine. (4) Given that height and weight correlate, how independently different are we really? That's Mahalanobis. Each metric asks a completely different question — so the same "you" gets different "most-like-me" answers.

決策樹

二、如何挑選正確的距離？

🌳 距離選擇決策樹

Q1:
資料是純二元/集合（symptoms, SNPs, fingerprints）？ → Jaccard / Tanimoto / Dice
Q2:
資料是連續且方向比尺度重要（gene expression、TF-IDF）？ → Cosine / Pearson
Q3:
資料是連續同單位低維？ → Euclidean（記得先 scaling）
Q4:
資料是高維且雜訊多？ → Manhattan / Cosine（避免「維度詛咒」）
Q5:
資料是混合型 EHR（age + sex + 用藥）？ → Gower (R: cluster::daisy)
Q6:
資料是時間序列且長度不同？ → DTW (dtaidistance / tslearn)
Q7:
需考慮特徵間共變異？ → Mahalanobis
Q1:
Pure binary / set data (symptoms, SNPs, fingerprints)? → Jaccard / Tanimoto / Dice
Q2:
Continuous, direction matters more than magnitude (gene expression, TF-IDF)? → Cosine / Pearson
Q3:
Continuous, same units, low-D? → Euclidean (after scaling!)
Q4:
High-D and noisy? → Manhattan / Cosine (mitigate curse of dimensionality)
Q5:
Mixed EHR (age + sex + meds)? → Gower (R: cluster::daisy)
Q6:
Time series of differing length? → DTW (dtaidistance / tslearn)
Q7:
Need covariance awareness? → Mahalanobis
⚠️
維度詛咒：在高維空間中（>30 維），所有點之間的 Euclidean 距離趨於相等，使 KNN 變得無意義。解法：先 PCA / UMAP 降維，或改用 Cosine / Manhattan。 Curse of dimensionality: In high-D (>30), all pairwise Euclidean distances converge — KNN becomes meaningless. Fix: PCA / UMAP first, or use Cosine / Manhattan.

完整案例走讀：6 種距離手算

x=(5,8,0,1), y=(4,7,1,0)
Setup
兩個 4 維點： x = (5, 8, 0, 1)、y = (4, 7, 1, 0)。差值 |x−y| = (1, 1, 1, 1)。
共變異矩陣 Σ = diag(2, 3, 0.5, 1)，Σ⁻¹ = diag(0.5, 0.333, 2, 1)。 x = (5, 8, 0, 1), y = (4, 7, 1, 0). Diffs |x−y| = (1, 1, 1, 1).
Covariance Σ = diag(2, 3, 0.5, 1), Σ⁻¹ = diag(0.5, 0.333, 2, 1).
Euclidean
L2 距離： d = √(1² + 1² + 1² + 1²) = √4 = 2.000 → 拿尺直線量，得 2。 → Direct ruler measure: 2.
Manhattan
L1 距離： d = 1 + 1 + 1 + 1 = 4.000 → 各維差絕對值相加，是 Euclidean 的兩倍。 → Sum of absolute differences — twice the Euclidean value here.
Chebyshev
L∞ 距離： d = max(1, 1, 1, 1) = 1.000 → 因為每維差相同，最大值就是 1。 → All dims tied at 1, so max = 1.
Cosine
餘弦距離： ‖x‖ = √(25+64+0+1) = √90 ≈ 9.487；‖y‖ = √(16+49+1+0) = √66 ≈ 8.124。
x·y = 5·4 + 8·7 + 0·1 + 1·0 = 76。
sim = 76 / (9.487 × 8.124) ≈ 76 / 77.075 ≈ 0.986。
d = 1 − 0.986 = 0.014。方向幾乎一致——這就是為什麼 cosine 在「強度」差別大但「模式」相同時很有用。 ‖x‖ = √(25+64+0+1) = √90 ≈ 9.487; ‖y‖ = √(16+49+1+0) = √66 ≈ 8.124.
x·y = 5·4 + 8·7 + 0·1 + 1·0 = 76.
sim = 76 / (9.487 × 8.124) ≈ 0.986.
d = 1 − 0.986 = 0.014 — directions nearly aligned. That's why cosine shines when magnitudes differ but patterns match.
Jaccard
Jaccard 距離 (二元化)： 將 >0 視為 1：x_bin = (1, 1, 0, 1)、y_bin = (1, 1, 1, 0)。
交集 = {pos 1, pos 2} = 2；聯集 = {pos 1, 2, 3, 4} = 4。
J = 2/4 = 0.5；d = 1 − 0.5 = 0.5。 Treat >0 as 1: x_bin = (1, 1, 0, 1), y_bin = (1, 1, 1, 0).
Intersection = {pos 1, 2} = 2; union = {pos 1, 2, 3, 4} = 4.
J = 2/4 = 0.5; d = 1 − 0.5 = 0.5.
Mahalanobis
馬氏距離： d² = (x−y)ᵀ Σ⁻¹ (x−y) = 1·0.5 + 1·0.333 + 1·2 + 1·1 = 3.833。
d = √3.833 ≈ 1.958。
注意第 3 維的權重最高 (2)——因為該維變異最小 (0.5)，1 單位的差距相對更顯著。 d² = (x−y)ᵀ Σ⁻¹ (x−y) = 1·0.5 + 1·0.333 + 1·2 + 1·1 = 3.833.
d = √3.833 ≈ 1.958.
Note dim 3 carries the highest weight (2) — because its variance is smallest (0.5), the 1-unit gap matters most.
總結
比較表： 同一對 (x, y)：Euclidean=2.0、Manhattan=4.0、Chebyshev=1.0、Cosine=0.014、Jaccard=0.5、Mahalanobis=1.96。
Cosine 認為「幾乎相同」（方向接近）；Jaccard 認為「半相似」（集合差一半）；其他都認為「中等相似」。同一對資料，6 種度量，6 種結論。 Same (x, y): Euclidean=2.0, Manhattan=4.0, Chebyshev=1.0, Cosine=0.014, Jaccard=0.5, Mahalanobis=1.96.
Cosine says "nearly identical" (direction); Jaccard says "half similar" (sets); the rest say "moderately similar." One pair, six metrics, six verdicts.
思辨

三個值得爭論的問題

爭論 #1

Cosine 忽略 magnitude，何時是優勢？

兩個文件的 TF-IDF 向量：A 出現「cancer」5 次，B 出現 50 次——它們相似嗎？
Two documents' TF-IDF vectors: A says "cancer" 5 times, B says it 50 times — are they similar?
優勢： 如果它們都聚焦於癌症主題，只是「文件長短」不同——cosine 視為相似很合理（短摘要 vs 長 review article）。 If both focus on cancer themes and differ only by document length — cosine treating them as similar makes sense (short abstract vs long review).
劣勢： 在某些情境 magnitude 才是重點——例如基因表現量「過表現 10×」與「過表現 100×」是質的差異，cosine 會忽略。解：先 log + scale 再用 Pearson 相關。 Sometimes magnitude is what matters — gene expression "10× overexpression" vs "100×" is qualitatively different; cosine ignores this. Fix: log + scale first, then Pearson correlation.
爭論 #2

維度詛咒永遠是壞事嗎？

Aggarwal 2001 證明高維下 Euclidean 距離趨於相等——KNN 變得無意義。但⋯⋯真的嗎？
Aggarwal 2001 proved high-D Euclidean distances converge — KNN becomes meaningless. But is that actually always true?
實務派： 在 omics 與 deep embedding（如 BERT 768 維、CLIP 512 維）中，KNN 仍然很有用——關鍵是「intrinsic dimensionality」遠低於 nominal dimensionality（資料躺在低維 manifold 上）。 In omics and deep embeddings (BERT 768-D, CLIP 512-D), KNN still works well — because intrinsic dimensionality is far below nominal (data lies on a low-D manifold).
理論派： 原始高維「噪音 + 訊號」資料下確實崩盤。解法：先 PCA / UMAP / autoencoder 降維到 intrinsic D 約 20–50，再算距離。或改用 cosine（對 norm 不敏感）。 For raw "noise + signal" high-D data, it does collapse. Fix: PCA / UMAP / autoencoder to intrinsic ~20–50 dims first, then compute distance. Or switch to cosine (norm-insensitive).
爭論 #3

omics 資料應該用 Euclidean 嗎？

2 萬基因 × 10⁵ 細胞的 single-cell 矩陣——用 Euclidean 還是 Cosine？
A 20K-genes × 100K-cells single-cell matrix — Euclidean or Cosine?
觀點 A： 原始 count data 取 log-normalize 後 + Euclidean 是 Seurat 預設——大多數 paper 都這樣做。 After log-normalization on raw counts, Euclidean is Seurat's default — most papers use this.
觀點 B： Cosine 對「technical bias」（例如不同 sequencing depth）較穩健；2023 之後新版 Scanpy 與 scVI 都建議 cosine。Wasserstein 距離（如 PhEMD）更先進但運算貴。 Cosine is more robust to "technical bias" (e.g., differing sequencing depth); Scanpy and scVI post-2023 recommend cosine. Wasserstein (e.g., PhEMD) is more advanced but compute-heavy.

經典案例

Netflix 2014：從 Pearson 到「學習距離」的躍升

2014 年 Netflix 完成從「user-user collaborative filtering with Pearson similarity」遷移到一個學習出的距離 metric（基於 deep embedding + 隱式回饋）。其 A/B test 顯示 watch time 上升 15%、unsubscribe 下降 8%。技術核心是用神經網路將每個使用者與每部電影投影到同一 128-D 向量空間，距離由 cosine + 學習的權重決定。Netflix Tech Blog 描述：「我們不再選擇『一個』距離度量——我們讓資料告訴我們什麼是相似。」

In 2014 Netflix completed a migration from "user-user collaborative filtering with Pearson similarity" to a learned distance metric (deep embedding + implicit feedback). A/B testing showed watch time up 15%, unsubscribes down 8%. The technical core: a neural net projects each user and each film into the same 128-D vector space, with distance = cosine + learned weights. The Netflix tech blog wrote: "We stopped choosing 'a' distance — we let the data tell us what similar means."

啟示：在資料量足夠的場景，metric learning（如 ITML、Siamese network、contrastive learning）可以超越任何固定的 hand-crafted 距離。生醫應用：CellOT 2023 用學習距離整合不同實驗的單細胞資料。

Implication: Given enough data, metric learning (ITML, Siamese networks, contrastive learning) can outperform any fixed hand-crafted distance. Biomedical example: CellOT 2023 uses a learned distance to integrate single-cell data across experiments.

CASE STUDY

Spotify 2014：用 word2vec 為「歌曲」造距離

2014 年 Spotify 工程團隊面臨一個經典挑戰：在 4,000 萬首歌曲中為 7,500 萬使用者做即時推薦。原本的 user-user collaborative filtering（以 Pearson similarity 為核心）在巨量 sparse matrix 上計算昂貴、且對新歌冷啟動失效。Erik Bernhardsson 與 Chris Johnson 領銜的團隊做了一個大膽決定：把「歌曲」當作「單詞」、把「使用者的播放 session」當作「句子」，直接用 Mikolov 2013 的 word2vec 訓練一個 40-D 的 song embedding——然後用 cosine similarity 在這個學到的空間中找鄰居。結果：engagement +15%、churn -3%。更重要的是，這個 embedding 自然解決了冷啟動（新歌一進入 session 就有 embedding）、自然支援 ANN 索引（Annoy 函式庫即由此誕生，2015 開源）。從這一刻起，業界明白：「正確的距離」就是「學出來的距離」。後續 Word2Vec 思維蔓延到 Pinterest (Pin2Vec)、Airbnb (Listing2Vec)、阿里 (Item2Vec)，並最終演化為今日的 dense retrieval 與 RAG。

In 2014, Spotify's engineering team faced a classic challenge: real-time recommendations across 40M songs for 75M users. Their user-user collaborative filtering (Pearson-similarity-based) was expensive on huge sparse matrices and failed cold-start. Led by Erik Bernhardsson and Chris Johnson, they made a bold call: treat "songs" as "words," "user listening sessions" as "sentences," and train a 40-D song embedding with Mikolov's 2013 word2vec — then use cosine similarity in this learned space to find neighbors. Engagement rose 15%, churn dropped 3%. More importantly, the embedding naturally solved cold-start (any new song picked up an embedding as soon as it appeared in a session) and supported ANN indexes (the Annoy library was born here, open-sourced 2015). From this moment on, the industry knew: "the right distance" is a learned distance. The Word2Vec-style trick spread to Pinterest (Pin2Vec), Airbnb (Listing2Vec), Alibaba (Item2Vec), and ultimately to today's dense retrieval and RAG pipelines.

啟示：當你的相似度需要捕捉「行為共現」而非「特徵重疊」時——learned embedding + cosine 幾乎永遠勝過 hand-crafted metric。生醫應用：scRNA-seq 的 Gene2Vec、藥物—靶點的 Mol2Vec 都採用相同精神。

Implication: When your similarity must capture "behavioral co-occurrence" rather than "feature overlap," learned embedding + cosine almost always beats a hand-crafted metric. Biomedical analogs: Gene2Vec for scRNA-seq, Mol2Vec for drug-target — all in the same spirit.

陷阱

五大常見陷阱

① 混合單位算 Euclidean

身高 (cm: 150–200) + 收入 (USD: 30K–500K)——收入差異會完全淹沒身高。Euclidean 認為「年薪差 1 萬」≈「身高差 1 萬 cm」。解：先 StandardScaler。

Height (cm: 150–200) + income (USD: 30K–500K) — income overwhelms height. Euclidean treats "$10K income gap" ≈ "10,000 cm height gap." Fix: StandardScaler first.

② 忽略高維詛咒

在 2 萬維基因表達直接跑 KNN——所有點都「差不多遠」，模型沒有學到任何東西。解：先 PCA 或 UMAP 降到 30–50 維，再算距離。

Running KNN directly on 20K-D gene expression — all points roughly equidistant, model learns nothing. Fix: PCA or UMAP to 30–50 dims first.

③ Cosine 套用 count data

原始文件 word-count 直接 cosine——常用詞（the、of）會主導向量方向。解：先 TF-IDF 賦予稀有詞權重，再 cosine。

Cosine on raw word counts — common words (the, of) dominate the direction. Fix: TF-IDF first to weight rare terms, then cosine.

④ 假設 Cosine 是 metric

Cosine distance 不滿足三角不等式——某些演算法（如 ball tree、metric tree）會給出錯誤結果。解：用 angular distance 1−cosθ/π（嚴格 metric）或改用 brute force。

Cosine distance violates the triangle inequality — algorithms like ball trees and metric trees can return wrong results. Fix: use angular distance 1−cosθ/π (a true metric) or brute-force.

⑤ similarity 與 distance 混用

把 cosine similarity (1=同) 直接丟進 KNN（KNN 期待 distance, 0=同）——結果完全顛倒。解：明確 distance = 1 − similarity 並文件化。

Passing cosine similarity (1=same) into KNN (which expects distance, 0=same) — results flip. Fix: explicit distance = 1 − similarity and document it.

⑥ 混合型用 one-hot + Euclidean

把性別 one-hot 變成 (1,0) / (0,1)，再用 Euclidean——距離永遠是 √2，沒有資訊。解：混合型資料用 Gower 距離 (cluster::daisy)。

One-hot encoding sex as (1,0)/(0,1) then Euclidean — distance is always √2, carrying no info. Fix: Gower distance for mixed types (cluster::daisy).

對比

同一份資料、兩種度量選擇

盲目用 Euclidean (錯誤)

病人資料：年齡 (0–100)、住院天數 (0–365)、Charlson Index (0–24)、藥物數 (0–50)、是否糖尿病 (0/1)。直接 Euclidean——住院天數差異主導，糖尿病標籤幾乎沒貢獻。聚類結果按「住院長短」分群而非「臨床相似度」。

Patient features: age (0–100), LOS (0–365), Charlson (0–24), med count (0–50), diabetes (0/1). Raw Euclidean — LOS overwhelms; diabetes label barely contributes. Clusters separate by "length of stay" rather than clinical similarity.

先選對度量 (正確)

混合型 → Gower distance（cluster::daisy in R / gower package in Python）。每個欄位依類型自動 0–1 範圍 + 加權平均。結果聚類反映「臨床相似度」——糖尿病有/無病人正確分離。

Mixed-type → Gower distance (cluster::daisy in R / gower in Python). Each column auto-normalized to 0–1 by type + weighted mean. Clusters reflect clinical similarity — diabetic vs non-diabetic correctly separate.

距離選擇不是技術細節——它是 ML 系統最深層的 inductive bias。

🌳 進階研究員

2023–2026 距離度量前沿

Wasserstein OT for omics integration (2023+)：把每個樣本視為 distribution（如 single-cell 中每個細胞是 gene expression 的 distribution）——Wasserstein 距離直接比較兩個 distribution。Cuturi 2013 Sinkhorn-EMD 讓計算可規模化；CellOT 2023 (Bunne et al., Nat Methods) 用 OT 整合不同 batch 的 single-cell 資料。DOI。

Wasserstein OT for omics integration (2023+): treat each sample as a distribution (e.g., each cell is a distribution over genes) — Wasserstein compares distributions directly. Cuturi 2013's Sinkhorn-EMD made it scalable; CellOT 2023 (Bunne et al., Nat Methods) integrates single-cell batches via OT.

ITML (Information-Theoretic Metric Learning) — Davis et al. 2007：透過 must-link / cannot-link constraints 學一個 Mahalanobis-like matrix M，使 d²(x,y) = (x−y)ᵀM(x−y)。M 由 KL divergence 正則化更新。實作：metric-learn Python 套件。

ITML (Information-Theoretic Metric Learning) — Davis et al. 2007: learns a Mahalanobis-like M via must-link/cannot-link constraints so that d²(x,y) = (x−y)ᵀM(x−y); M is regularized via KL divergence. Implementation: metric-learn Python package.

CLIP-style 學習距離 (Radford 2021)：用 contrastive learning 將 image + text 投影到同一 embedding 空間；推論時相同類別的 image-text pair cosine ≈ 0.8，跨類 ≈ 0.2。生醫版：BioClip 2024 把組織切片 + 病理報告對齊。

CLIP-style learned distance (Radford 2021): contrastive learning aligns image + text in one embedding space — same-class image-text pairs reach cosine ≈ 0.8, cross-class ≈ 0.2. Biomedical analog: BioClip 2024 aligns histology slides with pathology reports.

Riemannian distances for manifolds (Ye et al. 2024)：當資料躺在曲面上（如 diffusion tensor 的 SPD matrix manifold），直線 Euclidean 是錯的。需要 geodesic distance：在 manifold 表面上「最短曲線」長度。應用：DTI tractography、brain connectivity 比較。

Riemannian distances for manifolds (Ye et al. 2024): when data lies on curved surfaces (SPD matrix manifold of diffusion tensors), straight Euclidean is wrong. Use geodesic distance — the "shortest curve length" on the manifold. Applications: DTI tractography, brain connectivity.

Quantum distance kernels (Schuld 2024, IBM Quantum)：將特徵編碼到量子態 |ψ(x)⟩，量子線路計算 |⟨ψ(x)|ψ(y)⟩|²（quantum fidelity）。可表達某些 classical kernel 做不到的非線性。仍處於 NISQ 階段；對 ≤ 50 量子位元有效。

Quantum distance kernels (Schuld 2024, IBM Quantum): encode features into quantum states |ψ(x)⟩; quantum circuits compute |⟨ψ(x)|ψ(y)⟩|² (quantum fidelity). Captures nonlinearities no classical kernel can. Still NISQ-era; effective at ≤ 50 qubits.
