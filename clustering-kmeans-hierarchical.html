K-Means 與階層式分群

分群是探索性學習的代表：給未標記的資料找到「自然的群組」。K-Means 重速度、Hierarchical 重結構，兩者互補。

Clustering is the flagship of exploratory learning: find natural groups in unlabeled data. K-Means is fast; hierarchical reveals structure — they complement each other.

故事開場

場景：50,000 個單細胞

SCENE · 2024

同一份資料、兩種真相

實驗室凌晨三點。生物資訊師 Mei 正在分析來自人類腦皮層的 50,000 個單細胞 scRNA-seq 資料，希望找出細胞類型。她按教科書順序：先 PCA 取前 30 維，再標準化。第一次嘗試 K-Means、K=5：得到 5 個漂亮的球形群，看起來很乾淨——興奮地寄給 PI。

PI 回信只有一句：「你怎麼知道是 5 群？」隔天她改用 Ward + Euclidean 階層式分群，剪不同高度——畫出來的 dendrogram 顯示有 12 個嵌套的細胞亞群。silhouette 在 K=5、K=8、K=12 處都有局部峰。再跑 Gap statistic：3 個 K 值統計上都顯著。Tabula Muris 的論文用了 23 種。

哪一個才是「真」群數？這正是本章要回答的問題：K-Means 給你「劃分」、階層式給你「結構」、兩者都對——只是回答不同層次的問題。

3 a.m. in the lab. Bioinformatician Mei is staring at a 50,000-cell scRNA-seq dataset from human cortex, hoping to find cell types. She runs the textbook pipeline: PCA down to 30 components, then standard scaling. First try: K-Means with K=5 — five gorgeous spherical blobs. She emails the PI, excited.

The PI replies: "How did you know it was 5?" Next morning, she tries Ward + Euclidean hierarchical clustering and cuts the dendrogram at several heights: 12 nested sub-types appear. The silhouette curve has local peaks at K=5, K=8, and K=12. Gap statistic flags three K values as significant. Tabula Muris used 23.

Which is the "true" number? This is exactly what this chapter answers: K-Means hands you a partition; hierarchical hands you structure; both are right — they just answer different questions.

核心詞彙

12 個關鍵術語

Clustering
分群
把長得像的玩具放一堆，不像的放別堆——但沒有大人告訴你哪些是同一類。
Putting similar toys in piles — but nobody told you which is which.
無監督學習：將 N 個物件分為 K 群使群內相似度高、群間相似度低；目標函數常為 WCSS、平均 silhouette、log-likelihood。
Unsupervised partition of N objects into K groups maximizing intra-cluster similarity. Common objectives: WCSS, mean silhouette, log-likelihood.
Centroid
質心
一群點的「平均位置」，像一群孩子的中心點。
The average location of a group — the centre of mass.
μ_k = (1/|C_k|) Σ_{x∈C_k} x；對 L2 距離為最小化 Σ ‖x − μ_k‖² 的 minimizer。
μ_k = (1/|C_k|) Σ_{x∈C_k} x; minimizer of Σ ‖x − μ_k‖² under L2.
Lloyd's algorithm
Lloyd 演算法
「指派→平均」反覆做，直到沒人換組為止。
Repeat "assign → average" until nobody switches groups.
EM-like 兩步：(E) 指派每點到最近 μ_k；(M) 重算 μ_k = mean(C_k)。對 K-Means 目標函數單調遞減，保證收斂至局部最小。
EM-like two-step: (E) assign each x to nearest μ_k; (M) recompute μ_k as mean of C_k. Monotone decrease in K-Means objective, converges to local minimum.
k-means++
k-means++ 初始化
先挑一個座位，下一個座位要離已挑過的越遠越好——讓初始位置散得開。
Pick a seed; the next seed favours points far from existing seeds — spread the starts.
Arthur & Vassilvitskii 2007；機率 ∝ d(x, 最近已挑質心)²。期望近似比 O(log K)，遠優於隨機 init。
Arthur & Vassilvitskii 2007; sample with probability ∝ d(x, nearest chosen)². Expected O(log K) approximation, far better than random init.
Inertia / WCSS
類內平方和
每個小孩離自己組中心多遠？把所有距離平方加起來，越小越緊密。
How far is each kid from their own group's centre? Sum the squared distances — lower means tighter groups.
WCSS = Σ_k Σ_{x∈C_k} ‖x − μ_k‖²。K 增加時必然單調遞減——這就是 elbow 法的依據。
WCSS = Σ_k Σ_{x∈C_k} ‖x − μ_k‖². Monotonically decreasing in K — the basis of the elbow heuristic.
Silhouette coefficient
輪廓係數
如果你離自己組比較近、離別人組比較遠，分數就高（最高 1）。
If you're closer to your own group than the next group, you score high (max 1).
s(i) = (b−a)/max(a,b)；a=同群平均距、b=最近他群平均距。範圍 [-1, 1]，平均 s 越大越好。Rousseeuw 1987。
s(i) = (b−a)/max(a,b); a = mean within-cluster distance, b = mean distance to nearest other cluster. Range [-1, 1]; higher is better. Rousseeuw 1987.
Gap statistic
Gap 統計量
把資料隨機洗牌當對照組，比較真的 WCSS 比洗牌後低多少。
Compare real WCSS against a shuffled "no-cluster" baseline — how much better are we?
Tibshirani 2001：Gap(K) = E*[log W_K] − log W_K，使用 bootstrap 自參考分佈估期望。選使 Gap(K) ≥ Gap(K+1) − s_{K+1} 的最小 K。
Tibshirani 2001: Gap(K) = E*[log W_K] − log W_K, expectation estimated via bootstrap from a reference distribution. Pick smallest K with Gap(K) ≥ Gap(K+1) − s_{K+1}.
K-Medoids (PAM)
K-中位點
不用「平均座位」，改用「真正存在的點」當中心，比較不怕怪人 (outlier)。
Use an actual data point as the centre, not a hypothetical average — robust to weirdos (outliers).
Partitioning Around Medoids (Kaufman & Rousseeuw 1990)：質心限為真實樣本，最小化絕對距離總和；可搭配任意距離（Gower、Manhattan）。
Partitioning Around Medoids (Kaufman & Rousseeuw 1990): centre restricted to actual data points; minimizes sum of absolute distances; works with any distance (Gower, Manhattan).
Agglomerative hierarchical
凝聚式階層分群
從每個人都是一群，最像的兩群合併，再合併，再合併⋯⋯像家族樹由下往上長。
Start with each point alone; merge the two most similar; repeat — bottom-up family tree.
每步合併「最近」兩群，由 linkage 函數定義「最近」。複雜度 O(N² log N)（heap-based）至 O(N³)（naive）。
At each step merge the two nearest clusters under a linkage function. Complexity O(N² log N) heap-based to O(N³) naive.
Ward linkage
Ward 連結
合併時挑「讓總體變最小」的兩群——最小化亂度。
Merge whichever pair causes the smallest jump in overall spread.
Ward 1963：ΔE = |C_a||C_b|/(|C_a|+|C_b|) · ‖μ_a − μ_b‖²；只在歐氏距離下定義良好。傾向產生大小相近的球狀群。
Ward 1963: ΔE = |C_a||C_b|/(|C_a|+|C_b|) · ‖μ_a − μ_b‖²; well-defined only under Euclidean. Tends to produce balanced, spherical clusters.
Dendrogram
樹狀圖
像家族樹：縱軸是「合併距離」、橫軸是樣本，剪不同高度就得到不同群數。
A family tree: y-axis is "merge distance," x-axis is samples; cut at different heights for different K.
遞迴二元樹，葉=樣本、節點高=合併時的 linkage 距離。提供 multi-resolution view；可由 cophenetic distance 評估保真度。
A recursive binary tree: leaves = samples, node height = linkage distance at merge. Gives a multi-resolution view; fidelity assessed by cophenetic distance.
Cophenetic correlation
餘弦相關係數
樹狀圖把兩個人放多遠？vs 他們真實的差距？越像越好。
How far apart does the tree put two points vs how far they actually are? Closer match = better tree.
corr(cophenetic distance, original pairwise distance)；>0.75 一般視為 dendrogram 忠實保留原距離結構。Sokal & Rohlf 1962。
corr(cophenetic distance, original pairwise distance); >0.75 generally taken as the dendrogram faithfully preserving structure. Sokal & Rohlf 1962.

🧑‍🏫
CLASSROOM ANALOGY

K-Means：分組坐位；Hierarchical：建家族樹

想像一群學生站在操場上。K-Means 像是請 K 個老師散開站好——每個學生走到「最近」的老師那邊，然後老師走到自己學生群的中心。重複，直到沒人換組。階層式 則是先把每位學生當「家族」，找到最相近的兩個家族合併成核心家庭，再合併成大家族，再合併成整個社區——畫成樹，剪不同高度就得不同分組。

Picture students on a playground. K-Means is like K teachers spreading out — each student walks to the nearest teacher; each teacher moves to the centre of their students. Repeat until no one switches. Hierarchical starts with every student as a one-person "family"; merge the two closest into a nuclear family, then merge into extended families, then a whole community — draw the tree, cut at any height for any K.

點開看：為何 K-Means 容易卡在局部最小？
K-Means 最小化的 WCSS 是 非凸 函數（隨 partition 階梯式變化）。Lloyd 演算法每步單調遞減，但只保證收斂到當下 basin 的底；對稱配置如「兩群被分成上下半」是常見的局部最小。對策：(1) 多重 random init 取最佳（n_init=25 是慣例），(2) k-means++（讓初始質心遠離已選），(3) 用 hierarchical 結果做為 init。
K-Means minimizes WCSS, which is non-convex (stepwise in the partition). Lloyd's algorithm decreases monotonically but only finds the bottom of the current basin; symmetric mistakes like "splitting two clusters into top and bottom halves" are classic local minima. Fixes: (1) many random inits (n_init=25 is standard), (2) k-means++ (seeds far apart), (3) initialize from hierarchical labels.

概覽

兩大派典：劃分式 vs 階層式

劃分式 (partitional)：使用者事先指定 k，演算法找到使類內平方和 (WCSS) 最小的指派。代表：K-Means、K-Medoids (PAM)、CLARA。

階層式 (hierarchical)：建立樹狀 dendrogram，可由下而上 (agglomerative) 或由上而下 (divisive)。不需事先決定 k——切樹高度即得分群。

在生物醫學應用上：基因表現的 patient subtyping（TCGA 各 cancer type 都用 Ward + Euclidean 起家），單細胞 RNA-seq 用 Leiden / Louvain，臨床表型分群常用 K-Medoids + Gower。

Partitional: user specifies k, the algorithm finds an assignment that minimizes within-cluster sum of squares (WCSS). Examples: K-Means, K-Medoids (PAM), CLARA.

Hierarchical: builds a dendrogram, either bottom-up (agglomerative) or top-down (divisive). k need not be pre-specified — cut the tree at a chosen height.

Biomedical examples: patient subtyping from gene expression (TCGA tumor-type analyses often start with Ward + Euclidean), single-cell RNA-seq uses Leiden / Louvain, and clinical phenotype clustering often pairs K-Medoids with Gower distance.

K-MEANS 細節

一、Lloyd 演算法與 k-means++

Lloyd 迭代

1) 初始化 k 個質心。2) 指派每點到最近質心。3) 重算每群平均成為新質心。4) 重複至收斂或 max_iter。

1) Initialize k centroids. 2) Assign each point to nearest centroid. 3) Recompute means as new centroids. 4) Repeat until convergence or max_iter.

k-means++

隨機初始化常陷入區域最小值。k-means++ 以「距離平方加權」依序挑質心，提高初始品質——sklearn 與 R 的 stats::kmeans 預設皆採用。

Random init often stalls at local minima. k-means++ picks subsequent seeds with probability proportional to squared distance — both sklearn and R's stats::kmeans use it by default.

⚠️
K-Means 的隱含假設：(1) 群為球狀且等變異、(2) 群大小相近、(3) 特徵須事先 scaling。違反任一條件時，DBSCAN / GMM 更合適。 K-Means assumes: (1) clusters are spherical with equal variance, (2) similar cluster sizes, (3) features are pre-scaled. Violate any of these and DBSCAN / GMM may be better.

逐步演算

K-Means 手算：6 個點、K = 2

6

A(1,1) B(1.5,2) C(3,4) D(5,7) E(3.5,5) F(4.5,5)

K=2 · Lloyd
Step 1
初始化質心。隨機挑 c1 = A = (1, 1)、c2 = D = (5, 7)（最遠的兩點，k-means++ 也會這樣挑）。 Initialize centroids. Pick c1 = A = (1, 1), c2 = D = (5, 7) — the two farthest points, also what k-means++ would pick.
Step 2
指派每點。A→c1 (d=0)、B→c1 (d=1.12)、C→c1 (d=3.61)；D→c2 (d=0)、E→c2 (d=2.5)、F→c2 (d=2.06)。
C1 = {A, B, C}；C2 = {D, E, F}。 Assign each point. A→c1 (d=0), B→c1 (d=1.12), C→c1 (d=3.61); D→c2 (d=0), E→c2 (d=2.5), F→c2 (d=2.06).
C1 = {A, B, C}; C2 = {D, E, F}.
Step 3
更新質心。c1 = mean(A,B,C) = ((1+1.5+3)/3, (1+2+4)/3) = (1.83, 2.33)。c2 = mean(D,E,F) = (4.33, 5.67)。 Update centroids. c1 = mean(A,B,C) = (1.83, 2.33). c2 = mean(D,E,F) = (4.33, 5.67).
Step 4
再指派。距離重算後：A、B 仍歸 C1；C 距 c1=2.32、距 c2=2.05，切換 至 C2。D、E、F 仍歸 C2。
C1 = {A, B}；C2 = {C, D, E, F}。 Reassign. Recomputing: A, B stay in C1; C is 2.32 from c1 and 2.05 from c2 — switches to C2. D, E, F stay.
C1 = {A, B}; C2 = {C, D, E, F}.
Step 5
再更新質心。c1 = (1.25, 1.5)；c2 = (4.0, 5.25)。第三輪指派與上一輪相同——收斂！ Update again. c1 = (1.25, 1.5); c2 = (4.0, 5.25). The third pass yields the same assignment — converged!
Step 6
WCSS 計算。WCSS = ‖A − c1‖² + ‖B − c1‖² + ‖C − c2‖² + ‖D − c2‖² + ‖E − c2‖² + ‖F − c2‖² ≈ 0.31 + 0.31 + 2.56 + 4.06 + 0.31 + 0.31 ≈ 7.85。
對比 K=3：WCSS 降到 ~2.1，但 silhouette 可能下降——這就是「複雜度 vs 緊密度」的拉鋸。 WCSS calculation. WCSS = ‖A − c1‖² + ‖B − c1‖² + ‖C − c2‖² + ‖D − c2‖² + ‖E − c2‖² + ‖F − c2‖² ≈ 0.31 + 0.31 + 2.56 + 4.06 + 0.31 + 0.31 ≈ 7.85.
For K=3: WCSS drops to ~2.1, but silhouette may dip — the classic "complexity vs tightness" trade-off.
動手做：把這六點用 sklearn 重跑，比較與手算結果——分群是少數能完全手算驗證的演算法之一。
思辨

三個常被爭辯的問題

Q · METHOD

怎麼挑 K？Elbow / Silhouette / Gap 意見不一時聽誰的？

三個指標各有偏好：Elbow 主觀、Silhouette 偏好分得開的群、Gap 統計嚴謹但慢。實務上常出現 Elbow K=4、Silhouette K=6、Gap K=8。

Three criteria, three preferences: Elbow is subjective; Silhouette favours well-separated clusters; Gap is rigorous but slow. You'll often see Elbow K=4, Silhouette K=6, Gap K=8.

觀點 A（生物學家）： 「我有先驗知識——我知道腦皮層有 6 大細胞型。直接 K=6 並驗證亞型。」 "I have priors — cortex has 6 broad cell types. Set K=6 and validate sub-types."
觀點 B（統計學家）： 「Gap statistic 給出統計顯著的 K——任何主觀挑法都是 cherry picking。」 "Gap statistic gives a significant K — any subjective pick is cherry picking."
觀點 C（資料科學家）： 「下游任務（分類準確率、生物意義）說了算——選讓下游表現最好的 K。」 "Let the downstream task decide — pick the K that maximizes downstream classification or biological interpretability."
Q · BIAS

K-Means 真的「偏好球狀群」嗎？

是的——因為 WCSS 用歐氏距離平方。即使真實群是橢圓或鏈狀，K-Means 會把它切成幾個球。診斷：跑完 K-Means 後檢查每群的 covariance——若特徵值差很大就是被切碎了。對策：(1) PCA 後 whitening、(2) GMM with full covariance、(3) DBSCAN。

Yes — because WCSS uses squared Euclidean distance. Even if the true clusters are elliptical or chain-shaped, K-Means slices them into spheres. Diagnosis: after K-Means, check per-cluster covariance — large eigenvalue spread means you fragmented an elongated cluster. Fixes: (1) PCA + whitening, (2) GMM with full covariance, (3) DBSCAN.

Q · LINKAGE

生物資料該用 Ward 還是 Average linkage？

基因表現 / scRNA-seq 通常用 Ward + Euclidean——產生大小相近的緊湊群，適合 patient subtyping。系統發育 (phylogenetics) 則用 Average linkage (UPGMA) + 演化距離，理論上是 ultrametric tree。Single linkage 在 microbial 16S 上常見但容易 chain。

For gene-expression / scRNA-seq: Ward + Euclidean — yields balanced compact groups, great for patient subtyping. Phylogenetics uses average linkage (UPGMA) + evolutionary distance, theoretically an ultrametric tree. Single linkage is common in 16S microbial work but chains easily.

🌟
SPOTLIGHT · 2019

Tabula Muris Senis：跨組織老化圖譜

2019 年 Nature 發表的 Tabula Muris Senis 計畫測序了老鼠 23 個組織、~ 350,000 個細胞，定義 23 種共通細胞型。方法：先做 hierarchical clustering 找 broad-class、再 Louvain on KNN graph 細分亞型——hybrid 方法成為 scRNA-seq 標準作業。所有結果以 cell-type ontology 表示，下游研究者可直接呼叫。

The 2019 Nature paper sequenced 23 mouse tissues, ~350,000 cells, defining 23 shared cell types. Pipeline: hierarchical clustering for broad classes, then Louvain on a KNN graph for sub-types — this hybrid became the standard scRNA-seq workflow. Results were exported as a cell-type ontology, queryable by any downstream user.

陷阱

K-Means / 階層式 五大常見錯誤

忘了 scaling

基因表現 TPM 範圍 0–10⁵，年齡 0–100——不 scale，年齡完全沒影響。對策：K-Means 前一定 StandardScaler 或 log1p。

Gene TPM ranges 0–10⁵, age 0–100. Without scaling, age has zero influence. Fix: always StandardScaler or log1p before K-Means.

K 固定不驗證

用 K=3 因為「直覺」——但 silhouette、gap、生物意義都該檢查。對策：掃 K=2–15，畫 elbow + silhouette + gap 三聯圖。

"K=3 because intuition" — but silhouette, gap, and biological meaning should all be checked. Fix: sweep K=2–15, plot elbow + silhouette + gap together.

K-Means 跑類別變數

「性別=男/女」做 one-hot 進 K-Means 沒道理——平均「0.5 男」是什麼？對策：K-Modes (Huang 1998) 或 K-Prototypes（混合）。

One-hot encoding gender into K-Means makes no sense — what is "0.5 male"? Fix: K-Modes (Huang 1998) or K-Prototypes (mixed).

Ward + 非歐距離

Ward 的數學定義只在歐氏空間有意義；對 Manhattan、Gower、cosine 用 Ward 是統計上的錯誤。對策：非歐距離請改用 average/complete linkage。

Ward is only well-defined in Euclidean space; using Ward with Manhattan, Gower, or cosine is a statistical error. Fix: use average/complete linkage for non-Euclidean distances.

Dendrogram 剪一刀不驗證

剪在 h=2.5 因為「畫起來剛好」——但 bootstrap stability 才告訴你哪個分支可信。對策：pvclust（Suzuki & Shimodaira 2006）給每節點 p-value。

Cutting at h=2.5 because "it looks right" — but bootstrap stability tells you which branches are reliable. Fix: use pvclust (Suzuki & Shimodaira 2006) for per-node p-values.

忽略 outlier 影響

一個極端值能把 K-Means 質心拉走、把 Ward 的合併方向扭曲。對策：K-Medoids (PAM) 對 outlier 比 K-Means 穩健；或先以 IQR 過濾。

A single outlier can drag a K-Means centroid or skew a Ward merge. Fix: K-Medoids (PAM) is more robust; or filter outliers via IQR first.

不好的做法

直接 KMeans(n_clusters=5).fit(raw_data)，不 scale、不掃 K、不驗證。報告寫「5 個亞型」就送 PI。

Just KMeans(n_clusters=5).fit(raw_data) — no scaling, no K sweep, no validation. Email PI saying "5 sub-types".

好的做法

(1) log1p + StandardScaler；(2) n_init=25, init="k-means++"；(3) 掃 K=2–15、看 silhouette + gap；(4) 用 bootstrap stability 驗證；(5) 與生物學標註對齊。

(1) log1p + StandardScaler; (2) n_init=25, init="k-means++"; (3) sweep K=2–15 with silhouette + gap; (4) bootstrap stability; (5) align with biological annotation.

  1. 為什麼 K-Means 的 WCSS 隨 K 增加單調遞減？這意味著 K=N 時 WCSS=0——這對嗎？
  2. Why does WCSS monotonically decrease with K? That means K=N gives WCSS=0 — is that right?
  3. 想像你的資料分布是兩條平行直線（如基因 X 與其轉錄產物）。K-Means 會給出什麼樣的分群？階層式呢？
  4. Suppose your data lies on two parallel lines (e.g., gene X and its transcript). What partition does K-Means give? Hierarchical?
  5. silhouette = 0.3 算「好」嗎？取決於什麼？
  6. Is a silhouette of 0.3 "good"? It depends on what?

  8. What's the relationship between Ward and GMM (spherical Σ)? If any, is it a coincidence?
  9. 為什麼說「K-Means 是 EM 的特例」？
  10. Why is K-Means often called "a special case of EM"?
進階

2024–2026 最前沿

SOTA · 2024–2026

從 mini-batch 到 streaming + consensus

Streaming K-Means。BIRCH (Zhang 1996) 至今仍是大型流式資料的標準預處理：建 CF-tree 樹存匯總統計，再對葉節點 K-Means。2024 cuML 在 GPU 上把 BIRCH + Mini-batch K-Means 結合，處理 10M+ 點 < 30 秒。

Streaming K-Means. BIRCH (Zhang 1996) is still the go-to preprocessing for large streaming data: build a CF-tree of summary statistics, then K-Means on the leaves. In 2024 cuML chained BIRCH + Mini-batch K-Means on GPU to process 10M+ points in < 30 s.

Consensus clustering。Monti et al. 2003 (Mach. Learn.) — 反覆對 subsample 跑 K-Means，記錄每對樣本「在同一群」的頻率，得到 consensus matrix。這是 TCGA cancer subtyping 的標配，至今仍寫進 BMC Bioinformatics 教程。延伸：ConsensusClusterPlus R 套件 (Wilkerson 2010)、SHC 階層版本 (2024)。

Consensus clustering. Monti et al. 2003 (Mach. Learn.) — re-run K-Means on subsamples, count how often each pair lands together, build a consensus matrix. Standard in TCGA cancer subtyping, still in BMC Bioinformatics tutorials. Extensions: ConsensusClusterPlus R package (Wilkerson 2010), SHC sub-hierarchical clustering (2024).

單細胞的當代做法。2026 Seurat v5、Scanpy 1.10 預設都不再用 K-Means——而是 PCA → KNN graph → Leiden (Traag et al. 2019)。原因：Leiden 不需事先指定 K、自動決定群數、處理弱連結時比 Louvain 穩定。K-Means 退居「展示 / 初篩」的角色。

Modern single-cell practice. Seurat v5 and Scanpy 1.10 (2026) no longer use K-Means by default — they use PCA → KNN graph → Leiden (Traag et al. 2019). Reason: Leiden needs no K, decides cluster count automatically, and is more stable than Louvain on weakly connected graphs. K-Means is now a "demo / first-pass" tool.

論文路徑。Lloyd 1957/1982 (IEEE Trans Inf Theory); Ward 1963 (JASA); Arthur–Vassilvitskii 2007 (SODA); Tibshirani 2001 (JRSS-B); Monti 2003 (Mach Learn 52); Wilkerson 2010 (Bioinformatics); Traag 2019 (Sci Rep); Tabula Muris Consortium 2018, 2020 (Nature); Stuart 2019 (Cell, Seurat v3)。

Paper trail. Lloyd 1957/1982 (IEEE Trans Inf Theory); Ward 1963 (JASA); Arthur–Vassilvitskii 2007 (SODA); Tibshirani 2001 (JRSS-B); Monti 2003 (Mach Learn 52); Wilkerson 2010 (Bioinformatics); Traag 2019 (Sci Rep); Tabula Muris Consortium 2018, 2020 (Nature); Stuart 2019 (Cell, Seurat v3).
