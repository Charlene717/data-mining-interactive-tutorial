開場故事
病例

🏥 11 點 03 分的潛在低血鉀

外科加護病房的 12 號床，57 歲術後第二天。床邊監視器每 30 秒記錄一次：心率 (HR)、血壓 (BP)、SpO₂、呼吸頻率 (RR)、體溫，再加上每 6 小時抽血一次的血鉀 (K⁺)、肌酸酐。值班護理師眼睛瞄到的螢幕顯示一切「在範圍內」——HR 88、BP 122/76、SpO₂ 97%。然而背景跑著一個 Isolation Forest 異常偵測模型，它監控 14 維生命徵象向量；早上 11 點 03 分，警示燈悄悄亮起：score=0.78，警報層級「黃」。

單看每個變數都正常，但 14 維「協同模式」異常：HR 在 PR interval 微妙拉長、SpO₂ 下緣下移 1.2%、體溫掉 0.4°C——這正是 低血鉀引發 ECG 早期變化 的特徵組合。3 小時後，10:53 抽血結果出來：K⁺ = 2.6 mEq/L。臨床團隊提前補鉀，避免了致命的尖端扭轉型心律不整 (Torsades de Pointes)。這就是異常偵測：找出「整體不該出現的點」，比任何單一閾值早一步。

Bed 12, surgical ICU, post-op day 2, 57-year-old. The bedside monitor logs HR, BP, SpO₂, RR, temperature every 30 s, plus K⁺ and creatinine every 6 h. To the eye, everything is "in range" — HR 88, BP 122/76, SpO₂ 97%. But a background Isolation Forest detector sniffing a 14-dim vital signs vector lights up at 11:03 AM with score=0.78, alert level "yellow".

No single variable is abnormal — but the joint 14-D pattern is: a subtle PR-interval lengthening, SpO₂ floor dropping 1.2%, temperature 0.4°C down. That is the early-ECG fingerprint of hypokalaemia. The 11 AM blood draw lands 3 h later: K⁺ = 2.6 mEq/L. Clinical team pre-empts the potassium crash and dodges a Torsades de Pointes. That is anomaly detection — catching what "shouldn't be there" before any threshold blinks red.

概覽

什麼是異常？三種類型

異常 (anomaly / outlier) 是「顯著偏離整體分布」的觀測。Hawkins (1980) 的定義：「一個與其他觀測差異大到讓人懷疑它由不同機制產生的點」。在生物醫學上，這通常代表 儀器錯誤、罕見疾病、不良事件，或一個重要訊號的前兆（如 ICU 病人惡化）。

異常依結構分三類：(1) 點異常 (Point)——單筆觀測本身極端，如 HbA1c=20；(2) 情境異常 (Contextual)——只在特定情境下異常，如夏季氣溫 5°C；(3) 集體異常 (Collective)——個別正常但組合異常，如 ECG 突然規律的平坦段 (asystole)。

An anomaly / outlier is an observation that deviates markedly from the bulk. Hawkins (1980): "an observation that differs so much from others that it appears generated by a different mechanism." In biomedicine this often means instrument error, rare disease, adverse event, or an early warning of deterioration.

Three structural types: (1) Point — a single extreme record (HbA1c=20); (2) Contextual — anomalous only in context (5°C in summer); (3) Collective — individually normal but combined anomalous (a flat ECG segment indicating asystole).

類比

異常偵測就像博物館警衛

Z-score 是看身高體重：偏離平均多遠？LOF 是看這個人是否「比鄰居更孤獨」——周圍人很多但他自己孤伶伶。Isolation Forest 則像玩 20 問遊戲：隨機問問題，能用很少問題就把這個人從人群中分出來的，多半就是闖入者。Autoencoder 像把訪客拍照、再請畫家憑記憶重畫——畫得最不像的就是「不正常」的訪客。

Z-score watches stature: how far from average? LOF asks "is this point lonelier than its neighbors?" — surroundings dense, but it sits alone. Isolation Forest plays 20-questions: if random splits separate someone from the crowd in just a few cuts, they're likely an intruder. Autoencoder photographs each visitor and asks an artist to redraw from memory — the worst likeness is the outlier.

名詞地圖

關鍵名詞（小孩版 + 技術版）

Anomaly / Outlier
異常值 / 離群點
就是「不應該出現在這裡的點」，像一群企鵝裡的火鶴。
A point "that doesn't belong" — a flamingo standing in a flock of penguins.
與大多數觀測差異大到看似由另一個機制產生 (Hawkins 1980)。
An observation so different it looks generated by a different mechanism (Hawkins 1980).
Novelty
新奇點
「沒看過的新東西」，跟訓練時的世界不一樣。
"Never-seen-before" — different from what we saw during training.
在「純正常」訓練集上學包絡，新樣本若偏離即新奇；半監督場景。
An envelope learned from normal-only training data; new points outside it are novel (semi-supervised).
Point Anomaly
點異常
單獨一筆「值」就明顯不對，例如血壓 320。
A single value is obviously off, like BP 320.
在整體分布中位於極端尾部的單一觀測，無需上下文。
A single observation in the extreme tail of the marginal distribution.
Contextual Anomaly
情境異常
夏天 35°C 沒問題，冬天 35°C 才奇怪——要看上下文。
35°C in summer is fine; 35°C in winter is weird — context matters.
在條件分布 P(X | context) 下異常；需顯式建模上下文變數。
Anomalous under P(X | context); requires explicit context modelling.
Collective Anomaly
集體異常
每個小步都正常，連起來卻不對——像 ECG 突然平坦一秒。
Each step alone is normal, but the sequence is wrong — like a flat ECG second.
子序列/子集合層級偏離，需時序或結構模型（LSTM-AE、TranAD）。
A subsequence/subset deviates; needs sequence-aware models (LSTM-AE, TranAD).
Z-score / IQR rule
標準分數 / 四分位距規則
看「離平均幾個標準差」或「在中位數方圓的 1.5 倍範圍外」。
"How many SDs from the mean?" or "outside 1.5× the inter-quartile range?"
Z=|x−μ|/σ；IQR：x < Q1−1.5·IQR 或 x > Q3+1.5·IQR。對重尾分布敏感。
Z=|x−μ|/σ; IQR rule flags x < Q1−1.5·IQR or x > Q3+1.5·IQR. Heavy-tail sensitive.
Mahalanobis distance
馬氏距離
考慮「變數彼此關聯」的距離——身高 180+ 體重 50 看起來才奇怪。
A distance that accounts for "how variables move together" — 180 cm + 50 kg looks odd.
D² = (x−μ)ᵀΣ⁻¹(x−μ)；對多變量常態下尾部 χ² 分布。
D² = (x−μ)ᵀΣ⁻¹(x−μ); follows χ²(d) under multivariate normality.
LOF (Local Outlier Factor)
局部離群因子
「我比我的朋友更孤獨嗎？」>1 就是。
"Am I lonelier than my friends?" > 1 = yes.
LOF_k(p) = mean(lrd(q)/lrd(p)) over k 個鄰居 q (Breunig 2000)。
LOF_k(p) = mean over k-NN of lrd(q)/lrd(p) (Breunig 2000).
Isolation Forest
孤立森林
用隨機切割切到只剩這個點，需要的切割越少越異常。
Random cuts until only this point remains — fewer cuts = more anomalous.
s(x,n) = 2^(−E(h(x))/c(n))，c(n)=2·H(n−1)−2(n−1)/n (Liu 2008)。
s(x,n) = 2^(−E(h(x))/c(n)), c(n)=2·H(n−1)−2(n−1)/n (Liu 2008).
ECOD
經驗累積分布偵測器
用「尾巴有多薄」打分，每個變數獨立看。
Scores by "how thin the tail is" — one variable at a time.
無參、O(nd)；對每維 ECDF 取 −log(min(F,1−F)) 後加總 (Li 2022)。
Parameter-free, O(nd); per-dim ECDF tail score −log(min(F,1−F)) summed (Li 2022).
One-Class SVM
單類別 SVM
畫一個圈把所有「正常」包進去；圈外就是異常。
Draw a bubble around all "normals"; outside = anomaly.
Schölkopf 2001；ν 控制邊界鬆緊；常用 RBF kernel。
Schölkopf 2001; ν tunes the envelope; RBF kernel typical.
Autoencoder reconstruction error
自編碼器重建誤差
壓縮再展開，「畫得不像」的就是異常。
Compress then restore — worst likenesses are anomalies.
‖x − f_θ(g_θ(x))‖²；常見 deep SVDD, VAE-LL, DAGMM。
‖x − f_θ(g_θ(x))‖²; variants: deep SVDD, VAE-LL, DAGMM.
contamination ratio
汙染率
你猜會有多少「壞蘋果」——影響閾值。
Your guess at the "bad-apple" fraction — sets the threshold.
sklearn 用此設 decision threshold；非真實基率，是「願意誤標」率。
sklearn uses it to set the decision threshold — a willingness-to-misflag, not the true base rate.
PyOD
PyOD 套件
Python 異常偵測「工具箱」，60+ 個偵測器一個介面。
A "Swiss-army toolbox" — 60+ detectors behind one API.
Zhao 2019 JMLR；v2.0 (2024) 加 LLM 選模型；v3.0 (2025) 多模態。
Zhao 2019 JMLR; v2.0 (2024) adds LLM-based selection; v3.0 (2025) multi-modal.
Unsupervised vs Semi-supervised
無監督 vs 半監督
無監督＝沒 label；半監督＝只用「正常」訓練。
Unsupervised = no labels; semi-supervised = train on normals only.
無監督 (IF, LOF, ECOD) 假設稀少；半監督 (OC-SVM, AE) 學包絡。
Unsupervised (IF, LOF, ECOD) assumes rarity; semi-supervised (OC-SVM, AE) learns an envelope.
時間線

從 1969 Grubbs 到 2026 PyOD 3

1969 Grubbs 離群值檢定統計界第一個正式的單變量離群測驗：T = max|x−x̄|/s。簡單但僅限近常態。First formal univariate outlier test: T = max|x−x̄|/s. Simple but normality-bound.
2000 Breunig LOFSIGMOD 經典——首度把「局部密度比」變成可計算分數，能處理變密度群集。SIGMOD classic — first to make "local density ratio" a computable score, handles varying density.
2008 Liu/Ting/Zhou Isolation ForestICDM 翻轉典範——不需建「正常模型」、用「易隔離」反過來定義異常。ICDM paradigm shift — no normal model needed; "isolatability" inverts the problem.
2017 PyOD 套件首發 (Zhao)JMLR；30+ 偵測器同介面，從此 Python 異常偵測「平民化」。JMLR; 30+ detectors behind one API — anomaly detection becomes accessible in Python.
2018 Deep SVDD (Ruff)ICML；把 One-Class SVM 推進深度神經網路時代。ICML; brings One-Class SVM into the deep era.
2022 ECOD (Li)TKDE；無參、極快、可解釋（每維尾部分數可加總）；常打贏複雜模型。TKDE; parameter-free, fast, interpretable (per-dim tail scores sum). Often beats complex models.
2024 MGDD + TranAD多變量 Gaussian + 差分圖；TranAD 用 transformer 處理多元時序。Multivariate Gaussian + differential graph; TranAD applies transformers to multivariate time series.
2025 PyOD 2.0 + LLM 選模型12 個深度模型 + LLM-based MetaOD 自動選擇——資料看一眼就推薦最適偵測器。12 deep models + LLM-based MetaOD — auto-recommend the best detector after a glance.
2026 PyOD 3 多模態60+ 偵測器，跨 tabular / time / graph / text / image；序列感知 (LSTM-AE, TranAD)。60+ detectors across tabular / time / graph / text / image; sequence-aware (LSTM-AE, TranAD).
演算法家族

一、五大偵測方法家族

📊

① 統計型 Statistical

Z-score、modified Z (MAD)、IQR (1.5×)、Grubbs、Mahalanobis（多變量+共變異）。簡單、可解釋，但假設分布。

Z-score, modified Z (MAD), IQR (1.5×), Grubbs, Mahalanobis (multivariate + covariance). Simple, interpretable, but assumes a distribution.

📏

② 距離型 Distance

KNN distance score：到第 k 個鄰居的距離越大越異常。對全域結構敏感、易受維度詛咒。

KNN distance score — the larger the distance to the k-th neighbor, the more anomalous. Captures global structure; suffers from curse of dimensionality.

🌌

③ 密度型 Density

LOF (Local Outlier Factor)：比較點的局部密度與鄰居的局部密度比值，>1 即異常。能處理變密度群集。

LOF (Local Outlier Factor) — ratio of a point's local density vs its neighbors'; >1 means anomalous. Handles varying-density clusters.

🌲

④ 隔離型 Isolation

Isolation Forest：隨機切割空間，異常點通常用更少切割就被隔離（路徑短）。O(n log n) 線性可擴展。

Isolation Forest — random partitioning; anomalies are isolated with fewer splits (shorter paths). O(n log n), highly scalable.

🧠

⑤ 學習型 Learning

One-Class SVM（決策邊界包絡）、Autoencoder（重建誤差 reconstruction error）、ECOD（empirical CDF, 無參、O(nd)）。

One-Class SVM (envelope boundary), Autoencoder (reconstruction error), ECOD (empirical CDF, parameter-free, O(nd)).

🔬

⑥ PyOD 2.0 / 3

PyOD 2.0 (2024) 加入 12 個深度模型與 LLM-based 自動選擇；PyOD 3 (2025) 擴至 60+ 偵測器，涵蓋 tabular/time/graph/text/image。

PyOD 2.0 (2024) added 12 deep models + LLM-based selection; PyOD 3 (2025) expands to 60+ detectors across tabular/time/graph/text/image.

Q4:

💡
無監督 vs 半監督：無監督 (Isolation Forest, LOF, ECOD) 不需 label，假設異常稀少；半監督 (One-Class SVM, Autoencoder) 在「純正常」資料上訓練，偵測新偏離。監督式則需要標記異常——但通常異常太少。 Unsupervised vs semi-supervised: Unsupervised (Isolation Forest, LOF, ECOD) needs no labels and assumes anomalies are rare. Semi-supervised (One-Class SVM, Autoencoder) trains on "normal-only" data and flags deviations. Supervised needs labeled anomalies — usually too few to be practical.

手算演練

Isolation Forest：10 位病人、6 步走完

IF

情境：10 位 ICU 病人、2 維特徵 (HR, K⁺)

n=10 · 100 trees
STEP 1
建 100 棵隨機樹。每棵樹從 10 位病人中抽 256（這裡 n<256 全用），每次切一個隨機特徵、隨機切點，直到每位病人各自獨佔一個葉節點。
Build 100 random trees. Each tree subsamples 256 patients (here n<256 so use all), at every node pick a random feature and random split until each patient sits alone.
STEP 2
逐位病人計算平均路徑長 E(h(x))。病人 #7 (HR=148, K⁺=2.4) 平均只走 3.1 層就被切出；其餘 9 位平均走 6.5 層才孤立。
Compute average path length E(h(x)) per patient. Patient #7 (HR=148, K⁺=2.4) gets isolated after only 3.1 levels on average; the other 9 take 6.5 levels.
STEP 3
路徑短 = 異常。直覺：異常點處於空間中孤立位置，少數隨機切割就能與其他分開。常態樣本擠在密集區，要多次切才能逐一隔離。
Short path = anomaly. Anomalies sit in sparse regions; a few random cuts already isolate them. Normals cluster densely — many cuts required.
STEP 4
計算正規化分數 s(x,n) = 2^(−E(h(x))/c(n))，其中 c(10)=2·H(9)−18/10 ≈ 3.7。病人 #7 分數 = 2^(−3.1/3.7) = 0.56；常態病人 ≈ 2^(−6.5/3.7) = 0.30。
Normalise score s(x,n) = 2^(−E(h(x))/c(n)) with c(10)=2·H(9)−18/10 ≈ 3.7. Patient #7: 2^(−3.1/3.7) = 0.56; normals ≈ 2^(−6.5/3.7) = 0.30.
STEP 5
選 top-5% 為旗標。n=10 → top-1 = 病人 #7。Sklearn 的 contamination=0.1 會剛好設此閾值。
Flag top 5%. n=10 → top-1 = patient #7. sklearn's contamination=0.1 sets exactly this cut.
STEP 6
用 ground truth 驗證。已知病人 #7 確實是「低血鉀引發 ECG 變化」的真實案例 → TP=1，FP=0，Precision=1.0，Recall=1.0。實務上會跑 ROC/PR 看各閾值下的表現。
Validate with ground truth. Patient #7 was indeed hypokalaemia → TP=1, FP=0, Precision=1.0, Recall=1.0. In practice scan ROC/PR across thresholds.
s(x, n) = 2^(−E(h(x)) / c(n))其中 c(n) = 2·H(n−1) − 2(n−1)/n，H(k) 為調和數；s 越接近 1 越異常，0.5 為基準。where c(n) = 2·H(n−1) − 2(n−1)/n, H(k) = harmonic number; s near 1 = anomalous, 0.5 = baseline.
🔬 為什麼用對數 / c(n) 正規化？

在一棵 BST 中，隨機分裂下隔離一個葉的期望深度 等於 c(n) = 2H(n−1) − 2(n−1)/n（與 BST 不成功查詢的平均路徑相同 / Knuth Vol.3）。因此把每點 E(h(x)) 除以 c(n) 後再放進 2^(−·) 才能把分數壓在 (0,1) 並讓 0.5 ≈ 平均樣本。如果不做正規化，n 不同的資料集分數無法比較。

In a binary-search-tree built by random splits, the expected depth to isolate a leaf equals c(n) = 2H(n−1) − 2(n−1)/n (same as the average path of unsuccessful BST searches, Knuth Vol. 3). Dividing E(h(x)) by c(n) and feeding into 2^(−·) compresses the score to (0,1) and centres "average" at 0.5. Without it, scores are not comparable across different n.

研究討論

三個值得思考的真問題

Q1

無監督 vs 半監督——什麼時候用哪種？

兩者表面相似，差別在「你信不信你的訓練集是純淨的」。

They look alike, but differ on "do you trust your training set to be pristine?"

無監督觀點：真實世界很難拿到「保證乾淨」的資料；ICU 病歷裡早有未被發現的低血鉀。Isolation Forest、LOF、ECOD 假設「異常稀少」，能容忍少量汙染。
Unsupervised view: Real data is rarely guaranteed pristine — undetected hypokalaemia hides in ICU logs. IF, LOF, ECOD assume "anomalies are rare" and tolerate mild contamination.
半監督觀點：如果你能保證「術前正常生命徵象 24 h」是純淨的，那 One-Class SVM 或 Autoencoder 可以學一個極緊的「正常包絡」，靈敏度比無監督高很多。製造業 SECS-II 異常監控常採此路。
Semi-supervised view: If "pre-op 24 h vitals" really are clean, OC-SVM or AE learn a tight "normal envelope" with much higher sensitivity than unsupervised. Common in semiconductor SECS-II monitoring.
Q2

為什麼 contamination 永遠設錯？

sklearn 預設 0.1，但真實敗血症基率 ~0.5%、ICU 心臟事件 ~2%，永遠差幾個數量級。

sklearn defaults to 0.1, but real sepsis base rate is ~0.5%, ICU cardiac events ~2% — off by orders of magnitude.

實務妥協：把 contamination 視為「我願意每天看幾個警報」而非真實基率。每天 1000 筆觀測、要 ≤10 警報 → contamination ≈ 0.01。再用 ROC/PR 在標記資料上挑「不依賴此參數」的閾值。
Pragmatic stance: Treat contamination as "how many alerts I can handle per day", not the true base rate. 1000 obs/day, want ≤10 alerts → contamination ≈ 0.01. Use ROC/PR on labelled data to pick a parameter-free threshold.
Q3

2026 年深度自編碼器真的勝過 Isolation Forest 嗎？

DAGMM、deep SVDD、TranAD 等深度模型大放異彩；但 ECOD/IF 仍常出現在 OOD benchmark 前段。

DAGMM, deep SVDD, TranAD shine on benchmarks — yet ECOD/IF still appear near the top of OOD leaderboards.

支持深度：在影像、ECG 波形、多模態時序，深度模型抓非線性結構，2024 ADBench benchmark 在 21/48 任務上深度勝出。
Pro-deep: On images, ECG waveforms, multi-modal time series, deep models capture non-linear structure — ADBench 2024 found deep wins in 21/48 tasks.
反對深度：同一 ADBench 顯示，在 27/48 tabular 任務上 IF / ECOD 仍最佳——而且訓練 1000× 快、不需 GPU、可解釋。Han 2022 結論："深度沒有銀彈"。
Anti-deep: The same ADBench: IF/ECOD top on 27/48 tabular tasks — 1000× faster, no GPU, interpretable. Han 2022: "deep is no silver bullet".

案例

Google Borg 叢集異常監控系統 (2020-)

Google 全球資料中心由 Borg cluster manager 統一調度，每天運行數千萬 job。2020 年起導入 PyOD + 自製 LSTM Autoencoder 異常偵測系統，監控 40,000+ 節點的 CPU、記憶體、磁碟 IO、網路延遲——每天偵測到約 2,000 個節點異常，避免異常累積成大規模當機。據 SRE 報告，光是「無用 job 提前砍掉」一年省下約 2,000 萬美元的閒置運算費用。

Google's global datacentres run millions of jobs/day under the Borg cluster manager. Since 2020 a PyOD + custom LSTM Autoencoder system monitors 40,000+ nodes on CPU, memory, disk IO, network latency — catching ~2,000 node anomalies/day before they cascade. SRE reports estimate ~$2 M/year saved by killing zombie jobs early.

教訓：(1) 多模型 ensemble 比單一模型穩定；(2) 結合 領域規則（CPU>95%×30s 等）和 ML score；(3) 嚴格 人工複檢前 5% 警報，避免 alert fatigue。

Lessons: (1) multi-model ensembles beat single models; (2) combine domain rules (CPU>95% × 30 s) with ML scores; (3) strict human review of top-5% to dodge alert fatigue.

常見陷阱

五個地雷與修正

① contamination 設成真實基率

敗血症 0.5% 設 contamination=0.005 看似合理，但模型會因「太罕見」連真異常也錯過。修正：視為「每日警報配額」，從 0.01–0.05 開始 + PR-curve 調整。

Sepsis 0.5% → contamination=0.005 sounds principled, but the model misses true positives. Fix: treat it as "daily alert budget"; start at 0.01–0.05 and tune via PR-curve.

② 在被汙染的資料上訓練

把 ICU 全部歷史當「正常」訓練 OC-SVM——但 ICU 本來就有 5% 病人惡化。修正：用「術前 24 h」或「出院健康」資料；或改用對汙染穩健的無監督方法。

Using all ICU history as "normal" for OC-SVM — but 5% truly deteriorated. Fix: use pre-op 24 h or discharge-stable data; or switch to contamination-robust unsupervised models.

③ 忽略情境異常

把所有觀測攤平處理——但「夜間 HR=120」和「白天 HR=120」意義不同。修正：加入 time-of-day、體位、活動分類為條件特徵；用 conditional anomaly detection。

Flatten everything — but HR=120 at night ≠ HR=120 mid-day. Fix: include time-of-day, posture, activity as conditioning features; use conditional anomaly detection.

④ 深度自編碼器過擬合異常

模型容量過大時，連異常都重建得很好——重建誤差失靈。修正：限制 bottleneck 維度、加 noise、用 robust loss (Huber)，或改 deep SVDD/DAGMM。

Over-capacity nets reconstruct anomalies too well — error breaks down. Fix: tight bottleneck, denoising, robust loss (Huber), or switch to deep SVDD/DAGMM.

⑤ 用 accuracy 評估嚴重不平衡

0.5% 真實異常下，永遠預測「正常」的 dummy 模型 accuracy 99.5%——卻完全沒用。修正：用 PR-AUC、F1、precision-at-k，並繪 ROC + PR 曲線。

At 0.5% base rate, "always normal" dummy hits 99.5% accuracy — useless. Fix: use PR-AUC, F1, precision-at-k; plot ROC + PR curves.

⑥ 沒看分數分布

直接信 contamination 閾值，沒看分數分布——bimodal vs unimodal 意義天差地別。修正：每次跑完務必畫 score histogram，雙峰才能信賴閾值。

Trusting contamination blindly without viewing the score histogram — bimodal vs unimodal differ profoundly. Fix: always plot the score histogram; only trust thresholds when modes separate.

壞做法 vs 好做法

壞做法

把 ICU 過去 6 個月全部資料當「正常」訓練 OC-SVM，contamination 預設 0.1，最後用 accuracy 報告「99.7% 準確」，結束。

Train OC-SVM on 6 months of unfiltered ICU data, leave contamination=0.1 default, report "99.7% accuracy" — done.

好做法

用「術前 24h 穩定區段」當 normal-only 訓練 + 用 IF/ECOD 做 ensemble；contamination 從 0.01 起跑、PR-AUC 校正、繪 score-histogram + ROC + PR；和臨床團隊一起 review top-20 警報；每月用新 label 重訓。

Train on "pre-op 24 h stable" as normal-only + ensemble with IF/ECOD; start contamination=0.01, tune via PR-AUC; plot score histogram + ROC + PR; review top-20 alerts with clinicians; monthly retrain with new labels.

研究前沿
SOTA 2025-2026

從 PyOD 2.0 到多模態異常偵測

PyOD 2.0 (2024)：引入 LLM-based MetaOD——把資料元統計（dim、cardinality、skewness）丟給 LLM，請它推薦最適 detector + hyperparam。實測在 30 個 ADBench tabular 任務平均 AP 提升 ~12%。

PyOD 2.0 (2024) introduces LLM-based MetaOD — feed dataset meta-stats (dim, cardinality, skewness) to an LLM, get a detector + hyper-parameter recommendation. On 30 ADBench tabular tasks, avg AP +12%.

MGDD (NeurIPS 2024)：多變量高斯混合 + 學習差分圖 (differential graph)，把「變數間關係」也納入異常判斷，在多元時序如 ICU 14-lead ECG 取得 SOTA。

MGDD (NeurIPS 2024): multivariate Gaussian mixture + learned differential graph — captures inter-variable structure, SOTA on multivariate time series like 14-lead ECG.

TranAD (VLDB 2022, 2025 extension)：Transformer + 對抗訓練，把點異常與集體異常一起學；用於 NASA SMAP/MSL 衛星遙測，F1 較 LSTM-AE 提升 30%+。

TranAD (VLDB 2022, 2025 extension): Transformer + adversarial training, jointly models point + collective anomalies. On NASA SMAP/MSL telemetry, F1 up 30%+ vs LSTM-AE.

PyOD 3 (2026)：宣稱「第一個跨 5 模態」(tabular / time / graph / text / image) 的統一框架。包含 sequence-aware 模型 (LSTM-AE, TranAD)、圖層級偵測 (DOMINANT)、視覺異常 (PaDiM, FastFlow)。未來方向：LLM-as-detector、causal anomaly attribution、聯邦學習下的醫療異常偵測。

PyOD 3 (2026) claims to be the first cross-5-modality framework (tabular / time / graph / text / image): sequence-aware (LSTM-AE, TranAD), graph-level (DOMINANT), visual (PaDiM, FastFlow). Frontiers: LLM-as-detector, causal anomaly attribution, federated medical anomaly detection.
