🦠
CH 3

1854 倫敦霍亂

John Snow 醫師在 Broad Street 街口畫了一張死亡地圖，揭穿了「瘴氣理論」、確認了水井污染。視覺化拯救生命的原型故事。

Dr. John Snow drew a death map at the Broad Street intersection — overturned miasma theory and identified contaminated wells. The original visualization-saves-lives story.

🚀
CH 1

2009 Netflix 百萬美元挑戰

BellKor's Pragmatic Chaos 團隊集合 800+ 模型奪冠——示範完整 CRISP-DM 循環在規模化下的價值。後續啟發了一整個世代的 ensemble 工程。

BellKor's Pragmatic Chaos won by combining 800+ models — demonstrating CRISP-DM's value at scale; inspired a generation of ensemble engineering.

⚖️
CH 20

2019 Obermeyer Science

一個服務 2 億美國人的健康演算法——黑人病人被低估需求 50%。原因：用「醫療花費」當「醫療需求」的代理變數。引爆整個演算法公平性研究領域。

A US healthcare algorithm serving 200M Americans systematically under-served Black patients by 50% — used "cost" as proxy for "need." Sparked the field of algorithmic fairness.
