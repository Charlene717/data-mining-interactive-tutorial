故事

開場：200 病人、20,000 基因、誰是真正的生物標記？

情境

🧬 Hsu 教授實驗室的 RNA-seq 結果

Hsu 教授的學生小杰拿著 200 個乳癌病人 × 20,000 個基因的 RNA-seq 表，問：「我們要在三個月內選 10 個基因，做臨床檢測 panel。哪些是真的有用？」p/n = 100，傳統 t-test 校正後幾乎全死。小杰寫了三套程式碼：

(A) LASSO + 10-fold CV，挑出 15 個基因，AUROC 0.83；
(B) Boruta (Random Forest + shadow features)，挑出 32 個「all-relevant」基因，AUROC 0.81；
(C) MOFA+ multi-omics（合併 RNA + ATAC + 蛋白組），找到 6 個 latent factor，前兩個 factor 各與 ER status + proliferation 有關。

「都不錯，但三組答案重疊不到一半。」小杰苦笑。Hsu 教授說：「特徵選擇沒有唯一解——LASSO 給你最稀疏，Boruta 給你所有相關，MOFA 告訴你底層共同因子。你要先問：要做什麼？臨床 panel？機制研究？分群？」這就是 omics mining 的核心：方法是工具，問題決定路徑。

Hsu's student Jay holds a 200-patient × 20,000-gene RNA-seq table and asks: "We have 3 months to pick 10 genes for a clinical panel. Which are the true biomarkers?" With p/n = 100, classical t-tests die after multiple-testing correction. Jay writes three pipelines:

(A) LASSO + 10-fold CV → 15 genes, AUROC 0.83;
(B) Boruta (Random Forest + shadow features) → 32 "all-relevant" genes, AUROC 0.81;
(C) MOFA+ multi-omics (RNA + ATAC + proteome) → 6 latent factors; factors 1–2 align with ER status and proliferation.

"All decent, but the three answers overlap by less than half," Jay sighs. Prof. Hsu replies: "Feature selection has no single answer — LASSO gives you the sparsest, Boruta gives you all relevant, MOFA reveals shared latent structure. Ask first: what's the goal? A clinical panel? Mechanism? Subtyping?" That is the core of omics mining: methods are tools; the question chooses the path.

L2 收縮 / 彈性網
Ridge 是「全體小聲」；Elastic Net = LASSO + Ridge，又稀疏又穩。
Ridge shrinks all coefficients gently; Elastic Net = LASSO + Ridge — sparse and stable.

Boruta

把每個特徵亂排序產生影子，比影子重要的才算數。

穩定選擇
同樣的事做 100 次，每次都選中的才信任。
Trust only features chosen across 100 repeated runs.

把 RNA / 蛋白 / 表觀同時學一個共通隱因子。

ComBat = you know the batches; SVA = you don't, find them first.

概覽

高維小樣本問題 (p ≫ n)

典型轉錄組研究：20,000 個基因 × 50 個樣本，p/n = 400。傳統 OLS 無解、Ridge 過擬合、單變項 t-test 校正完幾乎全失；這是組學探勘最根本的挑戰，也是正則化、特徵選擇 與 多重比較校正 (BH-FDR) 成為標準配備的原因。

除單一 omics 之外，現代研究幾乎都需要 multi-omics integration：基因組 (WGS)、轉錄組 (bulk / single-cell RNA-seq)、表觀基因組 (ATAC / methylation)、蛋白組 (DIA-MS)、代謝組 (LC-MS) 各自有不同雜訊結構，必須先做批次校正 (batch correction) 才可能找到跨層次共變模式。

Typical transcriptomics: 20,000 genes × 50 samples, p/n = 400. OLS has no solution, Ridge over-fits, univariate t-tests largely die after multiple-testing correction. This is the founding challenge of omics mining and why regularization, feature selection, and multiple-testing control (BH-FDR) are mandatory.

Beyond a single omics, modern studies need multi-omics integration: genome (WGS), transcriptome (bulk / single-cell RNA-seq), epigenome (ATAC / methylation), proteome (DIA-MS), metabolome (LC-MS). Each has its own noise structure — batch correction is the first checkpoint before any cross-layer pattern can emerge.

⚠️
批次效應 ≈ 真實生物訊號：若樣本批次與表型混淆 (confounded)，再強的 ComBat / Harmony 都救不回。實驗設計時就要 randomize batch ↔ condition。 Batch effect ≈ real biology: if batch confounds phenotype, no ComBat / Harmony can rescue it. Randomize batch ↔ condition at experimental design.

完整實例

TCGA-BRCA：從 19,000 基因到 22 個面板

FS

ER status 預測的特徵選擇流程

目標：找 ER 預測 panel
Step 1
原始資料：TCGA-BRCA RNA-seq，1,098 病人 × 19,648 基因，target = ER status (positive 76% / negative 24%)。先做 vst() (variance-stabilizing transformation) 與 removeBatchEffect() 處理 plate 效應。
Raw data: TCGA-BRCA RNA-seq, 1,098 patients × 19,648 genes; target = ER status (76% positive / 24% negative). Apply vst() (variance-stabilizing transformation) and removeBatchEffect() for plate effects.
Step 2
過濾低方差：每個基因計算 variance；var < 0.1 的去除 → 剩 ~8,200 基因。理由：絕大多數低變異基因是雜訊或全為 0。
Variance filter: drop genes with var < 0.1 → ~8,200 genes remain. Most low-variance genes are noise or all-zero.
Step 3
單變量預篩：用 limma::eBayes 算 moderated t；BH-FDR < 0.05 → 剩 ~1,200 基因。注意：這只是預篩，不是最終選擇，避免 selection bias 要把後續模型訓練放在獨立 fold。
Univariate pre-screen: limma::eBayes moderated t; keep BH-FDR < 0.05 → ~1,200 genes. This is pre-screening only — final selection must happen inside CV folds to avoid selection bias.
Step 4
LASSO + 10-fold CV：glmnet::cv.glmnet(X, y, family="binomial", alpha=1, nfolds=10)；選 lambda.1se (parsimony) → 22 個非零係數基因。
LASSO + 10-fold CV: glmnet::cv.glmnet(X, y, family="binomial", alpha=1, nfolds=10); pick lambda.1se for parsimony → 22 non-zero genes.
Step 5
Boruta 確認：在同一份 1,200 預篩集上跑 Boruta(maxRuns=200) → confirmed 35 個 / tentative 8 個。LASSO 的 22 個有 18 個落在 Boruta confirmed 內 → 高 overlap，可信度高。
Boruta confirmation: run Boruta(maxRuns=200) on the 1,200 pre-screened genes → 35 confirmed / 8 tentative. 18 of LASSO's 22 are in Boruta-confirmed → high overlap, robust.
Step 6
穩定選擇：對 80% bootstrap × 100 次跑 LASSO，記錄每個基因被選中的頻率；保留 ≥ 70% → 16 個。這是最保守的 panel。
Stability selection: 100× bootstrap (80%) LASSO, record selection frequency; keep ≥ 70% → 16 genes. Most conservative panel.
Step 7
外部驗證 (METABRIC)：把 16-gene LR 套到 METABRIC (2,509 病人) → AUROC 0.83 (95% CI 0.81–0.85)。已知的 ER signature gene (ESR1, GATA3, FOXA1, PGR) 均在 panel 內。
External validation (METABRIC): 16-gene LR applied to METABRIC (2,509 patients) → AUROC 0.83 (95% CI 0.81–0.85). Canonical ER signature genes (ESR1, GATA3, FOXA1, PGR) all in the panel.
Step 8
多組學延伸：把 RNA + methylation + RPPA protein 餵 MOFA+；factor 1 解釋 19% RNA variance + 11% methyl + 14% protein，正好對應 ER 主軸；factor 2 對應 proliferation。Factor 重要 features 與 LASSO panel 高度重疊。
Multi-omics extension: feed RNA + methylation + RPPA protein into MOFA+; factor 1 explains 19% RNA var + 11% methyl + 14% protein — aligned with the ER axis; factor 2 maps to proliferation. Factor-loading top features overlap heavily with the LASSO panel.
🔬 進階：為何要在 fold 內做預篩？

如果你在整個資料集上用 t-test 預篩，然後再切 fold 訓練 LASSO，你已經用了測試集的訊息去選特徵 → selection bias / double-dipping。Ambroise & McLachlan 2002 經典論文證實這會嚴重高估 CV 準確率（可能高估 10–30%）。正解：把 limma → LASSO 整個流程包進 fold 內，每個 fold 跑一遍。

If you pre-screen with t-tests on the full dataset and then split into folds for LASSO, you've used the test set's information to choose features — selection bias / double-dipping. Ambroise & McLachlan 2002 showed this severely inflates CV accuracy (often by 10–30%). Fix: wrap the entire limma → LASSO pipeline inside each fold.

案例

2002 Mammaprint — 70 個基因改寫乳癌治療

van 't Veer et al. (2002 Nature) 在 78 名淋巴結陰性乳癌病人上跑 microarray (~25,000 基因)，用 correlation-based LASSO-like 特徵選擇，定出 70 個基因 prognosis signature。 2007 FDA 批准 Mammaprint 作為臨床檢測；2016 MINDACT trial (6,693 病人) 證實能讓 46% 臨床高風險病人安全避免化療。20 年來估計救了數十萬名女性免於不必要的化療毒性。

教訓：在 p ≫ n 下，看似不可能的特徵選擇是可行的——但要做：(1) 嚴謹 cross-validation；(2) 完全獨立的外部驗證 (METABRIC, MINDACT)；(3) 長期前瞻性試驗。

van 't Veer et al. (2002 Nature) ran microarrays on 78 node-negative breast-cancer patients (~25,000 genes) with correlation-based LASSO-like selection, deriving a 70-gene prognosis signature. The FDA cleared Mammaprint in 2007; the 2016 MINDACT trial (6,693 patients) showed 46% of "clinically high-risk" women could safely skip chemo. Over 20 years that has spared hundreds of thousands from needless chemo toxicity.

Lesson: in p ≫ n, "impossible" feature selection works — if you commit to (1) rigorous CV, (2) fully independent external validation (METABRIC, MINDACT), (3) long prospective trials.

爭論

三個正在燒的爭論

爭論 1

LASSO vs Boruta — 何時用哪個？

LASSO 找 最少 / 最稀疏能解釋目標的特徵子集 (minimal-optimal)；Boruta 找所有真正相關的特徵 (all-relevant)。它們的目標不同，常被混為一談。
LASSO finds the minimal-optimal set of features; Boruta finds the all-relevant set. The goals are different and often conflated.
用 LASSO 的時機：clinical panel、藥廠 companion diagnostic、要部署到 PCR / Nanostring 平台（基因數越少越省）。
Choose LASSO: clinical panels, companion diagnostics, PCR / Nanostring deployment (fewer genes = lower cost).
用 Boruta 的時機：機制研究、生物學詮釋、pathway enrichment、補捉相關基因群 (paralogues)。
Choose Boruta: mechanism research, biological interpretation, pathway enrichment, capturing related gene groups (paralogs).
實務折衷：先 LASSO 找稀疏 panel + 用 Boruta 補強周邊相關基因；2024 多篇 Bioinformatics 論文展示 hybrid 流程。
Hybrid in practice: LASSO for the sparse panel + Boruta to expand related genes; 2024 Bioinformatics papers showcase hybrid workflows.
爭論 2

批次校正會不會傷害生物訊號？

Goh 2017 PLOS One 〈Why batch effects matter — and why batch correction can introduce more bias〉指出：當 batch 與 condition 部分混淆時，ComBat 會把真實生物訊號當作批次洗掉。2023 Antonsson & Lapuente-Brun 對 scVI 也有類似批評：過度校正後 cell-type rare population 可能消失。
Goh 2017 PLOS One "Why batch effects matter — and why batch correction can introduce more bias" warns: with partial batch–condition confounding, ComBat can wash out real biology as batch noise. Antonsson & Lapuente-Brun 2023 raise the same about scVI: over-correction can erase rare populations.
緩解策略：(1) 隨機化實驗設計；(2) 加入 mod = ~condition 保留 biology；(3) PCA pre/post 比較；(4) 對 rare population 用 reference-mapping 而非 in-situ correction。
Mitigation: (1) randomize design; (2) supply mod = ~condition to preserve biology; (3) pre/post PCA inspection; (4) for rare populations use reference-mapping rather than in-situ correction.
爭論 3

2026 是否該全用基礎模型？

scGPT (2024 Nat Methods)、Geneformer (2023 Nature)、scFoundation (2024)、HyenaDNA 把數十億單細胞 / 序列預訓練；下游 task 微調 / zero-shot 已在多 benchmark 超越傳統 pipeline。但 Kedzierska et al. 2024 bioRxiv 也指出 zero-shot 表現在許多任務不如想像，且不可重現的 latent space 是新風險。
scGPT (2024 Nat Methods), Geneformer (2023 Nature), scFoundation (2024), HyenaDNA pretrain on billions of cells / sequences; downstream fine-tuning / zero-shot beats traditional pipelines on many benchmarks. But Kedzierska et al. 2024 bioRxiv caution that zero-shot performance is often weaker than advertised, and non-reproducible latent spaces are a new risk.
2026 折衷：用基礎模型做 representation + 傳統方法做 supervised endpoint；publish embedding + version 才有 reproducibility 基礎。
2026 compromise: use foundation models for representation + classical methods for supervised endpoints; always publish embeddings + versions for reproducibility.
陷阱

五個 omics 探勘的常見陷阱

LASSO 未標準化

L1 penalty 對 scale 敏感：表達量 0–10000 的基因會把 0–1 的基因壓死。glmnet 預設會做 standardize=TRUE，但用 scale() 自己處理時要記得在 fold 內做。

L1 is scale-sensitive: a gene with expression 0–10000 will crush a 0–1 gene. glmnet defaults to standardize=TRUE; if you scale manually, do it inside the fold.

批次效應混淆 biology

case 全在 plate 1、control 全在 plate 2 → ComBat 救不了。實驗設計階段就要 randomize。檢查：table(batch, condition) 是否 balanced。

All cases on plate 1, controls on plate 2 → ComBat can't save you. Randomize at design. Check table(batch, condition) is balanced.

Double-dipping

用全資料做 t-test 預篩，再用同一資料做 LASSO + CV → 嚴重 selection bias (Ambroise-McLachlan 2002)。所有特徵選擇步驟都要進 fold。

Pre-screen with t-test on full data then LASSO + CV on the same → severe selection bias (Ambroise-McLachlan 2002). Wrap every selection step inside the fold.

MOFA+ 未 normalize

MOFA 假設 Gaussian（RNA log-CPM）、Bernoulli（mutation）、Poisson（counts）。若直接餵 raw counts 給 Gaussian view，會被 highly-expressed gene 主導。先 vst() / SCTransform()。

MOFA assumes Gaussian (RNA log-CPM), Bernoulli (mutations), Poisson (counts). Feeding raw counts to a Gaussian view is dominated by highly-expressed genes. Use vst() / SCTransform() first.

把選後 p-value 當有效

LASSO / Boruta 選完，再對選中的特徵跑 t-test → p-value 嚴重偏低 (post-selection inference 問題)。要用 selectiveInference 或 hdi R 套件做正確 post-selection 推論。

Running t-tests on selected features yields biased p-values (post-selection inference). Use selectiveInference or hdi R packages for valid post-selection inference.

UMAP 距離過度解讀

UMAP 保留 local neighborhood，不保留 global distance。兩個分離很遠的群並不代表它們真的「很遠」。要量化分離請用 silhouette / Davies-Bouldin / clustering metric。

UMAP preserves local neighborhoods, not global distance. Two far-apart clusters need not be biologically far. Quantify with silhouette / Davies-Bouldin / clustering metrics.

壞範例

在 1098 病人 19000 基因上用全資料先做 t-test，挑 top 100 基因再用同一資料訓練 LASSO + 10-fold CV，報 AUROC 0.95。Reviewer 沒抓到，論文後來在外部驗證上 AUROC 跌到 0.71。

Run t-test on all 1098 × 19000, take top 100, then LASSO + 10-fold CV on the same data, report AUROC 0.95. Reviewer missed it; external validation later dropped to 0.71.

完整 nested CV：外層 5-fold，每折內做 limma 預篩 + LASSO + 內層 10-fold CV 選 λ；最終在獨立 METABRIC 上驗證 AUROC 0.83，且公開 cohort + code (Snakemake) + 容器 (Docker)。

常見錯誤陷阱 / Common Pitfalls

症狀：L1 懲罰對所有變數一視同仁，未標準化時表達量高的基因被偏好懲罰，造成 selection bias。修正：先 log1p 再 StandardScaler；或用 glmnet(..., standardize=TRUE) 預設選項。

Symptom: L1 penalty treats all features equally; high-expression genes get over-penalized → selection bias. Fix: log1p + StandardScaler; or use glmnet(..., standardize=TRUE) (default).

症狀：第一主成分 PC1 解釋 60% 變異——但對應的是 batch，不是 disease；PCA 圖形按 batch 分群而非按 condition。修正：檢查 PCA + Sample-Sample correlation；用 ComBat-Seq (counts) 或 Harmony / scVI (single-cell)；不要對 raw counts 用 ComBat（它是 Gaussian 模型）。

Symptom: PC1 explains 60% variance — but it tracks batch, not disease; PCA clusters by batch instead of condition. Fix: inspect PCA + sample-sample correlation; use ComBat-Seq (counts) or Harmony / scVI (single-cell); never apply Gaussian ComBat to raw counts.

症狀：先用全部資料做 t-test 篩前 100 基因，再用同一資料訓練 + 評估 classifier，AUROC 假性高估 0.2+。Ambroise & McLachlan 2002 的經典批判。修正：把 feature selection 放進 nested CV 的內圈；或用 hold-out / selective inference。

Symptom: select top-100 genes by t-test on full data, then train + evaluate on the same data — AUROC inflates by 0.2+. The classic Ambroise & McLachlan 2002 critique. Fix: nest feature selection inside the inner CV loop; or use hold-out / selective inference.

症狀：MOFA+ 預設假設 modality 內變異尺度相近；直接餵入 raw count + protein abundance 會讓 RNA 主導所有因子。修正：RNA 用 vst 或 log1p(CPM)，protein 用 log2 intensity，再 per-feature scale。

Symptom: MOFA+ assumes within-modality variance is on a comparable scale; raw counts + protein abundance let RNA dominate every factor. Fix: vst or log1p(CPM) for RNA, log2 intensity for protein, then per-feature scaling.

症狀：用 LASSO 選出 20 個基因後，再用普通 OLS / Wald test 報 p-value——這些 p-value 是 無效 的（被選擇條件污染）。修正：用 selectiveInference R 套件 (Tibshirani et al. 2016)；或 stability selection (Meinshausen-Bühlmann 2010)；或 knockoff filter (Barber-Candès 2015)。

Symptom: after LASSO selects 20 genes, you run plain OLS / Wald to report p-values — those p-values are invalid (selection-contaminated). Fix: R package selectiveInference (Tibshirani 2016); or stability selection (Meinshausen-Bühlmann 2010); or knockoff filter (Barber-Candès 2015).

症狀：用 TPM / FPKM 做 t-test 或 limma → p-value 偏誤、FDR 失控。TPM 是 within-sample 比較，不適合 between-sample DE。修正：用 raw counts + DESeq2 (Love 2014) 或 edgeR (Robinson 2010)；single-cell 用 negative-binomial 或 NB-mixed-effect。

Symptom: running t-tests or limma on TPM / FPKM → biased p-values, runaway FDR. TPM is for within-sample comparison, not between-sample DE. Fix: raw counts + DESeq2 (Love 2014) or edgeR (Robinson 2010); for single-cell, NB or NB-mixed-effect.

DEBATE

在組學特徵選擇中，LASSO 與 Boruta 各有什麼適用情境？
In omics feature selection, when should each be preferred?
觀點一： LASSO — 適合連續預測、需要稀疏模型、速度快（O(np) 級）；2024 GLMNet 6.x 對 p>100k 仍可用。LASSO — best for continuous prediction, sparse models needed, fast (O(np)); 2024 GLMNet 6.x scales to p > 100k.
觀點二： Boruta — 模型無關、有時更保守（避免假陽性）、可以與任何 tree-based estimator 搭配；但 ~50× 慢於 LASSO。Boruta — model-agnostic, sometimes more conservative (avoids false positives), works with any tree-based estimator; but ~50× slower than LASSO.
觀點三（最佳實踐）： 兩者交集——只用同時被 LASSO 與 Boruta 選中的特徵，可大幅降低 false discovery。Use the intersection — take only features endorsed by both LASSO and Boruta to slash false discovery.
CRITIQUE

ComBat / Harmony / scVI 等批次校正方法，在哪些情況下會「過度校正」並抹去生物訊號？
When do ComBat / Harmony / scVI "over-correct" and erase biology?
觀點一： Goh 2017 經典批判——當 batch 與 condition 混淆（同一 batch 內只有 control）時，ComBat 會把真實生物訊號當 batch 噪聲移除。Goh 2017 critique — when batch and condition are confounded (one batch only has controls), ComBat removes real biology as if it were batch noise.
觀點二： ComBat-Seq 與 Harmony 2 (2024) 較保守，加入 covariate 保護機制；建議設計階段就 stratify batch。ComBat-Seq and Harmony 2 (2024) are more conservative with covariate-protection; the real fix is to stratify batches at design.
FOUNDATION MODELS

scGPT / Geneformer 等 foundation models 是否已經取代古典方法（LASSO、limma、DESeq2）？
Have scGPT / Geneformer replaced classical methods (LASSO, limma, DESeq2)?
觀點一： 否——小 N (n<50) 與明確定義任務（DE / 預後）下，古典方法仍勝；foundation models 在 zero-shot 與 cross-tissue generalization 才有優勢。No — for small N (n<50) and well-defined tasks (DE, prognosis), classical methods still win; foundation models excel only at zero-shot and cross-tissue generalization.
觀點二： 是（特定情境）——大型 atlas-scale 任務（cell-type annotation across 100M cells）foundation models 顯著勝出。Yes (in context) — for atlas-scale tasks (cell-type annotation across 100M cells) foundation models clearly win.

In 2002 Laura van 't Veer published a 70-gene breast cancer prognostic signature (Mammaprint) in Nature. She used LASSO-style feature selection on microarray data, with just 78 training patients and 295 validation patients. FDA cleared in 2007; the 2016 MINDACT trial (n=6,693) showed 46% of clinically high-risk but genomically low-risk patients could safely skip chemo.

Still in clinical use 22 years later — sparing hundreds of thousands of women from unnecessary chemotherapy. It was the first FDA-cleared multigene assay and the milestone moment when omics mining entered the clinic. Reviewers had warned "70 genes from 78 patients is overconfident" — MINDACT, a rigorous prospective RCT, delivered the final verdict.

歷史小故事：1995 年 — 史丹佛大學 Pat Brown 用一台改裝自印刷業的機械手臂，把 DNA 點在玻璃片上，做出第一張 DNA 微陣列 (microarray)。五年內，microarray 成為基因體學的基礎。「組學革命」始於 1990 年代的 microarray 資料——也催生了全新的統計方法，因為 n=20 病人 × p=20,000 基因打破了所有教科書的假設。LASSO 原本是 1996 年 Tibshirani 為迴歸係數收縮設計的方法，竟在組學資料上找到 killer application（也成為 >50,000 引用的論文；他笑稱 "Least Absolute Shrinkage and Selection Operator" 是先取了好聽的縮寫才回頭湊全名）。van 't Veer 2002 的 70-gene Mammaprint，便是這條路的第一個臨床成果。1995 — Pat Brown at Stanford built the first DNA microarray by spotting DNA onto a glass slide with a robot adapted from a printing press. Within five years microarrays became the bedrock of genomics. The "omics revolution" began with 1990s microarray data — and birthed entirely new statistical methods because n=20 patients × p=20,000 genes broke every textbook assumption. LASSO, originally Tibshirani's 1996 regression-shrinkage method, found its killer application here (a >50,000-cited paper; he joked the acronym "Least Absolute Shrinkage and Selection Operator" came first and the full name was back-fitted). van 't Veer's 2002 70-gene Mammaprint was the first clinical fruit of this lineage.
