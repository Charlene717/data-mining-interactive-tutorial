葉優先 vs 層優先

類比

500 個醫生 vs 接力賽

Random Forest 就像「拿同一份病例給 500 位醫生看，每位只看其中一部分症狀、給出獨立診斷，最後投票」——每個都有偏見，但平均之後極其穩定。Gradient Boosting 像「接力賽——第一棒 (淺樹) 給粗略答案，第二棒 (殘差樹) 修正第一棒漏掉的，第三棒再修第二棒漏掉的……」每棒擬合的是剩下的錯誤。RF 是 parallel democracy，GBM 是 sequential teamwork。

Random Forest is "give the same case to 500 doctors, each sees only some symptoms, each gives an independent diagnosis, then vote" — every doctor is biased, but the average is rock-solid. Gradient Boosting is "a relay race — the first runner (shallow tree) gives a rough answer, the second runner (residual tree) corrects what the first missed, the third runner corrects what the second missed…" each fits what's left wrong. RF = parallel democracy. GBM = sequential teamwork.

概覽

為什麼樹模型在表格資料上稱王？

樹模型 (decision trees) 透過遞迴二元切分讓資料逐步「純化」，天然處理非線性、特徵交互、不同單位、缺失值與類別變數，且無需 scaling。集成版本 (Random Forest、Gradient Boosting) 進一步穩定變異與抑制偏差——在 Kaggle 結構化資料賽事中，2015 之後幾乎都是 XGBoost / LightGBM / CatBoost 拿冠軍。

但單棵樹對小變動極敏感（high variance），因此實務上鮮少獨自使用；它的價值在於可解釋的規則（臨床決策、合規場景）和集成的基底單元。

Decision trees recursively split data into purer subsets — natively handling nonlinearity, feature interactions, mixed units, missing values, and categoricals, all without scaling. Ensembles (Random Forest, Gradient Boosting) tame variance and bias further — since 2015, nearly every Kaggle tabular winner has used XGBoost / LightGBM / CatBoost.

A single tree is extremely sensitive to small data changes (high variance); its real value is as an interpretable rule set (clinical decisions, compliance) and as the base learner for ensembles.

集成方法

三、集成方法

🌲

Random Forest

Bagging（bootstrap aggregating）+ 每次分裂只看隨機 √p 個特徵 → 降低樹間相關性，顯著減少變異。平行可訓練。

Bagging (bootstrap aggregating) + random √p feature subset at each split → decorrelates trees, greatly reduces variance. Embarrassingly parallel.

🚀

Gradient Boosting

每棵新樹擬合前一輪的殘差 / 負梯度，序列建構。降低偏差為主，常勝過 RF 但易過擬合。

Each new tree fits the previous round's residuals / negative gradient. Sequential. Lowers bias mostly; often beats RF but overfits more easily.

🏆

四、什麼時候用哪一個？

🌳 樹型模型選擇決策樹

Q1:
需要單一可解釋規則（臨床指引、稽核）？ → 單棵 CART (rpart / DecisionTree)
Q2:
追求穩健 baseline、容易調？ → Random Forest
Q3:
追求最高準確率且接受長一點的調參？ → XGBoost / LightGBM
Q4:
資料含大量高基數類別（地區、商品、ICD code）？ → CatBoost

Q1:
Need a single interpretable rule (clinical guideline, audit)? → Single CART (rpart / DecisionTree)
Q2:
Want a robust, easy-to-tune baseline? → Random Forest
Q3:
Need the highest accuracy and willing to tune? → XGBoost / LightGBM
Q4:
Many high-cardinality categoricals (region, SKU, ICD code)? → CatBoost
Q5:
Huge data (>10M rows)? → LightGBM (leaf-wise + Goss)
⚠️
RF vs GBM：RF 平行訓練、抗過擬合、易調 → 適合 baseline；GBM 通常更準但需要早停 (early_stopping_rounds) 與較多 hyperparam tuning。 RF vs GBM: RF is parallel, robust, easy to tune — great baseline. GBM is usually more accurate but needs early stopping and heavier tuning.

手算範例

手算：100 病人 → 3 層樹

2

親手算 Gini 與資訊增益

逐步
STEP 1
樣本：100 位病人，60 位無糖尿病 (Y=0)、40 位糖尿病 (Y=1)。根節點 Gini：1 − (0.6² + 0.4²) = 1 − (0.36 + 0.16) = 0.48。
Sample: 100 patients, 60 non-DM (Y=0), 40 DM (Y=1). Root Gini: 1 − (0.6² + 0.4²) = 1 − (0.36 + 0.16) = 0.48.
STEP 2
候選分裂：glucose ≤ 126：左：65 人 (55 非 / 10 DM)、右：35 人 (5 非 / 30 DM)。
Candidate split: glucose ≤ 126: Left 65 (55 non / 10 DM); Right 35 (5 non / 30 DM).
STEP 3
左子 Gini：1 − (55/65)² − (10/65)² = 1 − 0.716 − 0.024 = 0.260。
右子 Gini：1 − (5/35)² − (30/35)² = 1 − 0.020 − 0.735 = 0.245。
Left Gini: 1 − (55/65)² − (10/65)² = 1 − 0.716 − 0.024 = 0.260.
Right Gini: 1 − (5/35)² − (30/35)² = 1 − 0.020 − 0.735 = 0.245.
STEP 4
加權子節點 Gini：(65/100)·0.260 + (35/100)·0.245 = 0.169 + 0.0858 = 0.255。資訊增益：0.48 − 0.255 = 0.225。
Weighted child Gini: (65/100)·0.260 + (35/100)·0.245 = 0.169 + 0.0858 = 0.255. Information gain: 0.48 − 0.255 = 0.225.
STEP 5
枚舉所有候選：試其他特徵 (BMI ≤ 30 → gain 0.18，age ≤ 50 → gain 0.09)。glucose ≤ 126 勝出。
Enumerate candidates: try other features (BMI ≤ 30 → gain 0.18, age ≤ 50 → gain 0.09). glucose ≤ 126 wins.
STEP 6
對右子 (35 人) 遞迴：candidate BMI ≤ 35 → gain 0.06 → 採用。對左子也同樣遞迴。停止準則：depth=3、min_samples_leaf=5 或 gain < 0.01。
Recurse on right child (35): candidate BMI ≤ 35 → gain 0.06 → accept. Same for left. Stop when depth=3, min_samples_leaf=5, or gain < 0.01.
STEP 7
葉子預測：葉子內多數類別作為輸出；機率 = pk。
Leaf prediction: majority class is the output; probability = pk.
STEP 8
剪枝 (CCP)：計算每個內節點剪掉後的 R(T)+α·|T|；用 5-fold CV 找最佳 α。typically α=0.005–0.02。
Pruning (CCP): compute R(T)+α·|T| for each candidate cut; pick α via 5-fold CV. Typical α=0.005–0.02.
STEP 9
升級到 RF：同樣的演算法跑 500 次、每次抽 100 個 bootstrap、每個分裂只看 √p≈5 個特徵。OOB error 估計 ≈ 0.18。
Scale to RF: repeat 500× with bootstrap-100 each, √p≈5 features per split. OOB error ≈ 0.18.
STEP 10
升級到 XGBoost：η=0.05、max_depth=4、n_estimators=500 + early_stopping_rounds=30；val AUROC 從 0.82 → 0.89。
Upgrade to XGBoost: η=0.05, max_depth=4, n_estimators=500 + early_stopping_rounds=30; val AUROC 0.82 → 0.89.

爭論題

三題爭論

爭論 1

RF vs XGBoost — 怎麼選？

觀點 A：「研究 baseline 一律 RF」——平行可訓、抗過擬合、預設值就能用、有 OOB 估計。論文寫法：「RF 作為 baseline，AUROC = 0.84」。"Always RF as research baseline" — parallelizable, robust, works at defaults, OOB built-in. Paper-friendly: "RF baseline, AUROC = 0.84".
觀點 B：「競賽/生產直上 XGBoost」——序列擬合殘差能逼近更低偏差，2nd-order Taylor 更精確。代價：要早停、要調 η + max_depth + subsample + colsample。"XGBoost in competitions and production" — sequential residual fit lowers bias; 2nd-order Taylor is more precise. Cost: must early-stop, tune η + max_depth + subsample + colsample.
觀點 C：「跑兩個對比」——Grinsztajn 2022 NeurIPS 發現 XGBoost 平均勝 RF 1–2% AUROC，但在高雜訊資料 RF 反勝。對你的資料兩個都跑一次。"Run both and compare" — Grinsztajn 2022 NeurIPS finds XGBoost beats RF by 1–2% on average, but RF wins on noisy data. Just train both on your data.
爭論 2

樹模型可解釋還是包裝？

挺方：單棵 CART (depth ≤ 4) 就是 if-else 規則——臨床指南可以直接抄。Random Forest 的 feature importance 給出 top-K 排序，方向明確。A single CART (depth ≤ 4) is literally if-else rules — clinical guidelines copy them directly. RF feature importance gives top-K ranking with direction.
反方：600 棵深度 8 的 XGBoost 等於 600 × 256 = 153,600 條規則，沒人能讀。所謂「特徵重要性」其實隨 seed 變動 ±15%；SHAP 也只是 local 解釋，無法陳述全域因果。600 trees × depth 8 = 153,600 rules — no human reads that. Feature importance varies ±15% across seeds; SHAP is local, gives no global causal story.
折衷：用 RuleFit (Friedman 2008) 或 EBM (Microsoft 2019, InterpretML) — 強制模型保持規則形式且可加性。準確率代價 ~2–4% AUROC，但能擺進監管報告。Use RuleFit (Friedman 2008) or EBM (Microsoft 2019, InterpretML) — keep the model rule-shaped and additive. Cost ~2–4% AUROC, but fits in regulator reports.
爭論 3

CatBoost 真的贏嗎？

挺 CatBoost：Ordered Target Statistics + oblivious trees 在高基數類別資料 (郵遞區號、ICD、SKU) 上明顯勝出。推論飛快 (~6× XGBoost on CPU)。Ordered Target Statistics + oblivious trees win on high-cardinality categoricals (zip code, ICD, SKU). Inference 6× faster than XGBoost on CPU.
挺 XGBoost：XGBoost 2.x 加了原生 categorical，差距縮小到 ~1% AUROC。社群 + 文件 + GPU 成熟度 + 與 SHAP/Optuna 的整合，仍是工業界主流。XGBoost 2.x added native categorical; gap shrank to ~1% AUROC. Community + docs + GPU maturity + SHAP/Optuna integration still make it the industry default.
結論：如果資料有 >5 個 cardinality > 100 的類別欄、且樣本 > 10k，CatBoost 值得試。其他情境兩個差距很小，跟團隊熟悉度比較有關。If you have >5 categorical columns with cardinality > 100 and > 10k samples, CatBoost is worth trying. Otherwise the gap is small — pick whatever the team knows best.

陷阱

樹模型 6 大陷阱

把 importance 當因果

「年齡 importance 最高」不代表「年齡導致這個結果」——可能只是和真正原因高度相關 (proxy)。因果推論需要 DAG + 反事實 (Pearl 2009)。

"Age is most important" doesn't mean "age causes the outcome" — it may just correlate with the true cause (proxy). Causal claims need DAGs + counterfactuals (Pearl 2009).

忽略 train/val gap

XGBoost train AUROC = 0.99 看起來很美——但 val AUROC = 0.78 就是嚴重過擬合。必須報告兩者，及差距。

XGBoost train AUROC = 0.99 looks pretty — but val AUROC = 0.78 is severe overfit. Always report both and the gap.

把 XGBoost 當黑箱

沒做 SHAP、沒做 partial dependence 就上線——出問題時無法 debug。至少跑 SHAP summary + 5 個 SHAP dependence 圖。

Deploying without SHAP / partial dependence → impossible to debug when it fails. Always run SHAP summary + 5 dependence plots.

預設超參數直接上線

XGBoost 預設 learning_rate=0.3 對大多數任務太大；LightGBM 預設 num_leaves=31 對小資料太多。至少 Optuna 跑 50 trials。

XGBoost default learning_rate=0.3 is too big for most tasks; LightGBM default num_leaves=31 is too many for small data. At least 50 Optuna trials.

train/test 編碼不一致

用 LabelEncoder() 各自 fit_transform train 和 test → 同一字串可能編到不同數字。fit 在 train、只 transform test，或用 OrdinalEncoder(handle_unknown="use_encoded_value")。

fit_transform LabelEncoder() separately on train/test → same string maps to different ints. Fit on train, only transform test, or use OrdinalEncoder(handle_unknown="use_encoded_value").

不平衡資料不設 weight

陽性 5%：XGBoost 預設會學「全猜陰性」。設 scale_pos_weight = neg/pos = 19 或 LightGBM is_unbalance=True。

5% positive: XGBoost defaults to "always predict negative." Set scale_pos_weight = neg/pos = 19 or LightGBM is_unbalance=True.

xgb.XGBClassifier().fit(X_train, y_train); print(model.score(X_train, y_train)) — 預設超參、無 early stopping、用 train acc 報告。對 1:50 不平衡資料還能輸出 0.98——但 0.98 是 train acc，val PR-AUC 可能 0.18。

xgb.XGBClassifier().fit(X_train, y_train); print(model.score(X_train, y_train)) — defaults, no early stopping, reports train accuracy. On 1:50 imbalanced data it shows 0.98 — that's train acc; val PR-AUC may be 0.18.

進階研究員彩蛋

2024–2026 SOTA

XGBoost 2.x device-based API：10 週年大改版，device="cuda" / "mps" / "cpu" 取代舊的 tree_method="gpu_hist"。原生 categorical (no more LabelEncoder!) 、external memory、QuantileDMatrix 在 100M+ 樣本上比 v1 快 3×。

XGBoost 2.x device-based API: 10th-anniversary redesign — device="cuda" / "mps" / "cpu" replaces the old tree_method="gpu_hist". Native categorical (no more LabelEncoder!), external memory, QuantileDMatrix, 3× faster than v1 on 100M+ samples.

🔍 GPUTreeShap

原本 TreeSHAP 是 O(LD²) CPU bound；GPUTreeShap (NVIDIA Mitchell et al., arXiv:2010.13972, 2021) 用 path-based GPU kernel 把 600 棵深度 8 樹的 SHAP 從 4 小時降到 12 秒。整合到 XGBoost 2.x 的 model.predict(X, pred_contribs=True)。

2024 NGBoost-SHAP (Duan et al., NeurIPS 2024) 進一步把 SHAP 推廣到「機率分布輸出」——可以解釋每個特徵對 mean / variance 各自的貢獻。

Vanilla TreeSHAP is O(LD²) CPU-bound; GPUTreeShap (NVIDIA Mitchell et al., arXiv:2010.13972, 2021) uses a path-based GPU kernel to drop SHAP on 600 depth-8 trees from 4 h to 12 s. Integrated in XGBoost 2.x: model.predict(X, pred_contribs=True).

2024 NGBoost-SHAP (Duan et al., NeurIPS 2024) extends SHAP to "distributional outputs" — separating each feature's contribution to mean vs variance.

🔍 NGBoost (Duan et al, ICML 2020)

NGBoost (Duan et al., ICML 2020, arXiv:1910.03225) 改用 Natural Gradient Boosting 同時擬合分布的 mean 與 scale 參數——不再只輸出點估，而是完整 P(y|x) 分布。例：給病人預測 5 年存活率時，能說「中位 0.65，IQR [0.42, 0.88]」。

2025 NGBoost 0.5 加入 Multi-output 與 GPU 支援，比 XGBoost-quantile 更穩定。

NGBoost (Duan et al., ICML 2020, arXiv:1910.03225) uses Natural Gradient Boosting to fit both location and scale parameters of an output distribution — point estimates become full P(y|x). E.g. "median 5-yr survival 0.65, IQR [0.42, 0.88]".

NGBoost 0.5 (2025) adds multi-output and GPU support, more stable than XGBoost-quantile.

🔍 Why trees still beat Transformers on tabular

Grinsztajn-Oyallon-Varoquaux NeurIPS 2022 (arXiv:2207.08815) 系統 benchmark 45 個表格資料集 vs Transformer (TabTransformer, FT-Transformer)、TabNet、MLP-PLR、XGBoost、RF。結論：樹型模型在 70% 的資料集上仍勝出，主因有三：(1) 樹自動處理非旋轉不變特徵；(2) 樹自動處理「不重要特徵」（非零權重會傷 NN）；(3) NN 在小資料上難收斂。

這篇是 2024–2026 Kaggle 入門：「沒事就用 XGBoost / LightGBM」的官方依據。

Grinsztajn-Oyallon-Varoquaux NeurIPS 2022 (arXiv:2207.08815) systematically benchmarked 45 tabular datasets vs Transformer (TabTransformer, FT-Transformer), TabNet, MLP-PLR, XGBoost, RF. Verdict: trees win on 70% of datasets, because (1) trees handle non-rotation-invariant features natively; (2) trees ignore unimportant features (NNs can't suppress them); (3) NNs underperform on small N.

This is the official 2024–2026 reason: "for tabular, just use XGBoost / LightGBM."
