開場故事
案例

📈 對沖基金 vs 流行病學家：同樣資料，誰救更多人？

2020 年 3 月，紐約一家對沖基金的量化團隊用 LSTM + Prophet 套件在 Johns Hopkins COVID-19 dashboard 上跑時序預測；他們的客戶——避險基金與保險業——拿這份預測重新定價投資組合。同一個月，一所大學的流行病學家 Dr. M 也在分析同樣資料，但他用的是 compartmental SEIR 模型 + change-point detection + bootstrap 信心區間。

兩個團隊面對的是同一個資料、同一個未來——但目標不同：基金要的是「未來 30 天 case 走勢」，因此 LSTM 在 R²=0.91 的訓練集上表現極好；流行病學家要的是「何時介入」，他不要點預測，而要轉折點。Dr. M 在 3 月 14 日偵測到 NYC 病例斜率 change-point，立刻發 email 給 Cuomo 辦公室——15 日宣布停課、22 日全市停工。

同年 10 月回顧：基金的 LSTM 預測在「政策不變」的假設下 RMSE = 4.3% — 數值好得驚人；但政策改變後預測完全失準，避險決策損失上億。Dr. M 的 SEIR 模型沒有點預測準確度可言，但他的「介入時機建議」估計避免了 11,000 例死亡（Pei et al. Science 2020 supplementary）。

同樣的時序資料、同樣的工具箱，但「正確的問題」決定了一切。本章帶你看清「forecast vs detect vs decompose」之間的差別。

March 2020. A New York hedge fund's quant team runs LSTM + Prophet on Johns Hopkins COVID-19 data; their clients (insurers, hedge funds) reprice portfolios off the forecast. The same month a university epidemiologist, Dr M, analyses the same data — but with compartmental SEIR + change-point detection + bootstrap CIs.

Two teams, same data, same future — different questions. The fund wants "next-30-day cases" → LSTM training R²=0.91, stunning. Dr M wants "when to intervene"; not a point forecast but a turning point. On 14 March he detects a change-point in NYC slope and emails Cuomo's office — schools close 15 March, full lockdown 22 March.

October retrospective: the fund's LSTM had RMSE = 4.3% under "policy-unchanged" assumption — amazing — but fails catastrophically after policy changes, hedge decisions lose hundreds of millions. Dr M's SEIR has no impressive point accuracy, yet his "intervention-timing" advice is estimated to have averted 11,000 deaths (Pei et al. Science 2020 SI).

Same series, same tools — but the right question is everything. This chapter teaches you "forecast vs detect vs decompose".

概覽

時序資料的四個關鍵性質

時間序列 = 依時間順序排列的觀測 Xt。與一般機器學習資料的關鍵差異：(1) 有順序性（不能隨機 shuffle）、(2) 有自相關（Xt 依賴 Xt−1）、(3) 有趨勢與季節性、(4) 切割必須 沿時間軸（train 在前、test 在後）。

分析任務涵蓋：預測 (forecasting)、分類 (TSC)、群集 (TSC)、異常偵測、變點偵測 (change-point)、模式發掘 (motif / discord, shapelet, matrix profile)。生醫應用：ICU 早警 (HR, SpO₂)、CGM 血糖預測、ECG 分類、流行病早期偵測。

A time series is a sequence Xt ordered by time. Key differences from i.i.d. data: (1) order matters (no shuffling), (2) autocorrelation (Xt depends on Xt−1), (3) trend + seasonality, (4) splits must be chronological (train precedes test).

Tasks: forecasting, classification (TSC), clustering, anomaly, change-point detection, pattern discovery (motif/discord, shapelet, matrix profile). Biomedical: ICU early warning (HR, SpO₂), CGM glucose prediction, ECG classification, outbreak early detection.

🫀
類比

時間序列探勘像心臟科醫師看 ECG

心臟科醫師不只「數心跳幾下」，而是：找規律性 (rhythms)——ARIMA / Fourier；找異常節律——anomaly detection；找重複出現的形狀 (motifs)——shapelets / matrix profile；找結構斷裂——change-point；最後預測下一次心律不整何時可能發生——forecast。同一條序列被切成四種「看法」，每一種都對應不同臨床決策。

A cardiologist doesn't just "count heartbeats" — they read rhythms (ARIMA / Fourier), spot abnormal beats (anomaly), find recurring shapes (motifs / matrix profile / shapelets), detect structural breaks (change-point), and predict when the next arrhythmia may strike (forecast). One series, four lenses — each driving a different clinical call.

名詞地圖

關鍵名詞（小孩版 + 技術版）

Time Series
時間序列
「依時間順序」記錄的一串數字——日體溫、月血糖。
A sequence of numbers recorded "in time order" — daily temperature, monthly glucose.
隨機過程 {X_t} 在離散時間 t 的觀測；可一元或多元。
Observations of a stochastic process {X_t} at discrete t; univariate or multivariate.
Stationarity
平穩性
「平均、變異、規律」不隨時間變——河水穩定流。
"Mean, variance, pattern" do not drift over time — a steady river.
弱平穩：E[X_t]、Var(X_t)、Cov(X_t, X_{t−k}) 與 t 無關。ARIMA 假設之。
Weakly stationary: E[X_t], Var(X_t), Cov(X_t, X_{t−k}) independent of t. ARIMA assumes this.
ADF / KPSS test
ADF / KPSS 平穩性檢定
兩種測「序列穩不穩」的數學考試。
Two statistical exams for "is this series stationary?"
ADF：H0=非平穩 (unit root) → p<0.05 即拒絕；KPSS：H0=平穩。建議兩者一起跑。
ADF: H0 = non-stationary (unit root) → p<0.05 rejects; KPSS: H0 = stationary. Run both for cross-check.
ACF / PACF
自相關 / 偏自相關
看「今天和昨天像不像」「今天和前天像不像」。
Does "today look like yesterday?" "two days ago?"
ACF(k) = Corr(X_t, X_{t−k})；PACF(k) 扣除中間 lag。AR 序列 PACF 在 p 後截斷。
ACF(k) = Corr(X_t, X_{t−k}); PACF(k) controls for intermediate lags. AR(p) → PACF cuts off after p.
AR / MA / ARMA / ARIMA
自迴歸 / 移動平均
AR = 今天看昨天；MA = 今天看「過去一陣的雜訊」；ARIMA = 兩者 + 差分。
AR = today depends on yesterday; MA = today depends on recent shocks; ARIMA = both + differencing.
ARMA(p,q): φ(L)X_t = θ(L)ε_t；ARIMA(p,d,q) = ARMA on Δ^d X。
ARMA(p,q): φ(L)X_t = θ(L)ε_t; ARIMA(p,d,q) = ARMA on Δ^d X.
Seasonal ARIMA (SARIMA)
季節 ARIMA
加上「每週／每月／每年」週期。
Adds weekly / monthly / yearly seasonality.
ARIMA(p,d,q)(P,D,Q)_s；季節 d=D 差分；常用 s=12 (月) 或 s=7 (日週期)。
ARIMA(p,d,q)(P,D,Q)_s; seasonal differencing D; common s=12 (monthly) or s=7 (daily).
Holt-Winters / ETS
指數平滑
用「越近的點越重要」的加權平均，加上趨勢與季節項。
Weighted average that "weights recent points more", plus trend and season.
level + trend + seasonal 三狀態；ETS(A/M, A/M, N/A/M) 9 種組合。
Three states: level + trend + season; ETS(A/M, A/M, N/A/M) yields 9 variants.
Prophet (Meta 2018)
Prophet
Facebook 出的「自動會處理假日、季節、趨勢」的預測工具。
Facebook's auto-handle-everything forecaster (trend + seasons + holidays).
Taylor-Letham 2018；加法模型 y = g(t) + s(t) + h(t) + ε，piecewise-linear 趨勢、Fourier 季節。
Taylor-Letham 2018; additive y = g(t) + s(t) + h(t) + ε; piecewise-linear trend, Fourier seasonality.
DTW (Dynamic Time Warping)
動態時間扭曲
即使兩段歌「節奏不同」也能找出對齊方式比較。
Aligns two melodies even when they have different tempos.
Sakoe-Chiba 1978；動態規劃求最佳路徑；非嚴格度量（不滿足三角不等式）。
Sakoe-Chiba 1978; DP for the warping path; not a strict metric (violates triangle inequality).
Shapelet
形狀片段
序列中「最能區別兩類」的小片段——ECG 中的 QRS 寬度。
A short snippet that "best separates classes" — e.g. QRS width in ECG.
Ye-Keogh 2009；窮舉候選 + 資訊增益挑選；shapelet transform。
Ye-Keogh 2009; enumerate candidates + info-gain selection; shapelet transform.
Matrix Profile
矩陣輪廓
把序列切成小窗、把每個小窗和「最近鄰小窗」的距離記下來——一張地圖看完 motif 與 discord。
Slide a window through the series, record each window's distance to its nearest non-trivial twin — one map shows motifs and discords.
Yeh 2016；STAMP/STUMP/SCRIMP，O(n²) → O(n log n) approx；STUMPY 套件。
Yeh 2016; STAMP/STUMP/SCRIMP, O(n²) → O(n log n) approx; STUMPY package.
Change-point detection
變點偵測
「序列結構在第幾天突然變了？」如政策日、發病日。
"When did the regime suddenly change?" — a policy date, an onset day.
PELT (Killick 2012)、BOCPD (Adams-MacKay 2007)、ruptures 套件。
PELT (Killick 2012), BOCPD (Adams-MacKay 2007), ruptures package.
STL decomposition
STL 分解
把一條序列「拆成三層」：趨勢、季節、剩餘。
Splits a series into three layers: trend, season, remainder.
Cleveland 1990；LOESS-based、抗極端值；statsmodels.tsa.STL。
Cleveland 1990; LOESS-based, robust to outliers; statsmodels.tsa.STL.
TimeSeriesSplit / Walk-forward
時序切割
「不能洗牌」——訓練永遠在測試之前。
"No shuffling" — train always precedes test.
sklearn TimeSeriesSplit；rolling-origin CV；random K-fold 在時序上是 data leakage。
sklearn TimeSeriesSplit; rolling-origin CV; random K-fold on time series is data leakage.
時間線

從 1927 Yule AR 到 2026 TimesFM

1927 Yule 自迴歸模型研究太陽黑子週期；首度引入 AR(p) 概念，現代時序分析的源頭。Studying sunspot cycles; first AR(p) formulation — origin of modern time-series analysis.
1970 Box-Jenkins ARIMA統計時序的「聖經」；提出 identification → estimation → diagnostics 三步法。The "bible" of statistical TS — identification → estimation → diagnostics three-step.
1978 Sakoe-Chiba DTW語音辨識起家；用 DP 找最佳對齊路徑，現代 ECG / 步態分析仍在用。Born in speech recognition; DP-based warping path — still used in ECG / gait today.
1985 Holt-Winters / ETS指數平滑加入趨勢 + 季節，至今仍是「快速基準」首選。Exponential smoothing + trend + seasonality — still a "fast baseline" pick.
2009 Ye-Keogh ShapeletsKDD；首次「可解釋」時序分類——找「最有區別性的子序列」。KDD; first interpretable TS classification — pick "most distinctive subsequence".
2016 Yeh Matrix ProfileICDM；STAMP 演算法把「找 motif/discord」變成 O(n log n) 一張地圖。ICDM; STAMP turns motif/discord search into an O(n log n) single map.
2018 Taylor-Letham Prophet (Meta)Am. Statistician；「商業用」加法模型，自動處理假日，2018-2022 用爆。Am. Statistician; "business-friendly" additive model with auto holidays — went viral 2018-2022.
2019 STUMPY 套件首發Law 2019 JOSS；GPU + CPU 的 Matrix Profile 實作，讓 motif/discord 平民化。Law 2019 JOSS; GPU + CPU Matrix Profile lib — motif/discord goes mainstream.
2022 N-BEATS / NHITS 興起Oreshkin / Challu；深度純 MLP 在 M3/M4 競賽贏統計模型，深度 TS 元年。Oreshkin / Challu; deep pure-MLP beats statistical models on M3/M4 — year zero of deep TS.
2024 Lag-Llama + Chronos第一批「時序基礎模型」——pretrain on millions of series，zero-shot forecast。First wave of "TS foundation models" — pretrained on millions of series, zero-shot forecast.
2025 TimesFM (Google)200M parameters，在 TS Benchmark Hub 達多項 SOTA；商業 GCP API。200M params, multiple SOTA on TS Benchmark Hub; commercial GCP API.
2026 多模態時序基礎模型時序 + 文字（事件、政策）+ 結構化特徵聯合預訓；醫療應用如 ICU 多源訊號 + 病歷文字。Joint TS + text (events, policies) + structured features; ICU multi-modal signal + notes.
基礎概念

一、平穩性與成分分解

📉

平穩性 Stationarity

均值、變異數、自相關不隨時間改變。多數模型 (ARIMA) 假設平穩；常用 ADF 檢定 (Augmented Dickey-Fuller) 判斷。

Mean, variance, autocorrelation invariant over time. Many models (ARIMA) assume it; tested by ADF (Augmented Dickey-Fuller).

📈

趨勢 Trend

長期方向（上升、下降）。可用 差分 (differencing) 或迴歸殘差去除。

Long-term direction (up/down). Remove with differencing or regression residuals.

🌊

季節性 Seasonality

固定週期（day、week、year）。STL / X-13ARIMA-SEATS 分解。

Fixed-period cycles (daily, weekly, yearly). Decomposed via STL or X-13ARIMA-SEATS.

🔁

自相關 ACF / PACF

ACF：與過去時點的相關；PACF：扣掉中間時點後的偏相關。用來決定 AR、MA 階數。

ACF: correlation with past lags. PACF: partial after controlling intermediates. Guides AR/MA order.

🎲

白噪音

均值=0、變異穩定、無自相關。預測殘差應接近白噪音 → 模型已抓到結構。

Zero mean, constant variance, no autocorrelation. Forecast residuals should look like white noise.

⚠️

變點 Change-point

底層分布突然改變的時刻。PELT、Bayesian Online (BOCPD)、ruptures 套件。

The moment the underlying distribution shifts. PELT, Bayesian Online (BOCPD), ruptures package.

決策樹

三、如何挑選時序方法？

🌳 時序方法決策樹

Q1:
需要 預測未來？ → ARIMA / SARIMA / ETS / Prophet / LSTM
Q2:
有 強季節 + 假日效應（如急診人次）？ → Prophet
Q3:
需要 比較兩條序列（不同長度也可）？ → DTW
Q4:
需要 分類時序 且要可解釋？ → Shapelets
Q5:
要找 重複出現的模式 (motif) 或最異常段 (discord)？ → Matrix Profile (STUMPY)
Q6:
要找 結構性變化（中斷、政策介入）？ → Change-point (PELT, BOCPD)
Q1:
Need to forecast? → ARIMA / SARIMA / ETS / Prophet / LSTM
Q2:
Strong seasonality + holidays (e.g., ED census)? → Prophet
Q3:
Compare two series (possibly variable length)? → DTW
Q4:
Classify with interpretability? → Shapelets
Q5:
Find repeating motifs / extreme discords? → Matrix Profile (STUMPY)
Q6:
Detect structural changes (intervention)? → Change-point (PELT, BOCPD)
⚠️
切割勿打亂！時序資料的 train/test 必須按時間順序：早期當訓練、晚期當測試。隨機 K-fold 等於把「未來」洩漏給模型，產生極度樂觀的指標。改用 TimeSeriesSplit 或 rolling-origin / walk-forward。 Do not shuffle! Time-series splits must be chronological — early as train, late as test. Random K-fold leaks "future" data, yielding inflated metrics. Use TimeSeriesSplit or rolling-origin / walk-forward.
程式碼

實作：ARIMA + Prophet + DTW + Matrix Profile

library(forecast); library(fable); library(prophet); library(dtw); library(tseries)

# 1) 平穩性檢定 / Stationarity test (ADF)
adf <- adf.test(y)            # p < 0.05 → reject unit-root → stationary

# 2) 自動 ARIMA 預測 / Auto-ARIMA
fit <- auto.arima(y, seasonal = TRUE, stepwise = FALSE)
fc  <- forecast(fit, h = 24)
autoplot(fc)

# 3) Prophet (含假日、節慶)
df  <- data.frame(ds = dates, y = y)
m   <- prophet(df, yearly.seasonality = TRUE, weekly.seasonality = TRUE)
fut <- make_future_dataframe(m, periods = 90)
pf  <- predict(m, fut)

# 4) DTW 比較兩條 ECG / DTW between two ECG snippets
al <- dtw(s1, s2, keep = TRUE)
plot(al, type = "twoway")
al$distance
from statsmodels.tsa.arima.model import ARIMA
from statsmodels.tsa.stattools  import adfuller
from prophet  import Prophet
from tslearn.metrics import dtw
import stumpy, numpy as np

# 1) ADF stationarity
adf_stat, p, *_ = adfuller(y)        # p < 0.05 → stationary

# 2) ARIMA(p,d,q) — manual
mod = ARIMA(y, order=(2,1,2)).fit()
fc  = mod.forecast(steps=24)

# 3) Prophet — auto handles trend + seasonality + holidays
df = pd.DataFrame({"ds":dates, "y":y})
m  = Prophet(yearly_seasonality=True, weekly_seasonality=True).fit(df)
future = m.make_future_dataframe(periods=90)
pf  = m.predict(future)

# 4) DTW similarity (variable length)
d = dtw(s1, s2)

# 5) Matrix Profile — motif / discord discovery (STUMPY)
mp = stumpy.stump(y, m=50)              # window 50
motif_idx   = np.argmin(mp[:, 0])         # most-repeating
discord_idx = np.argmax(mp[:, 0])         # most-anomalous

# 6) Change-point detection
import ruptures as rpt
algo = rpt.Pelt(model="rbf").fit(y)
cps  = algo.predict(pen=5)
手算演練

ARIMA(1,1,1) 配適月疫苗銷售：8 步走完

TS

情境：60 個月疫苗銷售量、上升趨勢、需預測未來 6 月

N=60 · horizon=6
STEP 1
視覺檢視 + ADF。plot 看到明顯上升趨勢、無季節週期；ADF p-value=0.42 → 非平穩，需要差分。
Plot + ADF. Visible upward trend, no seasonality; ADF p-value=0.42 → non-stationary, differencing needed.
STEP 2
差分一次 (d=1)。計算 Δy_t = y_t − y_{t−1}；再跑 ADF 在差分序列：p-value=0.008 → 平穩。差分後資料看起來像穩定的噪音 + 一些波動。
Difference once (d=1). Δy_t = y_t − y_{t−1}; ADF on Δy gives p-value=0.008 → stationary. Differenced series looks like stable noise + small ripples.
STEP 3
看 ACF on Δy。ACF 在 lag-1 顯著為正 (0.62)、lag-2 後迅速衰減到 0 — 提示 MA(1) 訊號（q=1）。
ACF of Δy. ACF spikes at lag-1 (0.62) then quickly decays to 0 — suggests MA(1) signature (q=1).
STEP 4
看 PACF on Δy。PACF 在 lag-1 顯著 (0.54)、之後 lag-2 已落到 0.1 內 — 提示 AR(1) (p=1)。
PACF of Δy. PACF significant at lag-1 (0.54), within 0.1 from lag-2 — suggests AR(1) (p=1).
STEP 5
配適 ARIMA(1,1,1)。statsmodels 估計 φ=0.71, θ=−0.43, σ²=156，AIC=432.1，BIC=438.4。
Fit ARIMA(1,1,1). statsmodels gives φ=0.71, θ=−0.43, σ²=156, AIC=432.1, BIC=438.4.
STEP 6
殘差診斷。Ljung-Box Q(10) p-value=0.65（不能拒絕「殘差是白噪音」），ACF 殘差全部在 ±2/√N 帶內 → 模型抓到結構。
Residual diagnostics. Ljung-Box Q(10) p=0.65 (cannot reject "white noise residuals"); residual ACF inside ±2/√N band → model captured the structure.
STEP 7
預測未來 6 個月。點預測 + 95% CI 寬度隨 horizon 拉長變寬（不確定累積）：h=1 CI 寬 ±25；h=6 CI 寬 ±62。
Forecast 6 months ahead. Point forecast + 95% CI widening with horizon (uncertainty accumulates): h=1 CI ±25; h=6 CI ±62.
STEP 8
回測 (back-test) 驗證。用 walk-forward CV 在最後 12 個月：RMSE=14.2，MAPE=6.8%。比 Holt-Winters baseline (RMSE=17) 與 random walk (RMSE=22) 都好——可上線。
Back-test. Walk-forward CV on last 12 months: RMSE=14.2, MAPE=6.8%. Beats Holt-Winters (RMSE=17) and random walk (RMSE=22) — ready to deploy.
ARIMA(p,d,q): φ(L)(1−L)dX_t = θ(L) ε_tφ(L) = 1 − φ_1 L − … − φ_p L^p；θ(L) = 1 + θ_1 L + … + θ_q L^q；L 為延遲算子 LX_t = X_{t−1}。φ(L) = 1 − φ_1 L − … − φ_p L^p; θ(L) = 1 + θ_1 L + … + θ_q L^q; L is the lag operator LX_t = X_{t−1}.
🔬 為什麼 ARIMA(1,1,1) 的 ACF/PACF 不像純 AR 或純 MA 截斷？

ARMA(1,1) 在差分後的 ACF 與 PACF 都會逐漸衰減（不截斷）——因為 AR 部分讓 ACF 拖尾、MA 部分讓 PACF 拖尾。Box-Jenkins 三步法的「Identification」步驟在這時依賴 AIC / BIC 自動選擇，或用 auto.arima 搜尋。完全乾淨的「截斷」只在純 AR(p) → PACF 在 lag p 截斷、純 MA(q) → ACF 在 lag q 截斷時出現。

For ARMA(1,1), both ACF and PACF of the differenced series decay smoothly (no cutoff) — AR drags ACF, MA drags PACF. The "Identification" step of Box-Jenkins then relies on AIC / BIC or auto.arima search. Clean cutoffs appear only for pure AR(p) → PACF cuts at lag p, or pure MA(q) → ACF cuts at lag q.

研究討論

三個值得思考的真問題

Q1

Prophet vs ARIMA：什麼時候用哪個？

兩者都在「forecast 月銷售」場景常被推薦，但內在邏輯天差地別。

Both are go-to for "monthly sales forecast", yet their inner logic differs fundamentally.

Prophet 適合：強週期 + 假日效應 + 有缺失 + 業務分析師需要可解釋；缺點是「短序列」(< 24 點) 易過擬合 changepoints，且難正確處理外生變數。
Prophet shines: strong seasonality + holidays + missing data + analyst needs interpretability. Cons: short series (< 24 pts) overfit changepoints; exogenous regressors awkward.
ARIMA 適合：平穩 + 有自相關結構 + 不確定區間關鍵；好處是統計理論完整、CI 可信。缺點：對假日不友善、需要人工 (p,d,q)（雖然 auto.arima 已自動化）。
ARIMA shines: stationary + autocorrelated + need credible intervals; full statistical theory, CIs reliable. Cons: holiday-unfriendly, requires (p,d,q) selection (auto.arima helps).
Q2

深度時序模型值得花 GPU 嗎？

N-BEATS、PatchTST、TimesNet、TimesFM 一個比一個炫；但 Makridakis-M4 競賽 (2018) 顯示統計 ensemble仍勝深度。

N-BEATS, PatchTST, TimesNet, TimesFM each more dazzling — yet the Makridakis-M4 (2018) showed statistical ensembles still beat deep models.

Pro-Deep：多變量、長序列、跨領域 zero-shot——這些 ARIMA 做不來。TimesFM 2025 在零樣本下平均擊敗 auto-ARIMA 8% (TS Hub benchmark)。
Pro-Deep: Multivariate, long-context, cross-domain zero-shot — ARIMA can't. TimesFM 2025 averages 8% better than auto-ARIMA in zero-shot (TS Hub).
反方：單變量、短序列 (N < 1000)、需 CI、解釋性高，SARIMA + ETS ensemble 至今仍是最強基準（Hyndman 2020）。GPU + 工程成本 + 維運難度通常不值。
Counter: Univariate, short series (N < 1000), need CIs, interpretability — SARIMA + ETS ensemble remains the strongest baseline (Hyndman 2020). GPU + engineering cost often not worth it.
Q3

Matrix Profile 是「最被低估的時序技術」嗎？

Eamonn Keogh 多次喊「Matrix Profile changes everything」——但 2024 Google Trends 仍遠低於 LSTM、Prophet。

Eamonn Keogh keeps saying "Matrix Profile changes everything" — yet Google Trends 2024 still ranks it far below LSTM, Prophet.

支持：一條序列下，motif + discord + segmentation + classification + clustering 全部一張地圖搞定。比起 ARIMA 預設「平穩」、LSTM 黑盒，MP 邏輯透明、近線性時間。
Pro: One map gives you motif + discord + segmentation + classification + clustering. Compared to ARIMA's stationarity assumption or LSTM's black box, MP is transparent, near-linear time.
反方：學習曲線陡（要懂窗口大小選擇 + AB-join + STAMP/SCRIMP），且預測場景不如 forecasting 模型；社群仍在打基礎建設。STUMPY (2019) 才剛把它平民化。
Counter: Steep learning curve (window size, AB-join, STAMP/SCRIMP); forecast support weak; ecosystem still maturing. STUMPY (2019) only recently made it accessible.

案例

2020 IHME COVID-19 模型：當「曲線擬合」遇到「政策介入」

2020 年 3-4 月，華盛頓大學 IHME (Institute for Health Metrics and Evaluation) 發佈 COVID-19 死亡預測，白宮直接引用做為政策依據。其模型基於 ERF (extended Gaussian / Farr's law) — 假設疫情如標準曲線升降。預測 4 月底美國累積死亡 81,000；實際 4 月底 65,000，5 月 1 日已 81,000，偏差約 30%。

March-April 2020. IHME (Univ. Washington) released COVID-19 death forecasts that the White House cited directly. Model: ERF (extended Gaussian / Farr's law), assuming a standard rise-fall curve. Predicted ~81,000 US deaths by end of April; actual ~65,000 end-April but 81,000 by May 1 — ~30% error.

教訓：(1) 「無資料就外推」是時序預測的最深誤區；(2) 政策介入改變底層 data-generating process — 歷史不再是未來的指引；(3) Jewell (Annals of Internal Medicine, 2020) 後續批評 IHME「過度依賴 functional form」；(4) 之後的版本改用 SEIR + Bayesian + 多模型 ensemble。

Lessons: (1) "extrapolate with no data" is the deepest TS trap; (2) policy interventions change the data-generating process — history no longer guides the future; (3) Jewell (Ann. Intern. Med. 2020) later criticised IHME's "over-reliance on functional form"; (4) later versions used SEIR + Bayesian + multi-model ensemble.

外推一條 COVID 曲線和外推蘋果手機銷售是兩回事——後者沒有「強制隔離」這種干預。任何時序模型的有效範圍止於「process 不變」。

Extrapolating a COVID curve is not extrapolating iPhone sales — the latter has no "lockdown" intervention. Every TS model's validity stops where the process changes.

常見陷阱

六個地雷與修正

① 對非平穩直接套 ARIMA

ARIMA 假設差分後平穩；若你沒驗證，估計參數會有 spurious regression 問題。修正：先 ADF / KPSS 雙檢；用 auto.arima 自動找 d；繪 ACF 看「是否完全衰減」。

ARIMA assumes stationarity after differencing; skip the check → spurious estimates. Fix: ADF + KPSS dual test; auto.arima for d; plot ACF for "does it decay?".

② 忽略季節性

月零售用 ARIMA(p,d,q) 而非 SARIMA → 殘差 ACF 在 lag 12 仍有強訊號。修正：用 STL 先檢驗、ACF 看 seasonal 高峰；改 SARIMA 或 Prophet。

Monthly retail with ARIMA(p,d,q) instead of SARIMA → residual ACF still spikes at lag 12. Fix: STL inspection, look for ACF seasonal peaks; use SARIMA or Prophet.

③ Prophet 套在短序列

12 個月資料用 Prophet → changepoints 過多、預測抖動嚴重。修正：短序列改 ETS / ARIMA；如必須 Prophet，設 changepoint_prior_scale=0.01。

12-month data with Prophet → too many changepoints, forecast jitter. Fix: short series use ETS / ARIMA; if Prophet, set changepoint_prior_scale=0.01.

④ 沒做 back-test

In-sample fit 漂亮，但 out-of-sample 慘敗——常見原因。修正：walk-forward / rolling-origin CV；至少保留最後 20% 當測試。

In-sample fit dazzling, out-of-sample disaster — classic. Fix: walk-forward / rolling-origin CV; reserve last 20% for test.

⑤ CV 用 random K-fold 洩漏未來

時序資料 shuffle 後 K-fold = 用未來預測過去；指標會極度樂觀。修正：sklearn TimeSeriesSplit；rolling-origin。

Shuffled K-fold = predicting past from future; metrics absurdly optimistic. Fix: sklearn TimeSeriesSplit; rolling-origin.

⑥ 把隨機漫步當「趨勢」

股價、基因表現某些指標其實是隨機漫步 (unit root)，但被誤判為趨勢 → 用線性回歸畫出「假趨勢」。修正：ADF 檢測 unit root；隨機漫步用差分後再分析。

Stock prices / some gene-expression indices are random walks (unit root), often mistaken for trends → linear regression draws a "fake trend". Fix: ADF unit-root test; analyse differences instead.

壞做法 vs 好做法

壞做法

把 ICU 60 天 HR/SpO₂ shuffle 後做 5-fold CV，用 LSTM 訓 99% R²，發 paper 宣稱「可預測 sepsis」。實際上下午訓練、上午測試 = 完全未來洩漏。

Shuffle 60-day ICU HR/SpO₂ for 5-fold CV, train LSTM at R²=0.99, publish "predicts sepsis". Reality: afternoon train, morning test = total future leakage.

好做法

按時間切：前 80% 訓練、後 20% 測試；用 walk-forward CV 評估；同時跑統計基準 (SARIMA, ETS) 與深度 (LSTM, TimesFM)；報告 RMSE + MAPE + sMAPE + 95% prediction CI；最終由臨床專家評估「介入時機」的決策價值。

Split chronologically: 80% train, 20% test; walk-forward CV; run statistical baselines (SARIMA, ETS) and deep (LSTM, TimesFM); report RMSE + MAPE + sMAPE + 95% prediction CI; clinicians review the "intervention timing" decision value.

SOTA 2025-2026

從 Lag-Llama 到時序基礎模型生態系

Lag-Llama (Rasul 2024)：首個「decoder-only 時序 LLM」，pretrained 在 millions of synthetic + real series；可 zero-shot forecast。實測在 MAE 上和 auto-ARIMA 接近，但對「非看過資料」有優勢。

Lag-Llama (Rasul 2024): first decoder-only TS LLM, pretrained on millions of synthetic + real series; zero-shot. MAE close to auto-ARIMA, but shines on unseen series.

Chronos (Ansari 2024, Amazon)：把序列量化成 tokens，直接用 T5 / GPT 預訓——「時序 = 文本」概念革命。Chronos-large (710M params) 在 27 個 TS benchmark 平均勝 N-BEATS 12%。

Chronos (Ansari 2024, Amazon): quantises series into tokens, pretrains on T5/GPT — "TS = text" paradigm shift. Chronos-large (710M params) beats N-BEATS by 12% avg on 27 TS benchmarks.

TimesFM (Das 2025, Google)：200M 參數、100B time-points pretrain；在 zero-shot TS Hub benchmark 25/30 dataset 達 SOTA。tfm.forecast(series, horizon=24) 一行搞定。

TimesFM (Das 2025, Google): 200M params, 100B time-points pretrain; SOTA on 25/30 zero-shot TS Hub. tfm.forecast(series, horizon=24) in one line.

Matrix Profile 進階：MPdist (Gharghabi 2018) 把 motif distance 變成「distribution-aware」；SegMixer (2025) 結合 MP 與深度分割，可在 long ECG 上偵測心律不整段。

Matrix Profile advances: MPdist (Gharghabi 2018) makes motif distance distribution-aware; SegMixer (2025) combines MP + deep segmentation for arrhythmia bouts in long ECG.

2026 方向：(a) 多模態時序——TS + 文字 (events) + 結構化特徵聯合預訓；(b) causal forecasting——預測「介入後」反事實；(c) 時序基礎模型的fine-tune efficiency——LoRA / adapter 取代 full retrain；(d) online learning 對 concept drift 自動適應。

2026 frontiers: (a) multi-modal TS — TS + text events + structured features jointly pretrained; (b) causal forecasting for "post-intervention" counterfactuals; (c) fine-tune efficiency for TS FMs — LoRA / adapters replace full retrain; (d) online learning for auto adaptation to concept drift.
