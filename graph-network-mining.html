開場故事
案例

🌐 1998 史丹佛車庫：兩個博士生發現「網頁是一張圖」

1996 年，史丹佛大學電腦系兩位博士生 Larry Page 與 Sergey Brin 為了找一個能「自動排序網頁重要性」的方法而陷入苦思。當時的搜尋引擎 (AltaVista、Lycos) 依靠 keyword 配對 + TF-IDF——但搜「university」回傳的第一頁可能是某個無人問津的學生個人網頁，因為他剛好把「university」這詞放了 50 次。

Page 注意到一個現象：網頁本質上是「節點」、超連結是「邊」。整個 WWW 不是「一堆獨立文件」，而是「一張巨大的有向圖」。他與 Brin 從學術引文分析得到靈感：在科學圈，「被很多人引用」是衡量論文重要性的核心指標——尤其是「被重要的人引用」。為什麼網頁不能用同樣邏輯？

1998 年初，他們在 Proceedings of WWW7 發表 "The Anatomy of a Large-Scale Hypertextual Web Search Engine"——其中提出 PageRank 公式：PR(v) = (1−d)/N + d · Σ PR(u)/L(u)。這個簡單的「隨機漫步 + 阻尼因子」改變了世界：他們成立的公司叫 Google。直到 2026 年的今天，PageRank 的變體仍在搜尋、推薦、藥物網路分析、論文影響力評估、新聞傳播研究——所有需要「重要性排序」的地方持續扮演核心角色。

真正的革命不是演算法的複雜度，而是「把資料看成圖」這個轉念。

1996. Two Stanford CS PhD students, Larry Page and Sergey Brin, wrestle with "how to auto-rank web pages by importance". The search engines of the day (AltaVista, Lycos) used keyword matching + TF-IDF — searching "university" might return some random student page that happened to repeat "university" 50 times.

Page notices: web pages are nodes; hyperlinks are edges. The whole WWW is not "a pile of documents" but "a giant directed graph". From academic citation analysis they borrow the insight: in science, "cited often" is the core importance metric — especially "cited by the important". Why not pages?

Early 1998, "The Anatomy of a Large-Scale Hypertextual Web Search Engine" in WWW7 — proposing PageRank: PR(v) = (1−d)/N + d · Σ PR(u)/L(u). This simple "random walk + damping" reshaped the world: the company they founded is called Google. By 2026, PageRank variants still power search, recommendation, drug-network analysis, paper-impact scoring, news propagation — anywhere "importance ranking" is needed.

The revolution wasn't algorithmic complexity — it was the leap of seeing data as a graph.

概覽

什麼是圖？

圖 (Graph) G=(V,E) 由 節點 (V) 與 邊 (E) 組成。依結構分：(1) 有向 vs 無向（Twitter follow 有向，Facebook 朋友無向）、(2) 加權 vs 無加權（蛋白質互動置信度）、(3) 同質 vs 異質（藥物–基因–疾病多型態 KG）、(4) 靜態 vs 動態 (temporal)。

圖探勘任務：中心性（找重要節點）、社群偵測（cluster 模組）、節點嵌入（轉成向量做下游 ML）、連結預測 (link prediction)、知識圖譜推理。生醫應用：藥物再利用 (drug repurposing)、副作用預測、致病基因排序。

A graph G=(V,E) consists of nodes (V) and edges (E). Structurally: (1) directed vs undirected (Twitter follow vs Facebook friend), (2) weighted vs unweighted (PPI confidence), (3) homogeneous vs heterogeneous (drug–gene–disease KG), (4) static vs dynamic / temporal.

Tasks: centrality (important nodes), community detection (modules), node embedding (vectors for downstream ML), link prediction, knowledge-graph reasoning. Biomedical use: drug repurposing, side-effect prediction, disease-gene ranking.

🎉
類比

圖探勘像「參加一場大派對」

Degree centrality＝看誰握手最多；Betweenness＝看誰是「不同圈子之間的橋樑」；PageRank 是「你紅，是因為很多紅人知道你」——靠重要鄰居傳遞名氣；Community detection (Louvain/Leiden) 是「找出派對中自然形成的小圈圈」；GNN message passing 是「每個人把自己知道的講給鄰居，再聽鄰居的，更新自己的世界觀，再講給下一輪鄰居」——多輪後，你的「個人簡介」就含有全派對的脈絡資訊。

Degree centrality = "who shakes the most hands?". Betweenness = "who bridges different cliques?". PageRank = "you're famous because famous people know you" — importance propagates via important neighbours. Community detection (Louvain/Leiden) = "find the cliques forming naturally". GNN message passing = "each person tells neighbours what they know, listens, updates their worldview, tells the next round" — after a few rounds, your "profile" carries the entire party's context.

時間線

從 1736 Euler 到 2026 圖基礎模型

1736 Euler 哥尼斯堡七橋Euler 用「圖論」解決「能否一次走完七座橋」——圖論誕生時刻。Euler invented "graph theory" to solve "can you walk all 7 bridges once?" — birth of graph theory.
1965 Watts-Strogatz 小世界提出「六度分離」與「小世界網路」的數學模型。Mathematical model of "six degrees of separation" and small-world networks.
1998 Page-Brin PageRankWWW7 論文發表，1998 年 9 月 Google 正式成立——改變網際網路。WWW7 paper; Google founded Sept 1998 — changed the internet.
2008 Blondel LouvainJ. Stat. Mech.；快速社群偵測，2008-2019 主導生物網路分析。J. Stat. Mech.; fast community detection — ruled biological-network analysis 2008-2019.
2014 Perozzi DeepWalkKDD；首度把 Word2Vec 思想搬到圖上——random walk → embedding。KDD; first to port Word2Vec to graphs — random walk → embedding.
2016 Grover-Leskovec node2vecKDD；加 p, q 參數平衡 BFS/DFS，可同時抓社群與結構等價。KDD; p, q parameters balance BFS/DFS — captures both community and structural equivalence.

2017 Hamilton GraphSAGENeurIPS；引入鄰居抽樣，inductive——可推到未見過的新節點。NeurIPS; introduces neighbour sampling, inductive — generalises to unseen nodes.
2017 Himmelstein HetioneteLife；47k nodes/2.2M edges 生醫異質圖，預測藥物再利用包括「尼古丁 → 阿茲海默」。eLife; 47k nodes/2.2M edges biomedical heterograph — predicted "nicotine → Alzheimer's" amongst others.

中心性

一、四種重要的中心性

🔢

① Degree

節點鄰居數。最簡單：人脈廣的人；連結最多的蛋白。

Number of neighbors. Simplest: well-connected person; high-degree protein.

🌉

② Betweenness

該節點落在多少對最短路徑上。「橋樑」型：交通樞紐、跨社群中介者。

Fraction of shortest paths passing through. "Bridge" nodes: hubs, cross-community brokers.

⭐

③ Eigenvector

「重要鄰居讓你也重要」。是鄰接矩陣最大特徵值對應的特徵向量。

"Important neighbors make you important". Eigenvector of the largest eigenvalue of the adjacency matrix.

🏆

④ PageRank

有向圖版的 eigenvector + 「random surfer + 阻尼 d」。PR(v) = (1−d)/N + d · Σ PR(u)/L(u)。當 d=0.85 是經典設定。

Directed-graph version + "random surfer + damping d". PR(v) = (1−d)/N + d · Σ PR(u)/L(u). d=0.85 is classic.

⏱️

⑤ Closeness

到所有其他節點最短路徑的倒數平均。值高=「離大家都近」。

Inverse mean shortest-path to all others. High = "close to everyone".

🔄

⑥ HITS

Kleinberg：好的 hub 連到很多 authority，好的 authority 被很多 hub 連。雙向迭代。

Kleinberg: good hubs link to many authorities; good authorities are linked by many hubs. Mutually iterative.

GNN 與 KG

三、GNN 與生醫知識圖譜

GNN (Graph Neural Network) 透過 message passing：每層中每個節點把自己 + 鄰居的特徵 aggregate → transform → 更新隱藏狀態。GCN (Kipf 2017)、GAT (attention)、GraphSAGE (sampling) 是三大基本款；GIN 在分子預測上常見。

生醫知識圖譜 (Biomedical KG) 將藥、基因、蛋白、疾病、副作用、文獻等串成異質網路：Hetionet (Himmelstein 2017, 47K nodes / 2M edges, 11 types)、DisGeNET (gene-disease)、PrimeKG (2023, 4M+ relations) 用於藥物再利用、罕病基因排序。

GNN = message passing: at each layer, every node aggregates self + neighbor features → transform → update hidden state. GCN (Kipf 2017), GAT (attention), GraphSAGE (sampling) are the three classics; GIN dominates molecular tasks.

Biomedical KGs connect drug-gene-protein-disease-side effect-literature as heterogeneous networks: Hetionet (Himmelstein 2017, 47K nodes / 2M edges, 11 types), DisGeNET (gene-disease), PrimeKG (2023, 4M+ relations) — used for drug repurposing, disease-gene ranking.

決策樹

四、如何選擇圖探勘方法？

🌳 圖探勘決策樹

Q1:
找 重要節點？ → Degree / Betweenness / PageRank / Eigenvector
Q2:
找 社群 / 模組？ → Leiden > Louvain（前者保證連通）
Q3:
要把節點變成 向量 做下游 ML？ → node2vec (BFS/DFS 可調) 或 DeepWalk
Q4:
有 節點特徵 且要可歸納到新節點？ → GraphSAGE / GAT (PyG, DGL)
Q5:
異質圖（多種節點/邊）？ → KG 嵌入 (TransE, RotatE) 或 RGCN
Q6:
要 連結預測（推薦、藥物再利用）？ → GNN + 邊分類器 / KG 三元組 score
Q1:
Find important nodes? → Degree / Betweenness / PageRank / Eigenvector
Q2:
Detect communities? → Leiden > Louvain (the former guarantees connectivity)
Q3:
Need node vectors for downstream ML? → node2vec (BFS/DFS tunable) or DeepWalk
Q4:
Have node features, need inductive on new nodes? → GraphSAGE / GAT (PyG, DGL)
Q5:
Heterogeneous graph (multi-type nodes/edges)? → KG embedding (TransE, RotatE) or RGCN
Q6:
Link prediction (recommendation, drug repurposing)? → GNN + edge classifier / KG triple score

library(igraph); library(tidygraph); library(ggraph)

# 1) 建圖 / Create graph
g <- graph_from_data_frame(edges_df, directed = TRUE)

# 2) 中心性 / Centralities
deg <- degree(g, mode = "in")
btw <- betweenness(g, normalized = TRUE)
pr  <- page_rank(g, damping = 0.85)$vector

# 3) 社群偵測 — Leiden（首選）
clu <- igraph::cluster_leiden(g, objective_function = "modularity",
                                resolution_parameter = 1)
V(g)$comm <- membership(clu)

# 4) 視覺化 / Plot
ggraph(as_tbl_graph(g), layout = "fr") +
  geom_edge_link(alpha = .3) +
  geom_node_point(aes(size = pr, color = factor(comm))) +
  geom_node_text(aes(label = name), repel = TRUE)

PR

情境：4 節點有向圖，d=0.85，N=4

N=4 · d=0.85 · 10 iters
STEP 1
建邊：A→B, A→C, B→C, C→A, D→A。out-degree L(A)=2, L(B)=1, L(C)=1, L(D)=1。觀察：節點 C 被 A, B 都連到，可能 PR 高；D 沒人連，PR 低。
Edges: A→B, A→C, B→C, C→A, D→A. out-degrees L(A)=2, L(B)=1, L(C)=1, L(D)=1. Note: C is linked by A and B — likely high PR; D has no inbound — low.
STEP 2
初始化：PR(A) = PR(B) = PR(C) = PR(D) = 1/4 = 0.25。基底項 (1−d)/N = 0.15/4 = 0.0375。
Initialise: PR(A) = PR(B) = PR(C) = PR(D) = 1/4 = 0.25. Base term (1−d)/N = 0.15/4 = 0.0375.
STEP 3
核心公式：PR(v) = 0.0375 + 0.85 · Σ_{u→v} PR(u)/L(u)。例如 PR(A) 來自 C→A 與 D→A：PR(A)' = 0.0375 + 0.85·(PR(C)/1 + PR(D)/1)。
Core formula: PR(v) = 0.0375 + 0.85 · Σ_{u→v} PR(u)/L(u). E.g. PR(A) gets contribution from C→A and D→A: PR(A)' = 0.0375 + 0.85·(PR(C)/1 + PR(D)/1).
STEP 4
第 1 次迭代：PR(A)=0.0375 + 0.85·(0.25+0.25)=0.463 ; PR(B)=0.0375+0.85·(0.25/2)=0.144 ; PR(C)=0.0375+0.85·(0.25/2+0.25/1)=0.357 ; PR(D)=0.0375 (沒人連)。和≈1（小誤差，因 D 為 dangling — 實際算法需重新分配）。
Iter 1: PR(A)=0.0375 + 0.85·(0.25+0.25)=0.463 ; PR(B)=0.0375+0.85·(0.25/2)=0.144 ; PR(C)=0.0375+0.85·(0.25/2+0.25/1)=0.357 ; PR(D)=0.0375 (no inbound). Sum ≈ 1 (small drift; D is dangling — real solver redistributes).
STEP 5
迭代 10 次後收斂：PR(A)=0.32, PR(B)=0.20, PR(C)=0.36, PR(D)=0.12。Power iteration 通常在 30 步內 ε=1e-6 收斂。
Converged after 10 iters: PR(A)=0.32, PR(B)=0.20, PR(C)=0.36, PR(D)=0.12. Power iteration usually hits ε=1e-6 within 30 steps.
STEP 6
排名：C > A > B > D。即使 D 沒人連，仍有 0.12 是 baseline (1−d)/N 提供。洞見：C 雖然 out-degree 也只有 1，但因被「重要的 A、B」連到，名氣傳染上來。
Ranking: C > A > B > D. Even with no inbound, D keeps 0.12 from baseline (1−d)/N. Insight: C only has out-degree 1, but is linked by "the important A and B" — fame propagates.
PR(v) = (1−d)/N + d · Σ_{u→v} PR(u) / L(u)d = 阻尼因子（通常 0.85）；L(u) = u 的出度；矩陣形式：PR = (1−d)/N·1 + d·Mᵀ PR，求 stationary distribution。d = damping (typically 0.85); L(u) = u's out-degree; matrix form: PR = (1−d)/N·1 + d·Mᵀ PR — the stationary distribution.
🔬 為什麼 d=0.85？為什麼存在「dangling node」問題？

d=0.85 的由來：Page-Brin 1998 試了 d∈{0.5, 0.75, 0.85, 0.95}，發現 d=0.85 在「收斂速度 vs 結果穩定」間最好。理論上 d 越小 → 收斂越快但 PR 過度均勻；d 越大 → 區分度高但 dangling node sink 嚴重、收斂慢。

Dangling node：沒有出邊的節點（D 雖然在我們的例子有 A 的進邊，但沒出邊就會「吸收」隨機漫步機率）。實務上解法：(1) 把 dangling node 的 PR 在每次迭代後平均分到所有節點；(2) 加上虛擬邊讓它連回所有節點。

Why d=0.85: Page-Brin (1998) tried d∈{0.5, 0.75, 0.85, 0.95} and found d=0.85 best balances convergence speed and stability. Smaller d → faster convergence but flatter PR; larger d → sharper differentiation but worse dangling-node sinks and slower convergence.

Dangling node: a node with no out-edges absorbs random-walk probability. Fixes: (1) redistribute its PR evenly each iter; (2) add virtual edges back to all nodes.

研究討論

三個值得思考的真問題

Q1

PageRank 衡量「相關性」還是「人氣」？

PageRank 本質上是「人氣」測度——和搜尋 query「相關性」其實是兩件事。

PageRank fundamentally measures "popularity" — distinct from query "relevance".

純人氣觀點：PageRank 不看內容，只看「誰被誰連」。Wikipedia 的 PR 永遠超高，無論你搜什麼。所以早期 Google 是「PR + TF-IDF」乘法 — PR 量人氣、TF-IDF 量主題相關。
Pure popularity: PageRank ignores content — only "who links whom". Wikipedia's PR is always huge, no matter the query. Early Google multiplied PR × TF-IDF — PR for fame, TF-IDF for topical relevance.
個人化 PageRank：2002 Topic-Sensitive PR、2003 Personalised PR 把 base teleport vector 改為 query-biased，於是 PR 變成 "query-aware fame"。現代搜尋（BERT-based ranking + RAG）已大幅減少純 PR 權重，但對學術 KG / 藥物網路 PR 仍是強信號。
Personalised PR: Topic-Sensitive PR (2002), Personalised PR (2003) reshape the base teleport vector to be query-biased — PR becomes "query-aware fame". Modern search (BERT-ranker + RAG) downweights raw PR, but in academic KGs / drug networks it remains a strong signal.
Q2

2026 年 Louvain 還有地位嗎？

Leiden 在 Scanpy / Seurat v5 已是預設；許多 paper 把 Louvain 列為「應淘汰」。

Leiden is the Scanpy / Seurat v5 default; many papers call Louvain "to be retired".

Pro-Leiden：保證連通性 + fast-local-move + 收斂更快；Traag 2019 證明 Louvain 偶有「分裂社群」(disconnected community)，違反我們對「社群」的直觀理解。多數新分析應預設 Leiden。
Pro-Leiden: connectivity guarantee + fast-local-move + faster convergence; Traag 2019 proved Louvain sometimes returns "disconnected communities" — violating our intuition. New analyses should default to Leiden.
Louvain 仍有用：(1) 老 paper 可重現性（reproducing 2014-2018 結果）；(2) 教學情境（演算法更易解釋）；(3) 在某些 sparse 圖上速度仍有優勢。實務：報告新分析用 Leiden，但「歷史對齊」用 Louvain。
Louvain still useful: (1) reproducing 2014-2018 papers; (2) teaching (more explainable); (3) sometimes faster on very sparse graphs. Practical: new analyses → Leiden, historical alignment → Louvain.
Q3

生醫 KG 上 GNN 真的有那麼神嗎？

PrimeKG、Hetionet 等 KG 上 GNN 論文滿坑滿谷——但 Nelson et al. 2022 質疑：很多「藥物再利用」預測在實驗中失敗。

PrimeKG, Hetionet GNN papers proliferate — but Nelson et al. 2022 reports many "drug repurposing" predictions failed in wet-lab.

Pro-GNN：GNN 能整合異質節點（藥、基因、疾病）、利用結構訊號、做歸納（新節點不必重訓）。Hetionet 預測「nicotine → Alzheimer」最終得到弱實驗支持；DrugBank-GNN 對 COVID 重定位有實際成功。
Pro-GNN: handles heterogeneous nodes (drug/gene/disease), exploits structure, inductive on new nodes. Hetionet's "nicotine → Alzheimer" got weak experimental support; DrugBank-GNN had real success in COVID repurposing.
反方：很多 KG 邊有系統性偏差（藥物-副作用 KG 常 over-report 已上市藥）；GNN 在小 KG 過擬合；over-smoothing；解釋性低。Nelson 2022 在 Nat Mach Intell 主張：「結構訊號不足以替代 mechanism。」
Counter: Many KG edges have systematic bias (drug-AE KGs over-report marketed drugs); GNNs overfit small KGs; over-smoothing; low interpretability. Nelson 2022 (Nat Mach Intell): "structural signal cannot replace mechanism."
實戰案例
💊
案例

Hetionet (Himmelstein 2017 eLife)：47k 節點 + 220 萬邊 = 藥物再利用 KG 典範

Daniel Himmelstein 在 UCSF 博士論文期間整合了 29 個生醫資料庫（DrugBank、UniProt、DisGeNET、SIDER、GO 等），建出 Hetionet v1.0：47,031 個節點（涵蓋 compound、gene、disease、symptom、pathway、anatomy 等 11 種）+ 2,250,197 條邊（24 種關係）。論文發在 eLife 2017，且整個資料庫開源，至今 GitHub 仍有人 fork。

During his UCSF PhD, Daniel Himmelstein merged 29 biomedical databases (DrugBank, UniProt, DisGeNET, SIDER, GO, etc.) into Hetionet v1.0: 47,031 nodes (11 types: compound, gene, disease, symptom, pathway, anatomy…) + 2,250,197 edges (24 relations). eLife 2017, fully open; still forked on GitHub today.

最戲劇性的發現：他們用 DWPC (degree-weighted path count) 計算「drug → disease」隱含路徑，預測了 209 個藥物再利用機會。其中「尼古丁 → 阿茲海默症」被排序很高——當時看似荒謬。但 2018-2022 多項臨床前研究確實顯示尼古丁透過 α7-nAChR 對 Aβ 累積有保護效果（Nordberg et al. 2020），雖未進入臨床但機制部分得到驗證。

Most dramatic finding: using DWPC (degree-weighted path count) for "drug → disease" implicit paths, they predicted 209 repurposing opportunities. "Nicotine → Alzheimer's" ranked high — seemingly absurd. But 2018-2022 preclinical work showed nicotine's α7-nAChR-mediated protection against Aβ accumulation (Nordberg et al. 2020); not yet clinical but mechanistically supported.

教訓：(1) 異質性 + 規模是生醫 KG 的關鍵；(2) 開源 + 可重現讓社群能擴展；(3) 預測必須由實驗驗證；(4) 之後的 PrimeKG (2023) 規模放大 100×，但 Hetionet 仍是教學與基準的入門首選。

Lessons: (1) heterogeneity + scale are the keys for biomedical KGs; (2) open + reproducible lets communities extend; (3) predictions must be validated experimentally; (4) PrimeKG (2023) is 100× larger, but Hetionet remains the teaching benchmark.

常見陷阱

六個地雷與修正

① 對「不連通圖」套 Louvain

Louvain 對不連通圖會把每個連通分量當社群，結果 trivial。修正：先用 connected_components 把圖切開分別偵測，或改用 Leiden（可處理）。

Louvain on disconnected graphs trivially marks each component as its own community. Fix: split via connected_components first, or use Leiden (handles this).

② 忽略邊權重

PPI 網路中 STRING score 700 vs 900 的邊意義天差地別；忽略 weight 等於丟掉訊號。修正：centrality / community / GNN 都需設 weight；NetworkX 的 weight='conf' 參數。

In PPI nets, STRING 700 vs 900 edges differ enormously; ignoring weight throws away signal. Fix: set weight= in centrality / community / GNN; NetworkX accepts weight='conf'.

③ 對未清理的圖算中心性

自迴圈、重複邊、isolated node 都會扭曲 centrality。修正：計算前用 G.remove_edges_from(nx.selfloop_edges(G))、移除孤立點、合併平行邊。

Self-loops, duplicate edges, isolates distort centrality. Fix: G.remove_edges_from(nx.selfloop_edges(G)), drop isolates, merge parallel edges first.

④ GNN 在小圖過擬合

N<500 節點訓 GCN 兩層 → train 99% / test 60%。修正：用 dropout / edge-drop / mixup；可考慮 GraphSAGE + neighbour sampling；或退回 node2vec + LightGBM。

N<500 nodes for 2-layer GCN → 99% train / 60% test. Fix: dropout / edge-drop / mixup; switch to GraphSAGE + sampling; or fall back to node2vec + LightGBM.

⑤ 把有向邊當無向

Twitter follow、基因調控 (TF → target) 都是有向，丟掉方向 → PageRank 失準、社群定義錯。修正：networkx 用 DiGraph；GNN 用 directed message passing；KG 永遠保留 (h, r, t) 順序。

Twitter follows / gene regulation (TF → target) are directed; flattening kills PageRank and miscalls communities. Fix: networkx DiGraph; directed message passing in GNN; always keep (h, r, t) order in KG.

⑥ GNN over-smoothing

5+ 層 GCN 後所有節點 embedding 變相似，分類掉到 random。修正：限制 ≤3 層；用 residual / PairNorm / GCNII；或改用 graph attention + 邊 dropout。

After 5+ GCN layers, all embeddings collapse to similar — classification drops to random. Fix: ≤3 layers; use residual / PairNorm / GCNII; or graph attention + edge dropout.

壞做法 vs 好做法

壞做法

把生醫 KG 的所有邊讀進 NetworkX、跑 betweenness centrality（不顧 KG 有自迴圈、重複邊、不連通分量），輸出 top-10 排名直接報告為「最重要基因」。

Load all KG edges into NetworkX, run betweenness centrality (ignoring self-loops, duplicates, disconnected components), report top-10 as "most important genes".

好做法

先 audit：自迴圈、平行邊、connected_components、degree distribution；分別跑 degree / PageRank / betweenness 並交叉驗證；用 Leiden 找模組、檢查連通性；GNN 用 GraphSAGE + neighbour sampling，做 5-fold edge-split CV；最後請生物學家評估 top-10 是否符合領域常識。

Audit first: self-loops, parallel edges, components, degree distribution; cross-check degree / PageRank / betweenness; Leiden for modules with connectivity check; GraphSAGE + sampling for GNN with 5-fold edge-split CV; biologists review top-10 for domain plausibility.

2026 Graph FMs: OneForAll (Liu 2024 ICLR), GraphAny (2025), GFM attempt "cross-domain zero-shot" — same model transferring between social, citation, protein nets. Still early, but seen as the "BERT moment" candidate for graphs.
