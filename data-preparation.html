故事化導讀

📊 47% 缺失值、三種「Female」、與一個崩潰的下午

Karim 是一家跨國藥廠 R&D 的資料分析師。週一上午老闆把一個 12 GB CSV 丟過來：「這是我們三個臨床試驗的合併資料，下週要建糖尿病風險模型。」

Karim 打開檔案，第一個欄位 date_of_birth 立刻讓他傻眼：英國地點用 DD/MM/YYYY、美國地點用 MM/DD/YYYY、印度地點用 YYYY-MM-DD。第二個欄位 sex：「Female」、「F」、「female」、「Mujer」（西語）、「女」（中文）⋯⋯五種寫法。

HbA1c 缺失率 47%——但他發現缺失不是隨機的：缺失值大多來自已經死亡的病人（未抽血即離世）。這是 MNAR (Missing Not At Random)。如果他用 mean imputation 補值，模型會學到「HbA1c 平均 ≈ 高血糖人」的虛假相關，預測會嚴重偏差。

那個下午他做了三件事：（1）寫一個 schema_check.py 統一所有單位與日期；（2）為 HbA1c 新增 HbA1c_missing 二元欄位；（3）打電話給老闆說：「資料準備至少要兩週，不能下週交。」那一刻起，他真正成為資料科學家。

Karim is an R&D data analyst at a multinational pharma. Monday morning his boss hands him a 12 GB CSV: "Merged data from three clinical trials. Build a diabetes risk model by next week."

Karim opens it. Column 1: date_of_birth. UK sites use DD/MM/YYYY, US sites MM/DD/YYYY, India sites YYYY-MM-DD. Column 2: sex. "Female", "F", "female", "Mujer" (Spanish), 「女」 (Chinese) — five encodings.

HbA1c is 47% missing — but the missingness is non-random: most missing rows are patients who died before blood was drawn. This is MNAR (Missing Not At Random). Mean imputation would teach the model a fake "HbA1c ≈ average hyperglycemia patient" pattern, badly biasing predictions.

That afternoon he did three things: (1) wrote schema_check.py to harmonize units and dates; (2) added a HbA1c_missing binary feature; (3) called his boss: "Data prep needs at least two weeks. Not next week." That was the moment he became a real data scientist.

名詞速查

14 個關鍵詞：從直白到精確

MCAR / MAR / MNAR
三種缺失機制
MCAR=隨機掉牙；MAR=刷牙頻率決定掉牙；MNAR=「壞牙才不敢給人看」（缺失本身藏訊息）。
MCAR = random tooth loss; MAR = brushing frequency drives loss; MNAR = "won't show bad teeth" (missingness itself carries info).
Rubin 1976 提出。MCAR: P(R|X,Y)=const；MAR: P(R|X,Y)=P(R|X_obs)；MNAR: P(R|X,Y) 與 Y_miss 有關，必須建模。
Rubin 1976. MCAR: P(R|X,Y)=const; MAR: P(R|X,Y)=P(R|X_obs); MNAR: P(R|X,Y) depends on Y_miss — must be modeled.
MICE
多重補值連環方程式
先猜每個空格、再用周圍的人來矯正——做 5 次，看看你預測會抖多少。
Guess each blank, then correct with neighbors — repeat 5 times to see how much your prediction wobbles.
Multivariate Imputation by Chained Equations (van Buuren 2011)。對每個缺失欄位用其他欄位迴歸補值，重複 m=5–20 次得到不確定性。
van Buuren 2011. Regress each missing column on the others; iterate m=5–20 times to quantify imputation uncertainty.
KNN Imputer
最近鄰補值
缺空格時，找最像你的 5 個鄰居，把他們那一欄的平均填進去。
When a value is missing, find your 5 nearest neighbors and average their column.
Troyanskaya 2001 提出。用 k 個最近樣本的平均（或加權）填入缺失值；對高維與類別欄位失效。
Troyanskaya 2001. Uses k nearest samples' mean (or weighted mean); fails in high-D and on categoricals.
Target Leakage
目標外洩
考試前偷看答案——模型「作弊」了。
Peeking at answers before the exam — the model is cheating.
特徵包含預測時間點不可得的未來資訊；驗證 AUROC 過高 (>0.95) 是徵兆。
Features contain future info unavailable at prediction time; AUROC > 0.95 is a red flag.
Train/Val/Test Split
三段切割
訓練 = 寫筆記；驗證 = 模擬考；測試 = 真正大考。三個不能混。
Train = study notes; Val = mock exam; Test = real exam. Never mix them.
典型 60/20/20 或 80/10/10；時序資料用時間切；醫療資料用 by-site 切。
Typically 60/20/20 or 80/10/10; time-aware split for time series; by-site split for medical data.
Stratified Split
分層切割
確保訓練 / 測試各有相同比例的「少數族群」，不會偶然全集中在一邊。
Ensures train/test keep the same minority-class ratio — no accidental clustering on one side.
按 y 分層抽樣，保 P(y) 相同；對極不平衡資料必須。
Sample stratified by y, preserving P(y); essential for highly imbalanced data.
SMOTE
合成少數類過採樣
人少不夠用，就把兩個少數樣本連起來，中間「造」一個新樣本。
Too few minorities — connect two and "synthesize" a new sample on the line between them.
Chawla 2002。在 k-NN 線性插值合成少數樣本；只能用於訓練集！2022 多篇 paper 質疑效果。
Chawla 2002. Linear interpolation along k-NN for the minority; training set only! 2022 papers question its effectiveness.
ADASYN
自適應合成
SMOTE 的進階版——在「分類邊界」生成更多樣本，而非均勻分散。
An advanced SMOTE — generates more near the decision boundary, not uniformly.
He 2008。權重高給「難分」的少數樣本鄰域；可能放大噪音。
He 2008. Weights up "hard" minority neighborhoods; can amplify noise.
Undersampling
欠採樣
與其補少數，不如丟掉多數——但小心丟到重要訊息。
Instead of growing the minority, shrink the majority — but you may toss valuable info.
Random Undersampling、Tomek Links、Edited NN。對極不平衡與大資料較友善。
Random Undersampling, Tomek Links, Edited NN. Useful for extreme imbalance + large data.
Scaling
特徵縮放
身高 (cm) 跟體重 (kg) 數字不同——縮到同一尺度才能公平比較。
Height (cm) and weight (kg) live on different scales — normalize them before comparing.
Min-Max、StandardScaler、RobustScaler、QuantileTransformer。fit 只能在 train，再 transform 到 test！
Min-Max, StandardScaler, RobustScaler, QuantileTransformer. Fit on train only, then transform test!
Robust Scaling
穩健縮放
用「中位數 + IQR」代替「平均 + 標準差」——極端值不再影響整體。
Uses median + IQR instead of mean + std — extreme values stop dominating.
x_scaled = (x − median) / IQR；對含 outlier 的生物資料較佳。
x_scaled = (x − median) / IQR; preferred for biomedical data with heavy outliers.
One-Hot Encoding
獨熱編碼
把「紅／黃／藍」拆成三個「是 / 不是」欄位，每個樣本只在一欄填 1。
Split "red/yellow/blue" into three "yes/no" columns — each row gets a 1 in only one.
每個類別新增二元欄；高基數類別會爆維。建議 ≤ 50 unique values 才用。
Adds a binary column per category; explodes for high-cardinality. Recommended only when ≤ 50 unique values.
Target Encoding
目標編碼
用「在這個類別裡，多少人是陽性？」替代類別名稱——但小心洩漏。
Replace each category with "what fraction of this group is positive?" — but beware leakage.
x_cat → mean(y|x_cat)；高基數類別友善。必須 nested CV / out-of-fold 否則洩漏。
x_cat → mean(y|x_cat); high-cardinality friendly. Requires nested CV / out-of-fold or it leaks.
Data Dictionary
資料字典
每個欄位的「使用說明書」：意義、單位、範圍、誰填的、什麼時候填的。
A "user manual" for every column: meaning, unit, range, who filled it, when.
FAIR 原則第一步；常用 Frictionless Data、DCAT-AP、Open Targets ontology。
Step 1 of FAIR; common tools include Frictionless Data, DCAT-AP, Open Targets ontology.

核心步驟

一、六個關鍵子步驟

資料準備（含清理、整合、變形、特徵工程）通常佔資料探勘專案 60–80% 的時間。它決定了模型輸入的「天花板」——若資料含大量缺失、編碼不一致、外洩 (target leakage)，再強的演算法也只會放大雜訊。

在生物醫學脈絡下，這個階段更具挑戰：EHR 缺失非隨機 (MNAR)、不同醫院 ICD 碼版本不一、檢驗值單位混雜 (mg/dL vs mmol/L)、組學批次效應 (batch effect)。許多失敗的探勘專案——例如多個 sepsis 早期預警模型於外院驗證崩潰——根因都出在這一步。

Data Preparation (cleaning, integration, transformation, feature engineering) typically consumes 60–80% of the project time. It defines the ceiling of model input — if the data has heavy missingness, inconsistent encoding, or target leakage, the strongest algorithm only amplifies noise.

In a biomedical context, this phase is even harder: EHR missingness is non-random (MNAR), ICD versions differ across hospitals, lab units are mixed (mg/dL vs mmol/L), and omics batch effects dominate signal. Many failed mining projects — e.g., several sepsis early-warning models collapsing at external validation — trace back to this step.

🚨
Target leakage 警告：「為什麼我的 AUROC = 0.99？」十次有九次的答案是target leakage：例如把出院後的 discharge_diagnosis 餵進 readmit 模型；或把 follow-up CRP 當作 sepsis 預測特徵。任何使用「未來才會知道」的資訊都是不可接受的。 Target leakage warning: "Why is my AUROC = 0.99?" Nine times out of ten the answer is target leakage — e.g., feeding post-discharge diagnosis into a readmission model, or treating follow-up CRP as a sepsis predictor. Any feature that uses "future" information is unacceptable.

一、六個關鍵子步驟

🧼

① 清理 Cleaning

處理缺失值、重複列、不一致拼寫（如 'M' vs 'Male'）、單位轉換、時間戳對齊。

Handle missing values, duplicate rows, inconsistent spellings ('M' vs 'Male'), unit conversion, timestamp alignment.

🔗

② 整合 Integration

串接多個來源（demographic + lab + medication），處理鍵衝突、版本歧異與時間視窗對齊。

Join multiple sources (demographic + lab + medication); resolve key conflicts, version drift, time-window alignment.

✂️

③ 切割 Splitting

Train / Validation / Test split；時序資料用時間切割，醫院資料用 by-hospital 切割避免 leakage。

Train / Validation / Test split; use time-based split for temporal data, by-hospital split to avoid leakage.

⚖️

④ 縮放 Scaling

Min-Max、Z-score、Robust、Quantile。距離型 (KNN/SVM/K-Means) 必須；樹型則不需要。

Min-Max, Z-score, Robust, Quantile. Mandatory for distance-based (KNN/SVM/K-Means); not needed for tree-based.

🔢

⑤ 編碼 Encoding

One-Hot、Ordinal、Target、Frequency；高基數類別建議 Target Encoding + CV-fold。

One-Hot, Ordinal, Target, Frequency. For high-cardinality use Target Encoding + CV-fold guarding.

⚙️

⑥ 特徵工程

分箱、log/exp、交互特徵、時間特徵（season、weekday）、領域知識特徵（BMI、CHA₂DS₂-VASc）。

Binning, log/exp, interaction terms, time features (season, weekday), domain features (BMI, CHA₂DS₂-VASc).

資料準備就像料理

沒有人能把生肉、未洗的蔬菜、整罐鹽巴直接丟進烤箱，然後期待端出米其林菜。料理 80% 時間在備料：洗、切、調味、醃漬、平衡口味。建模就像「按下烤箱開關」——快但只是最後一步。把備料當作可有可無的「pre-step」，是新手廚師最大的迷思。

Modeling is "pressing the oven button" — fast, but it's just the final step. Treating prep as a skippable "pre-step" is the rookie cook's biggest mistake.

類型意義處理建議

🌳 缺失值處理決策樹

Q1:
缺失率 < 5% 且樣本量充足？ → 是 → 直接 listwise delete。
Q2:
是 MCAR/MAR？ → 是 → MICE（多重補值，建議 m=5–20 次）。
Q3:
樹型模型如 XGBoost？ → 是 → 直接交給 model 處理 NA（sklearn 1.6 後 ExtraTrees 也支援）。
Q4:
懷疑 MNAR？ → 是 → 加 is_missing 二元指標當特徵，避免直接補導致偏差。
Q1:
Missingness < 5% with abundant samples? → Yes → Listwise delete.
Q2:
MCAR/MAR? → Yes → MICE (multiple imputation, m=5–20).
Q3:
Tree-based model (XGBoost)? → Yes → Let the model handle NA natively (sklearn 1.6+ also for ExtraTrees).
Q4:
Suspect MNAR? → Yes → Add an is_missing indicator as feature; never directly impute.
程式碼

實作：Pipeline + Imputer + Scaler

library(tidymodels); library(mice); library(themis)

# 1) 缺失值多重補值 / Multiple imputation
imp <- mice(df, m = 5, method = "pmm", seed = 42)
df_imp <- complete(imp, 1)

# 2) recipe：縮放 + 編碼 + SMOTE / scale + encode + SMOTE
rec <- recipe(target ~ ., data = df_imp) |>
  step_impute_knn(all_predictors(), neighbors = 5) |>
  step_normalize(all_numeric_predictors()) |>
  step_dummy(all_nominal_predictors()) |>
  step_smote(target, over_ratio = 1.0)         # themis

# 3) Train/test 切割避免 leakage
sp <- initial_split(df_imp, prop = 0.8, strata = target)
prep <- prep(rec, training = training(sp))
tr <- bake(prep, training(sp))
te <- bake(prep, testing(sp))   # test 不可再 SMOTE
from sklearn.compose       import ColumnTransformer
from sklearn.impute        import SimpleImputer, KNNImputer
from sklearn.preprocessing import StandardScaler, OneHotEncoder
from imblearn.over_sampling import SMOTE
from imblearn.pipeline      import Pipeline   # note imblearn pipeline
from sklearn.model_selection import train_test_split

X_tr, X_te, y_tr, y_te = train_test_split(
    X, y, test_size=0.2, stratify=y, random_state=42)

pre = ColumnTransformer([
    ("num", Pipeline([
        ("imp",   KNNImputer(n_neighbors=5)),
        ("sc",    StandardScaler())]), num_cols),
    ("cat", OneHotEncoder(handle_unknown="ignore"), cat_cols)
])

pipe = Pipeline([
    ("pre",   pre),
    ("smote", SMOTE(random_state=42)),     # test 不會被 SMOTE
    ("clf",   XGBClassifier(n_estimators=300))
])
pipe.fit(X_tr, y_tr)
⚠️
SMOTE 的常見錯誤：絕對不能在 train/test split 之前 做 SMOTE！否則合成樣本會洩漏到測試集。Pipeline 內的 imblearn.Pipeline 會自動只對 train fold 套用。 SMOTE pitfall: Never apply SMOTE before train/test split — synthesized samples will leak into the test fold. The imblearn Pipeline automatically applies SMOTE only on training folds.

完整案例走讀：糖尿病 EHR 前處理

9 步
Step 1
Schema
schema 檢查： 用 pandera 或 great_expectations 定義欄位類型、單位、合理範圍。範例：age 必須 integer 0–120、HbA1c 必須 4–14 mg%、sex 必須 ∈ {M, F, Other}。 Define column types, units, valid ranges with pandera or great_expectations. e.g., age int 0–120, HbA1c 4–14 mg%, sex ∈ {M, F, Other}.
Step 2
Units
單位調和： 英美 HbA1c 用 mg%、歐洲用 mmol/mol。寫一個 convert_hba1c() 函式統一：mmol/mol = (mg% − 2.15) / 0.0915。Glucose mg/dL ↔ mmol/L 乘 0.0555。 US uses HbA1c mg%; EU uses mmol/mol. Write convert_hba1c(): mmol/mol = (mg% − 2.15) / 0.0915. Glucose mg/dL ↔ mmol/L × 0.0555.
Step 3
Encode
類別編碼： sex 五種寫法 → 用 dict map 為三類；diagnosis_code 高基數 → target encoding + 5-fold CV out-of-fold；medication ATC ontology 對齊。 sex with 5 encodings → dict-map to 3 classes; high-cardinality diagnosis_code → out-of-fold target encoding; medication aligned to ATC ontology.
Step 4
Impute
MICE 多重補值： 對 MAR 欄位用 MICE m=10、predictor matrix 限制只用同就診資料；對 MNAR (HbA1c) 加 HbA1c_missing 二元指標再補。 For MAR columns, MICE m=10 with predictor matrix restricted to same-visit data; for MNAR (HbA1c) add HbA1c_missing indicator then impute.
Step 5
Outlier
異常值處理： 用 IQR 標記 (Q1 − 1.5 IQR, Q3 + 1.5 IQR) 之外的值。不是自動刪：BMI=12 可能是真實的厭食症患者，需審查上下文。 Flag values outside (Q1 − 1.5 IQR, Q3 + 1.5 IQR). Don't auto-delete: BMI=12 could be a real anorexia case — review in context.
Step 6
Split
切割： 用 StratifiedGroupKFold，group=patient_id 避免同一病人跨 fold；stratify=y 保少數族群比例；tilt by site 模擬外院驗證。 Use StratifiedGroupKFold: group=patient_id to prevent patient-leak across folds, stratify=y for minority preservation, tilt by site to simulate external validation.
Step 7
Scale
縮放： RobustScaler（含 outlier 友善）；fit 只在 train、transform 套到 test。絕對不能 fit 整個 dataset 再切！ RobustScaler (outlier-friendly); fit on train only, transform on test. Never fit on the whole dataset then split!
Step 8
Test Enc.
測試集編碼： test 用 train 的 mean/std、target encoder、imputer 統計量；新類別預設用 handle_unknown="ignore"。 Test uses train's mean/std, target encoder, imputer statistics; new categories default to handle_unknown="ignore".
Step 9
Final QC
最終 QC： 檢查 tr.shape, te.shape、無 NaN、無 inf、特徵 mean ≈ 0 std ≈ 1（若 Standard）、保存 preprocessor.joblib + git commit hash + data version。 Check tr.shape, te.shape, no NaN, no inf, feature mean ≈ 0 std ≈ 1 (if Standard); save preprocessor.joblib + git commit hash + data version.
思辨

三個值得爭論的問題

爭論 #1

應該刪除任何含缺失的列嗎？

最簡單的 listwise deletion——但會丟掉多少訊息？
The simplest listwise deletion — but how much info does it discard?
支持： 當缺失 < 5% 且確定為 MCAR，listwise 是無偏估計、簡單可重現。 When missing < 5% and confirmed MCAR, listwise gives unbiased estimates — simple and reproducible.
反對： 在 EHR 缺失幾乎都是 MAR 或 MNAR——刪除會引入嚴重 selection bias（例如預測心衰時刪掉所有沒測 BNP 的病人 = 刪掉狀況最差的人）。 In EHR data, missingness is almost always MAR or MNAR — deletion introduces severe selection bias (e.g., dropping unmeasured-BNP patients in heart failure prediction = dropping the sickest).
爭論 #2

SMOTE 究竟有用還是有害？

2022 Goorbergh、2024 Elor & Averbuch-Elor 等多篇 systematic review 質疑 SMOTE 的實用效果。
Multiple 2022–2024 systematic reviews (Goorbergh, Elor & Averbuch-Elor) question SMOTE's real-world effectiveness.
支持： 當你只在乎 minority recall（如罕病偵測）、樣本量小、模型對類別不平衡敏感——SMOTE 仍可提升 recall 5–10%。 When you care only about minority recall (rare disease detection), small sample, model sensitive to imbalance — SMOTE can still boost recall 5–10%.
反對： 2024 review：在 73 個資料集中，SMOTE + 樹型模型相比僅用 class_weight 改善 < 1%；且會破壞 calibration——AUROC 看似上升，但 PPV 與決策變差。建議：先試 class weight，再考慮 SMOTE。 2024 review: across 73 datasets, SMOTE + tree models improved < 1% over class_weight alone; SMOTE breaks calibration — AUROC may rise but PPV and decisions degrade. Recommendation: try class weight first, SMOTE second.
爭論 #3

Target Encoding 必然洩漏嗎？

高基數類別（5000 zip codes、10000 ICD-10）用 one-hot 會爆維——但 target encoding 也常被罵「洩漏」。
High-cardinality features (5000 ZIP codes, 10000 ICD-10) explode with one-hot — yet target encoding is often blamed for leakage.
觀點 A： 如果用 out-of-fold 編碼（CV 內每 fold 重新計算）+ smoothing（小類別向全域平均收縮）+ 高 prior weight，理論上沒有洩漏。 With out-of-fold encoding (recompute within each CV fold) + smoothing (small categories shrink toward global mean) + high prior weight, leakage is bounded.
觀點 B： 即使 out-of-fold，編碼仍然「peek」過全 dataset 的標籤分佈——這在臨床外院驗證時會崩潰。改用 CatBoost 的 ordered target encoding（按時間順序）較安全。 Even out-of-fold "peeks" at the dataset label distribution — collapses at external validation. Safer: CatBoost's ordered target encoding (time-ordered).

經典案例

Epic Sepsis Watch：當資料準備出錯，AI 模型如何崩盤

Epic EHR 系統內建的 Sepsis Watch 早期預警模型曾部署至 170+ 美國醫院。2021 年 JAMA Internal Medicine 一項外部驗證（Wong et al.）發現，在 38,455 名病患中，模型敏感度僅 33%、PPV 12%——遠低於 Epic 宣稱的 ~80%。事後分析發現多個資料準備層面的問題：(1) Epic 用整個 dataset 計算 imputation 統計，造成 leakage；(2) 訓練資料的 sepsis 標籤定義與 deployment 環境不同；(3) 缺失值處理沒考慮 MNAR（敗血症高風險病人血壓往往未測即急救）。

Epic EHR's built-in Sepsis Watch early-warning model was deployed at 170+ US hospitals. A 2021 JAMA Internal Medicine external validation (Wong et al., 38,455 patients) found 33% sensitivity, 12% PPV — far below Epic's claimed ~80%. Post-mortem analysis revealed multiple data-prep failures: (1) Epic computed imputation statistics over the entire dataset, leaking; (2) sepsis labels in training differed from deployment definitions; (3) missing-value handling ignored MNAR (high-risk patients' BP often never measured before resuscitation).

教訓：資料準備的錯誤可能完全看不見——直到部署半年後在另一家醫院驗證時才爆出。一旦 leakage 進入訓練流程，連最強的 XGBoost 也救不回來。

Lesson: Data-prep errors can be invisible — only revealed months later at another hospital. Once leakage enters training, not even the strongest XGBoost can save you.

CASE STUDY

Apple Card 2019：「性別盲」演算法仍歧視女性

2019 年 11 月，Apple 聯合創辦人 Steve Wozniak 在 Twitter 抱怨：他與妻子的財產合併、信用紀錄相同，但 Apple Card 給他 10 倍於妻子的信用額度。隨後數百名用戶回報相同性別差異。紐約金融服務部 (DFS) 隨即展開調查。Apple 與 Goldman Sachs 強調模型「並未使用性別作為特徵」（gender-blind）——但這正是資料準備層面的根本誤解：不使用 protected attribute 並不等於模型公平。歷史上女性與男性的「消費速度」「主要持卡人 vs 副卡」「商家類別分布」等特徵都隱含性別信號，模型仍透過這些 proxy 學到了歧視。Apple-Goldman 的失敗不是模型問題，而是資料準備時沒有做 demographic parity / equalized odds 的審計。2021 年 DFS 結案聲明：「未發現故意歧視，但流程確實未檢測公平性，建議所有金融機構導入 fairness-aware preprocessing。」

In November 2019, Apple co-founder Steve Wozniak tweeted that he and his wife shared finances and credit history yet Apple Card gave him a 10× higher limit. Hundreds of users echoed the disparity; the NY Department of Financial Services opened an investigation. Apple and Goldman Sachs insisted the model was "gender-blind" — but this is the canonical data-prep misconception: not using a protected attribute does not make a model fair. Features like "spend velocity," "primary vs authorized user," and "merchant-category mix" all carry implicit gender signal; the model learned discrimination through these proxies. The failure wasn't in modeling — it was in data preparation, which never audited demographic parity or equalized odds. The 2021 DFS report concluded: "No intentional discrimination found, but fairness was never tested; we recommend fairness-aware preprocessing across the industry."

啟示：資料準備不只是「填缺失、做標準化」——更要做fairness preprocessing（reweighting、disparate impact remover、fair representation learning）。否則 leakage 不是訊息洩漏，而是偏見洩漏。

Implication: Data prep is not only "impute + scale" — it must include fairness preprocessing (reweighting, disparate-impact remover, fair-representation learning). Otherwise the leakage isn't information — it's bias.

陷阱

六大常見陷阱

① fit 整 dataset 再切

在切割前用整個 dataset 跑 StandardScaler / SimpleImputer / OneHotEncoder——test set 的統計資訊已洩漏。解：用 Pipeline，fit 只在 train。

Running StandardScaler / SimpleImputer / OneHotEncoder on the whole dataset before split — test statistics leak. Fix: use Pipeline; fit on train only.

② SMOTE 套用至 test

用 sklearn.Pipeline 包 SMOTE → 它會在預測時也呼叫 SMOTE！必須用 imblearn.Pipeline（自動知道 SMOTE 不在 transform 時執行）。

Wrapping SMOTE in sklearn.Pipeline → it triggers SMOTE at prediction time! Must use imblearn.Pipeline (knows SMOTE is fit-only).

③ 全刪缺失而無 MNAR 檢

看到 30% 缺失就 listwise delete——但其實大多缺失來自最重症病人。模型訓練在「健康 subset」，部署時遇到真實病人就崩。解：先檢查 P(missing | y) vs P(missing) 差異。

Seeing 30% missing and listwise-deleting — but most missing rows are the sickest patients. Trains on a "healthy subset," collapses on real patients. Fix: compare P(missing | y) vs P(missing).

④ 高基數 one-hot

5000 個 ZIP code 全部 one-hot → 維度爆炸、樣本變稀疏。解：頻率編碼 / target encoding（含 CV-fold 保護）/ entity embedding。

One-hot encoding 5000 ZIP codes → dimension explosion, data becomes sparse. Fix: frequency encoding / target encoding (with CV-fold) / entity embedding.

⑤ 無 out-of-fold target enc.

用整個 train set 算 target encoding，再丟進 CV——train fold 內已洩漏。解：category_encoders.TargetEncoder 配合 sklearn.model_selection.cross_val_score 用內 fold。

Computing target encoding over the whole train set then feeding into CV — leakage within each train fold. Fix: category_encoders.TargetEncoder with proper inner-fold CV.

⑥ 不存 preprocessor

部署時忘了保存 imputer / scaler / encoder——只存了 model.pkl。輸入資料分佈一變就完全崩。解：整個 pipeline 一起 joblib.dump。

Deployment time, forgot to save imputer / scaler / encoder — only saved model.pkl. Any input distribution change crashes it. Fix: dump the entire pipeline via joblib.dump.

🌳 進階研究員

2024–2026 前沿：缺失值與重採樣新方向

MissForest vs mice：Stekhoven & Bühlmann 2012 的 MissForest 用 Random Forest 迭代補值，對非線性與混合型資料較佳；mice 假設條件分佈為 GLM，較適合線性問題。DOI。

MissForest vs mice: Stekhoven & Bühlmann 2012's MissForest iteratively imputes via Random Forest — better for nonlinearity and mixed types; mice assumes conditional GLMs, better for linear problems.

"Don't impute, model missingness" (Halland 2021)：對 EHR 缺失常為 MNAR，補值反而引入偏差；新興方法直接把是否缺失當預測信號（HistGradientBoosting 原生 NA、CatBoost、LightGBM）。

"Don't impute, model missingness" (Halland 2021): In EHR data missing-NOT-at-random dominates; imputation introduces bias. Emerging practice: use is_missing as a feature (HistGradientBoosting, CatBoost, LightGBM all support native NA).

PyMC for Bayesian imputation：把補值寫成階層 Bayesian model，缺失值是後驗分佈而非單點估計——適合下游風險決策需要不確定性的場景。

PyMC for Bayesian imputation: write imputation as a hierarchical Bayesian model — missing values are posterior distributions, not point estimates. Suited for downstream decisions needing uncertainty.

CausalImpact for MNAR：2024 Pearl & Bareinboim 倡議的 do-calculus 補值——若知道缺失機制的 DAG，可以做 identifiable 推論。

CausalImpact for MNAR: Pearl & Bareinboim 2024 advocate do-calculus imputation — given the missingness mechanism's DAG, identifiable inference becomes possible.

2025 SMOTE 替代品：(1) SMOGN for regression；(2) Generative Models（GAN、Diffusion）合成；(3) Cost-sensitive learning + focal loss；(4) Threshold tuning（最簡單）。Carmona-Sáez 2024 在 Nat Methods 系統比較 12 種方法。

2025 SMOTE alternatives: (1) SMOGN for regression; (2) Generative models (GAN, Diffusion); (3) Cost-sensitive learning + focal loss; (4) Threshold tuning (simplest). Carmona-Sáez 2024 systematically compares 12 methods in Nat Methods.
