故事

開場：五家醫院的糖尿病足潰瘍

情境

🩺 Maria 的合作計畫

Maria 是醫療資訊學家。她想合作 5 家醫院、共 12,400 名糖尿病足潰瘍 (DFU) 病人，比較截肢率與長期存活。第一天打開資料，她哭笑不得：A 醫院用 L97.519、B 醫院寫成 E11.621 + L97.5、C 醫院只有自由文字「右足蜂窩性組織炎 c/o DM」、D 醫院還在用 ICD-9 707.14、E 醫院已升級到 ICD-11 EE3Z。光是「誰是 DFU 病人」就有五種寫法。

她安裝 OHDSI ATLAS，把所有醫院 ETL 進 OMOP CDM 5.4，用 Athena 把雜亂代碼全映射到 SNOMED 33334006；E 醫院的自由文字用 cTAKES 抽出 phenotype。三週後，她跑 Kaplan-Meier + Cox PH，得到風險比 HR=0.72 (95% CI 0.61–0.85)：穿 total-contact cast 的病人，一年截肢率下降 28%。論文上了 Diabetes Care。OMOP 不是學術玩具——是讓不同醫院的書能被一起讀的通用譯本。

Maria is a health informaticist. She wants to combine 5 hospitals — 12,400 patients with diabetic foot ulcers (DFU) — to compare amputation rates and long-term survival. Day one she nearly cries: Hospital A uses L97.519, B writes E11.621 + L97.5, C has free text "R foot cellulitis c/o DM," D is still on ICD-9 707.14, E upgraded to ICD-11 EE3Z. Defining "who has DFU" has five different answers.

She installs OHDSI ATLAS, ETLs each site into OMOP CDM 5.4, uses Athena to map every messy code to SNOMED 33334006; Hospital E's narratives go through cTAKES NLP. Three weeks later Kaplan-Meier + Cox PH gives hazard ratio HR=0.72 (95% CI 0.61–0.85): patients in total-contact casts have a 28% lower amputation rate at one year. The paper lands in Diabetes Care. OMOP is not academic theater — it is the universal translator that lets the five hospitals' books be read as one.

KM 畫出「存活機率隨時間的階梯」；Cox 告訴你「治療讓風險變成幾倍」。

Event hasn't happened by study end = censored; another outcome happens first = competing risk.

概覽

EHR 資料的雙重結構

電子病歷 (EHR) 同時包含結構化 (診斷、用藥、檢驗、生命徵象) 與非結構化 (出院摘要、影像報告、護理紀錄) 兩類資料。前者用標準術語 (ICD-9/10/11、CPT、LOINC、RxNorm、SNOMED CT) 編碼，後者需 NLP 萃取。任何一個臨床問題的探勘——再住院預測、敗血症早期警示、藥物副作用偵測——都必須先把這兩端整合成「分析就緒」資料表。

大型公開資料集 (MIMIC-IV 2023、eICU、All of Us、N3C) 與通用資料模型 (OMOP CDM 5.4、Sentinel CDM、PCORnet CDM) 讓跨機構分析變得可能；但 缺失非隨機 (MNAR)、編碼漂移 (code drift)、記錄者偏差 (charting bias) 是每個 EHR 探勘專案都得正面處理的三大陷阱。

EHR contains both structured (diagnoses, meds, labs, vitals) and unstructured (discharge summaries, imaging reports, nursing notes) data. The first uses standardized terminologies (ICD-9/10/11, CPT, LOINC, RxNorm, SNOMED CT); the second requires NLP extraction. Any clinical mining problem — readmission prediction, sepsis early warning, ADR surveillance — must first merge both sides into an analysis-ready table.

Large open resources (MIMIC-IV 2023, eICU, All of Us, N3C) and common data models (OMOP CDM 5.4, Sentinel CDM, PCORnet CDM) enable cross-institution analytics; but MNAR missingness, code drift, and charting bias are the three traps every EHR mining project must confront.

🚨
Charting bias：「沒有記錄 ≠ 沒有發生」。一個 ICU 病人沒有 GCS 紀錄，往往代表他清醒，而不是缺失。直接 mean-impute 會嚴重失真。 Charting bias: "Not charted ≠ did not happen." A missing GCS in an ICU patient often means alert, not missing. Naive mean-imputation will badly distort signal.

比喻

EHR 探勘 = 多語圖書館的考古

每家醫院的 EHR 就像一間用不同方言寫成的圖書館——同一個診斷可能寫在 ICD-9、ICD-10、ICD-11、SNOMED、自由文字註記，甚至只是一個藥單的線索。OMOP CDM 是「萬國音標」：先把每個方言映到 Athena vocabulary 的同一個 concept_id，所有書才讀得一樣；然後分析師才能像在同一張地圖上挖寶。

Each hospital's EHR is a library written in a different dialect — the same diagnosis may live in ICD-9, ICD-10, ICD-11, SNOMED, a free-text note, or just a hint in a prescription. OMOP CDM is the universal phonetic alphabet: every dialect maps to one Athena concept_id, so every book reads alike — only then can the analyst dig on the same map.

三、Computable Phenotype

表型 (phenotype) = 把「這個病人是不是 type 2 diabetes」的臨床定義轉成「在 EHR 上可以執行的查詢」。PheKB 與 eMERGE Network 提供經驗證、跨機構通用的演算法庫；OHDSI ATLAS 則讓你用 cohort definition JSON 跨網絡執行。

典型 T2DM 表型：(2 次門診 ICD-10 E11.* > 30 天) OR (1 次住院 E11.*) OR (HbA1c ≥ 6.5%) OR (處方降血糖藥) — 並排除 type 1 與妊娠糖尿病。

A phenotype turns the clinical definition of "this patient has type 2 diabetes" into an executable EHR query. PheKB and the eMERGE Network publish validated, cross-institution algorithm libraries; OHDSI ATLAS lets you run cohort-definition JSON across a network.

Canonical T2DM phenotype: (2 outpatient ICD-10 E11.* > 30 days apart) OR (1 inpatient E11.*) OR (HbA1c ≥ 6.5%) OR (antihyperglycemic prescription) — excluding type 1 and gestational diabetes.

完整實例

心衰 30 天再住院預測 (Step-by-Step)

HF

從原始 EHR 到 AUROC 0.74 模型

目標：30-day readmission
Step 1
定義 cohort：從 OMOP condition_occurrence 抽取 condition_concept_id ∈ {4329847 (CHF), 4127295 (HFrEF), 4023479 (HFpEF)}，限定 2018-01-01 ~ 2024-12-31，且具至少 365 天 observation_period。結果：n = 18,432 住院。
Define cohort: from OMOP condition_occurrence extract condition_concept_id ∈ {4329847 (CHF), 4127295 (HFrEF), 4023479 (HFpEF)}, restricted to 2018-01-01 to 2024-12-31, with ≥365-day observation_period. Result: n = 18,432 admissions.
Step 2
定 index admission：每位病人取「第一次以心衰為主診斷的住院」；排除轉院 (transfer) 及計畫性住院 (planned)。
Index admission: first hospitalization with HF as primary diagnosis per patient; exclude transfers and planned admissions.
Step 3
建立 outcome：look-ahead 0–30 天內任何 acute readmission（OMOP visit_occurrence.visit_type=ER + Inpatient 且 primary HF concept_id 命中），positive rate ≈ 18.4%。
Build outcome: any acute readmission within 0–30 days (OMOP visit_occurrence.visit_type=ER + Inpatient with primary HF concept_id), positive rate ≈ 18.4%.
Step 4
特徵工程：(a) Charlson Comorbidity Index (CCI) — 用過去 1 年的 OMOP condition；(b) 年齡、性別、保險、SDoH (z-code)；(c) LOS、ICU stay、past-year admissions；(d) admission lab values (BNP, creatinine, sodium)；(e) 出院用藥 (beta-blocker, ACEi/ARB, MRA, SGLT2i — 2024 ESC guideline) 的指標變項；(f) 出院後 7-day follow-up 安排 (boolean)。共 142 個 features。
Feature engineering: (a) Charlson Comorbidity Index (CCI) over the past year; (b) age, sex, insurance, SDoH (z-codes); (c) LOS, ICU stay, past-year admissions; (d) admission labs (BNP, creatinine, sodium); (e) discharge med indicators (beta-blocker, ACEi/ARB, MRA, SGLT2i per 2024 ESC); (f) 7-day post-discharge follow-up booked. Total: 142 features.
Step 5
切分：絕對不要 random split。Group-K-fold by hospital_id（5 折）+ time-based hold-out (2024)。理由：同一家醫院的病人不獨立（同一群醫師、護理流程、編碼員）。
Split: never random-split. Use group-K-fold by hospital_id (5-fold) + temporal hold-out (2024). Reason: patients within a hospital are non-independent (same physicians, nursing protocols, coders).
Step 6
三個基準模型：(i) Cox PH (time-to-readmission)；(ii) logistic regression + LASSO；(iii) XGBoost + SHAP。XGBoost 訓練：max_depth=6, learning_rate=0.05, n_estimators=500, early_stopping=20。
Three baselines: (i) Cox PH (time-to-readmission); (ii) logistic regression + LASSO; (iii) XGBoost + SHAP. XGBoost: max_depth=6, learning_rate=0.05, n_estimators=500, early_stopping=20.
Step 7
驗證 PH 假設：對 Cox 模型畫 Schoenfeld residual vs time，cox.zph() p > 0.05 通過；對年齡用 tt() 做 time-varying coefficient。
Validate PH assumption: Schoenfeld residuals vs time, cox.zph() p > 0.05; age modeled with time-varying coefficient via tt().
Step 8
結果：2024 hold-out AUROC = 0.74 (95% CI 0.71–0.77)；AUPRC 0.36 (base rate 0.18 → 2× lift)；C-statistic (Cox) 0.71；Brier 0.13；calibration slope 0.96。SHAP top features：past-year admissions、BNP、無 7-day follow-up、SGLT2i absent。

🔬 進階：為何不能用 LOS 當特徵？

住院長度 (LOS) 看似強預測因子，但它常常反映了 outcome 本身：複雜病情 → 長 LOS → 也更可能再住院。更危險的是，許多 EHR 系統的 length_of_stay 欄位在出院前還會被續更新——你可能在不知情下把未來資訊放進訓練集（target leakage）。安全做法：只用入院後前 24/48 小時內可觀測到的 LOS proxy（如 GCS at 24h、ICU admission flag、creatinine peak）。

Length-of-stay (LOS) looks like a strong predictor but often reflects the outcome itself: complex cases → long LOS → also more likely to be readmitted. Worse, many EHRs update length_of_stay until discharge — you may unknowingly leak the future into training. Safe alternative: use only LOS proxies observable in the first 24/48 hours (GCS at 24h, ICU admission flag, creatinine peak).

2018 Duke Sepsis Watch — 早 5 小時的代價

Duke deployed Sepsis Watch in 2018: a deep-learning model over EHR vital-sign streams, alerting ~5 hours earlier and used in 200+ rapid-response interventions. But a 2022 multi-site validation showed AUROC fell from 0.87 → 0.71 at new sites (different vitals-charting conventions), plus drift inside Duke during COVID-19's altered admission flow.

爭論 1

OMOP CDM vs FHIR：到底哪一個贏？

FHIR 是 HL7 的互通標準（傳輸 / API）；OMOP 是 OHDSI 的分析 schema。研究人員爭論：為了臨床決策應該以 FHIR 為基底建模，還是先 ETL 到 OMOP 跑跨機構研究？
FHIR is HL7's interoperability standard (transport / API); OMOP is OHDSI's analytics schema. Researchers argue: should clinical decision-support live on FHIR directly, or be ETL'd into OMOP first?
支持 FHIR：即時、與 EHR 系統原生整合、有 SMART on FHIR app ecosystem，適合 CDS Hooks 觸發。
Pro-FHIR: real-time, EHR-native, SMART on FHIR app ecosystem, ideal for CDS Hooks.
支持 OMOP：跨機構聯邦分析、固定 schema 不會隨 vendor 飄、Athena 詞彙統一、OHDSI 工具鏈完整 (ATLAS, ACHILLES, PatientLevelPrediction)。

爭論 2

表型可否真正可重現？

同一個 PheKB T2DM 表型，在 5 家醫院跑出的 PPV 在不同論文落於 79%–94%。是表型不夠精確，還是底層編碼實務不同？2024 Khera et al. JAMIA 發現：即使 OMOP 標準化後，concept_id 命中率仍有 15–20% 跨機構差異。

改善路線：(1) phenotype + chart-review feedback loop；(2) 用 Clinical-Longformer 對自由文字補強；(3) phecode 2.0 (2024) 對 ICD-10-CM 做更穩定的群組化。

爭論 3

可解釋是否阻礙了好模型？

部分學者主張：醫療場景應放棄黑盒，固守 interpretable models (LR, GAMs, EBM)；另一派則主張 SHAP/LIME + post-hoc explanation 足夠，且強加可解釋會犧牲精度。Rudin 2019 Nat MI 經典論文「停止解釋黑盒、改用本來就可解釋的模型」與 2024 NeurIPS workshops 仍在辯。
Some argue medicine must forbid black-boxes (use LR, GAMs, EBM); others argue SHAP/LIME post-hoc is enough and forced interpretability sacrifices accuracy. Rudin's 2019 Nat MI manifesto "Stop explaining black boxes, use inherently interpretable models" is still debated at NeurIPS 2024 workshops.
陷阱

五個 EHR 探勘的常見陷阱

假設 ICD 碼準確

ICD-10 在許多診斷的 sensitivity ≈ 80%，但 specificity 變動更大；糖尿病 ~92%，但精神病、罕病可低於 60%。記得做 chart-review PPV。

ICD-10 sensitivity is often ≈ 80%; diabetes ~92% but psychiatric/rare diseases can be <60%. Always validate with chart-review PPV.

未做 group-CV by 醫院

Random K-fold 會洩漏：同一個醫師、同一個編碼員、同一個 EHR 模板會把訓練集和測試集綁在一起。一定要 GroupKFold(groups=hospital_id) 或 StratifiedGroupKFold。

Random K-fold leaks: same physician, coder, EHR template tie train and test together. Use GroupKFold(groups=hospital_id) or StratifiedGroupKFold.

忽略 informative censoring

Cox / KM 假設 censoring 與事件獨立。但「病人因病況惡化轉院」會被當成 censored，這個假設立刻破功。檢查 Schoenfeld residuals + IPCW (inverse probability of censoring weights)。

Cox/KM assume censoring independent of event. "Transfer due to deterioration" treated as censored breaks this. Check Schoenfeld residuals + use IPCW (inverse probability of censoring weights).

把 LOS 當特徵 → outcome leakage

LOS 是「出院後才知道」的變項；放進 readmission 模型等於把答案抄到題目。只能用 admission window 內可觀測的代理。

LOS is only known at discharge; using it in a readmission model copies the answer onto the question. Use only admission-window proxies.

PH 不成立還跑 Cox

如果某共變數的 effect 隨時間改變（如治療效果初期強、後期消退），Cox 報出來的 HR 會誤導。要先 cox.zph()，必要時改 time-varying coefficient、stratified Cox、或 AFT 模型。

If a covariate's effect changes over time (e.g., early treatment effect fading), Cox HR misleads. Run cox.zph() first; switch to time-varying coefficient, stratified Cox, or AFT models.

忽略 SDoH

缺少保險、住址、ZIP-level 社經因子，可能讓模型在弱勢族群表現變差。OMOP 5.4 加入 SDoH domain；ICD-10 Z55–Z65 系列也別忽略。

Without insurance, address, ZIP-level socioeconomic factors, models perform worse on underserved groups. OMOP 5.4 added an SDoH domain; ICD-10 Z55–Z65 codes also count.

常見錯誤陷阱 / Common Pitfalls

症狀：把 ICD-10 當成 ground truth label，但很多疾病 ICD 敏感度僅 ~80%（敗血症甚至更低）。修正：用 PheKB / eMERGE 已驗證的可計算表型；高風險研究結合 chart review 抽樣校驗。

Symptom: treating ICD-10 as ground-truth labels; many conditions have only ~80% sensitivity (sepsis even lower). Fix: use PheKB / eMERGE validated computable phenotypes; for high-stakes studies, sample chart-review for calibration.

症狀：同一病人多次就診出現在 train 與 test，導致內部 AUROC 虛高，外部 drop 0.2+。修正：用 GroupShuffleSplit 或 GroupKFold 按 patient_id（甚至 hospital_id）分群；報告外部 (external) validation。

Symptom: same patient appears in both train and test; internal AUROC inflates, external drops 0.2+. Fix: GroupShuffleSplit / GroupKFold on patient_id (or hospital_id); always report external validation.

症狀：失訪 (lost to follow-up) 不是隨機的——病情惡化的病人更可能轉院。視為「censoring at random」會嚴重低估事件率。修正：敏感性分析（worst-case / IPCW），或用 multi-state model。

Symptom: loss-to-follow-up is non-random — sicker patients transfer out. Assuming "censoring at random" underestimates event rates. Fix: sensitivity analysis (worst-case / IPCW) or multi-state model.

症狀：用「住院天數 (LOS)」預測「30 天死亡」——LOS 本身已經包含結果信號（死掉的人 LOS 較短或更長）。修正：只用 index time（入院第 24 小時）以前可得的特徵；明確定義時間窗口。

Symptom: using length-of-stay to predict 30-day mortality — LOS already encodes outcome signal. Fix: only use features available before the index time (e.g., first 24h of admission); precisely define the time window.

症狀：Kaplan-Meier 曲線交叉、Schoenfeld 殘差 p<0.05，卻仍報告單一 HR。修正：檢查 PH 假設（cox.zph）；違反時用 time-varying coefficients、stratified Cox、或 Fine-Gray (競爭風險) / AFT 模型。

Symptom: KM curves cross, Schoenfeld p<0.05, yet a single HR is still reported. Fix: check PH (cox.zph); use time-varying coefficients, stratified Cox, or Fine-Gray / AFT when violated.

症狀：Epic Sepsis Watch 部署兩年後 AUROC 從 0.76 (廠商宣稱) 降到 0.63（Wong 2021）——病人組成、編碼習慣、臨床流程都在漂移。修正：建立 monitoring 儀表板，週/月度報告 PPV / sensitivity / alert burden；FDA PCCP 預設變更控制計畫。

Symptom: Epic Sepsis Watch dropped from vendor-claimed 0.76 to 0.63 AUROC after deployment (Wong 2021) — patient mix, coding habits and workflow drift. Fix: monitoring dashboard reporting weekly/monthly PPV / sensitivity / alert burden; FDA PCCP for pre-specified change control.

DEBATE

在 2026 年，研究團隊要選 OMOP CDM 還是 FHIR 作為 EHR 資料整合的核心？

觀點三（折衷）： 混合 — 2025+ 多數現代專案兩者並用：OMOP 做分析、FHIR 做交付。ETL 工具（如 OHDSI's FHIR2OMOP）讓兩者可互轉。Hybrid — most modern 2025+ projects use both: OMOP for analytics, FHIR for delivery. ETL tools (e.g., OHDSI's FHIR2OMOP) bridge them.

CASE STUDY

2018 年 Duke University Hospital 部署 Sepsis Watch（基於 LSTM 的早期敗血症預警系統），用於 200+ 例病人介入。最初內部報告 AUROC 0.94，宣稱能在發病前 ~5 小時偵測敗血症。18 個月後外部驗證揭露：AUROC 僅 0.63、敏感度 33%、警報負擔達 18% 病人（Wong et al. 2021 JAMA Intern Med）。

解剖失敗原因：(1) 病人未分組的隨機 k-fold CV；(2) 事後 threshold 最佳化；(3) 沒有時間外部驗證；(4) 訓練資料的 label 來自事後 chart review，部署時的 label 來自 ICD billing——兩者分佈不同。這個案例催生了 FDA 對 SaMD + PCCP 的明確規範，也是每一篇 TRIPOD-AI 教科書必引的範例。

In 2018 Duke University Hospital deployed Sepsis Watch (an LSTM-based early-warning system) to 200+ patient interventions. Initial internal report: AUROC 0.94, predicting sepsis ~5h before onset. 18 months later external validation revealed: AUROC just 0.63, sensitivity 33%, alert burden 18% of patients (Wong et al. 2021 JAMA Intern Med).

Autopsy of the failure: (1) random k-fold CV without patient grouping; (2) post-hoc threshold optimization; (3) no temporal validation; (4) training labels came from retrospective chart review while deployment labels came from ICD billing — different distributions. This case drove FDA's explicit SaMD + PCCP rules and is cited in every TRIPOD-AI textbook.

因果推論：MedRECord toolchain 整合 g-computation / IPW / TMLE / Target Trial Emulation (Hernán)；2025 JAMA 多篇用 target trial 取代不可行 RCT。

多模態：2026 PaLM-Med、Med-Gemini、Mistral-Med 把 EHR 結構化資料 + 影像 + 臨床 NLP（BioGPT + UMLS linker）整合於同一模型；BioGPT 的 UMLS linker 可將自由文本對應到 CUI，解決同義詞與縮寫問題。

Multimodal: 2026 PaLM-Med, Med-Gemini, Mistral-Med unify structured EHR + imaging + clinical NLP (BioGPT + UMLS linker); the UMLS linker maps free text to CUIs, solving synonym/abbreviation problems.

歷史小故事：1980 年代 — Larry Weed 醫師在佛蒙特大學 (University of Vermont) 發明「問題導向病歷」(Problem-Oriented Medical Record, POMR)，也就是今天的 SOAP note 結構：Subjective（主觀）/ Objective（客觀）/ Assessment（評估）/ Plan（計畫）。早在 1969 年他就預言「電腦終將改變醫療紀錄」，當時被當笑話。40 年後，美國每一間使用 Epic 或 Cerner 的醫院都用 SOAP note 結構。Weed 2017 年過世，未能親眼見證他完整的願景——但他發明的這個結構，仍在每一份現代電子病歷中悄悄運作。額外一筆：1958 年 Edward Kaplan 與 Paul Meier 各自獨立投稿 JASA 同主題論文，編輯 William Madow 將兩篇合併，成就今日 72,000+ 引用的 Kaplan-Meier 估計式。1980s — Dr. Larry Weed at the University of Vermont invented the "Problem-Oriented Medical Record" (POMR), the SOAP note structure: Subjective, Objective, Assessment, Plan. As early as 1969 he predicted "computers will transform medical records" — people laughed. 40 years later every Epic and Cerner instance in the US uses SOAP notes. Weed died in 2017 without seeing his full vision realized — yet the structure he invented quietly powers every modern EHR. Footnote: in 1958 Edward Kaplan and Paul Meier independently submitted JASA papers on the same topic; editor William Madow merged them into the 72,000+ cited Kaplan-Meier estimator.
