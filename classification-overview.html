分類概論：KNN 與 Logistic Regression

分類是監督式學習的基石。從最直觀的 KNN「物以類聚」，到 Logistic Regression 的機率輸出與可解釋係數——兩者分別代表 lazy learner 與 parametric model 的兩端。

Classification is the cornerstone of supervised learning. From the intuitive "birds of a feather" KNN to Logistic Regression's probabilistic outputs and interpretable coefficients — they represent the two ends of the lazy-vs-parametric spectrum.

開場故事
情境劇

放射科的 14:37

下午兩點半，林醫師盯著 LDCT 第 217 張切片：左上葉一顆 7 mm 結節，邊緣分葉、實心 65%、密度 −180 HU。她按下按鈕，AI 助手吐出兩個獨立答案——KNN 模組看遍歷史 12 萬顆結節，找出最像的 7 顆鄰居 (其中 5 顆惡性) → P(惡)=0.71；Logistic Regression 把這顆結節塞進線性方程，輸出 P(惡)=0.43，並列出每個係數的勝算比 (OR=1.8 / 0.9 / 2.4 / …)。

誰對？她兩個都不能盲信。KNN 沒模型可審，無法解釋為什麼那 7 顆鄰居最像；Logistic Regression 給了人話般的係數，卻假設了線性。林醫師最後把兩個機率「平均後再校準」，並轉診——這就是本章要學的：何時相信「鄰居投票」、何時相信「公式」、何時兩個都不能只看一個。

It's 2:37 PM. Dr Lin stares at slice 217 of an LDCT — left upper lobe, 7 mm nodule, lobulated edges, 65% solid, −180 HU. She clicks the AI button. Two independent answers pop up: a KNN module rifles through 120,000 historical nodules, finds the 7 most similar (5 were malignant) → P(malignant)=0.71. A Logistic Regression module dumps the nodule into a linear equation and outputs P(malignant)=0.43, with odds ratios for every feature (OR=1.8 / 0.9 / 2.4 / …).

Who's right? She can't blindly trust either. KNN has no model to audit — there's no explanation for why those 7 neighbours were closest. Logistic Regression hands her human-readable coefficients but assumes linearity. Dr Lin ends up averaging the two probabilities, recalibrating, and ordering a follow-up — exactly what this chapter is about: when to trust the "neighbour vote", when to trust the "formula", and when neither single number is enough.

關鍵詞 · 雙版本

Classification
分類
把東西分到不同的盒子裡——蘋果或橘子。
Putting things into labelled boxes — apple or orange.
監督式學習中，學一個函數 f: X→{0,…,K-1}，將特徵向量映射到離散標籤。
Supervised learning of f: X→{0,…,K-1}, mapping feature vectors to discrete labels.
KNN
K 最近鄰
問你周圍最近的 5 個朋友穿什麼，跟著穿。
Ask your 5 closest friends what they're wearing, copy them.
非參數惰性學習器：預測時對訓練資料做 k-NN 查詢，回傳多數類別或加權平均。
Non-parametric lazy learner: at predict time, query the training set for k nearest and return majority vote or weighted avg.
K (hyperparameter)
鄰居數 K
要問幾個朋友？1 個太極端，100 個太混雜。
How many friends to ask? 1 = too extreme, 100 = too muddled.
控制 bias-variance 取捨；常用 √n 作起點再以 CV 校準。多數投票需 K 為奇數避免平票。
Controls bias-variance trade-off; rule of thumb K≈√n then refine via CV. Choose odd K to avoid ties in binary.
Decision Boundary
決策邊界
畫一條線分開蘋果和橘子的地圖。
A line on the map separating apple-land from orange-land.
特徵空間中分類器預測類別發生切換的等高線/曲面 {x : P(y=1|x)=0.5}。
The hyper-surface {x : P(y=1|x)=0.5} where the classifier flips its predicted class.
Logistic Regression
邏輯迴歸
不是迴歸——是用一條 S 形曲線把分數變成「機率」。
Not really regression — uses an S-curve to turn scores into probabilities.
線性 log-odds 模型 log(p/(1-p))=β₀+β·x，以 MLE 估參，輸出校準 (Platt-able) 機率。
Linear log-odds model log(p/(1-p))=β₀+β·x, fit by MLE, outputs (Platt-calibratable) probabilities.
Sigmoid / Logit
Sigmoid / Logit
把任何數字壓成 0 到 1 之間的 S 形曲線。
Squashes any number into a smooth S between 0 and 1.
σ(z)=1/(1+e−z)；logit=σ⁻¹=ln(p/(1-p))，將機率單調對應到全實數軸。
σ(z)=1/(1+e−z); logit=σ⁻¹=ln(p/(1-p)) monotonically maps prob to ℝ.
Odds Ratio (OR)
勝算比
多吃一根香蕉，得病機率變成原本的多少倍？
Eat one more banana → how many times more likely to get sick?
eβj：xj 增 1 單位導致勝算 (odds=p/(1-p)) 之乘法因子。OR=1 即無效應。
eβj: multiplicative factor on odds (p/(1-p)) per unit increase in xj. OR=1 means no effect.
Log-Likelihood
對數似然
這套參數能解釋資料的「分數」加起來。
A "score" of how well your parameters explain the data, summed up.
ℓ(β)=Σ[yi·zi−log(1+ezi)]，凸函數；MLE 透過 Newton-Raphson 或 L-BFGS 求最大。
ℓ(β)=Σ[yi·zi−log(1+ezi)], convex; MLE solved via Newton-Raphson or L-BFGS.
L1 / LASSO
L1 / LASSO
逼模型把不重要的特徵變成 0——「斷捨離」。
Forces unimportant features to exactly 0 — Marie Kondo for models.
λΣ|βj| 懲罰；非平滑導致解在角點 (βj=0)。Tibshirani 1996 JRSS-B 提出。
λΣ|βj| penalty; non-smooth → corner solutions (βj=0). Tibshirani 1996 JRSS-B.
L2 / Ridge
L2 / Ridge
把所有係數一起縮小，但不讓任何一個歸零。
Shrinks all coefficients together but doesn't kill any.
λΣβj² 懲罰；解析解 (XᵀX+λI)⁻¹Xᵀy；處理多重共線。Hoerl-Kennard 1970。
λΣβj² penalty; closed form (XᵀX+λI)⁻¹Xᵀy; tames multicollinearity. Hoerl-Kennard 1970.
Softmax
Softmax
把 K 個分數變成 K 個總和為 1 的機率。
Turns K scores into K probabilities that sum to 1.
p(y=k|x) = ezk/Σjezj，多元 Logistic Regression 的鏈接函數。
p(y=k|x) = ezk/Σjezj, the link of multinomial logistic regression.
Lazy vs Eager Learner
惰性 vs 急切學習器
惰性：考試才翻書；急切：每天讀，考試秒答。
Lazy: cram at exam. Eager: studies daily, answers instantly.
KNN=lazy (訓練 O(1) 預測 O(n)；Logistic=eager (訓練 O(n·p²) 預測 O(p))。
KNN=lazy (train O(1), predict O(n)); Logistic=eager (train O(n·p²), predict O(p)).

類比

朋友票決 vs 鏡子公式

KNN 像「早上不知道穿什麼，跑去問離你最近 5 個朋友——3 個說牛仔褲，那就牛仔褲」。沒有公式、沒有記憶、只有當下查表。Logistic Regression 像「你心裡已經有一條公式：天氣 ×0.4 + 場合 ×0.8 - 0.3，丟進 sigmoid 就得到答案」。一個是查資料庫，一個是背公式——延伸到分類器，就是 lazy vs eager 的根本哲學分歧。

KNN is like "I don't know what to wear — let me ask my 5 closest friends. 3 say jeans, jeans it is." No formula, no memory, just a lookup right now. Logistic Regression is like "I have a formula in my head: weather ×0.4 + occasion ×0.8 − 0.3, run sigmoid, out comes the answer." One queries the database, the other recites a formula — the lazy-vs-eager philosophical split, applied to classifiers.

手算範例

手算 KNN ↔ Logistic Regression

1

UCI Wisconsin Breast Cancer

手算逐步
STEP 1
查詢病人：radius=15.0、texture=20.0、其他 28 個特徵省略。先做 StandardScaler：zradius=(15-14.13)/3.52=+0.247、ztexture=(20-19.29)/4.30=+0.165。沒 scaling 半徑(mm)會壓過 texture(灰階變異)。
Query patient: radius=15.0, texture=20.0, 28 other features omitted. First StandardScaler: zradius=(15-14.13)/3.52=+0.247, ztexture=(20-19.29)/4.30=+0.165. Without scaling, radius (mm) would swamp texture (greylevel SD).
STEP 2
選 K=5：√569≈23 是粗估；先用 5 並交叉驗證決定。一定要 odd 避免二元分類平票。
Pick K=5: √569≈23 is a rough rule of thumb; start at 5 then refine with CV. Must be odd in binary tasks to avoid ties.
STEP 3
計算 569 個歐式距離：d(query, patienti) = √Σ(zj,query − zj,i)²。複雜度 O(n·p) = 569×30 ≈ 17k 次浮點運算——KNN 預測慢就慢在這裡。
Compute 569 Euclidean distances: d(query, patienti) = √Σ(zj,query − zj,i)². Complexity O(n·p) = 569×30 ≈ 17k FLOPs — this is why KNN's prediction is slow.
STEP 4
取出最近 5 個：distances 排序，得到 {d=0.31 (M), 0.38 (M), 0.42 (B), 0.51 (M), 0.55 (M)}。投票 → 4M, 1B → 預測惡性。distance-weighted 版本：權重 1/d 給 M 加總 9.8、B 加總 2.4 → 仍是 M，但信心更高。
Take 5 nearest: sort, get {d=0.31 (M), 0.38 (M), 0.42 (B), 0.51 (M), 0.55 (M)}. Vote → 4M, 1B → predict malignant. Distance-weighted version: weight 1/d gives M-total 9.8, B-total 2.4 → still M, but higher confidence.
STEP 5
不確定性 (bootstrap)：對訓練集做 100 次 bootstrap 重抽，每次跑 KNN→得到 100 個機率估計。95% CI = [0.65, 0.92]。臨床上「點估 0.78、CI [0.65, 0.92]」遠比孤立的 0.78 有用。
Uncertainty (bootstrap): resample training 100×, run KNN each time → 100 probability estimates. 95% CI = [0.65, 0.92]. "Point 0.78, CI [0.65, 0.92]" is clinically far more useful than a lonely 0.78.
STEP 6
對照 Logistic Regression：同樣資料、L2 (C=1)。模型輸出 β₀ = −10.4、βradius=+0.83、βtexture=+0.32、βconcavity=+1.41。對此查詢 z=−10.4 + 0.83·0.247 + 0.32·0.165 + … = +1.86 → σ(1.86)=P(M)=0.87。
Compare Logistic: same data, L2 (C=1). Fitted β₀ = −10.4, βradius=+0.83, βtexture=+0.32, βconcavity=+1.41. For this query z=−10.4 + 0.83·0.247 + 0.32·0.165 + … = +1.86 → σ(1.86)=P(M)=0.87.
STEP 7
解讀 OR：e0.83=2.29——半徑每多一個標準差，惡性勝算約 ×2.3。這是論文 Table 1 寫法，KNN 給不了。
Interpret OR: e0.83=2.29 — each +1 SD in radius multiplies the malignancy odds by ≈2.3. This is the Table-1-of-the-paper output, which KNN cannot give.
STEP 8
校準檢查：對驗證集分 10 bin 畫 reliability diagram。Logistic 通常很校準 (Brier=0.05)，KNN majority-vote 容易離散階梯狀 → 用 Platt scaling 後 Brier 降到 0.07。
Calibration check: 10-bin reliability diagram on the holdout. Logistic is usually well-calibrated (Brier=0.05); KNN majority-vote tends to be stair-step → Platt scaling brings Brier to 0.07.
爭論題

三個值得吵的問題

爭論 1

KNN 何時打贏 Logistic Regression？

直覺上 KNN 比較土，但有研究顯示某些情境它就是會贏——什麼情境？

KNN feels "primitive" but it wins in specific scenarios — which ones?

觀點 A：當決策邊界嚴重非線性且區域複雜（多模態、簇分散），KNN 自動順應資料地形；Logistic 的單一線性平面無法捕捉，除非手動加大量交互特徵。When the boundary is strongly non-linear and locally complex (multi-modal, scattered clusters), KNN adapts to the local terrain; Logistic's single linear plane can't capture this without massive feature engineering.
觀點 B：中等樣本 (n≥1000)、低維 (p≤20)、且訓練資料覆蓋良好。高維時 curse of dimensionality 讓距離失去意義，KNN 立刻崩盤。Medium n (≥1000), low p (≤20), well-covered training space. In high-D the curse of dimensionality makes distances meaningless and KNN collapses.
觀點 C：當你不想做模型假設。Logistic 假設 log-odds 線性，違反時偏差大；KNN 沒這個假設。When you want zero model assumptions. Logistic assumes log-odds linearity; violate that and bias spikes. KNN makes none.
爭論 2

為什麼 L1 是稀疏的？

兩個都是「縮小係數」，為什麼只有 L1 會「壓到零」？

Both shrink coefficients — why does only L1 zero them out?

幾何直觀：L1 約束 |β₁|+|β₂|≤t 是菱形，等高線易在「尖角」（軸上）相切，產生 βj=0；L2 約束 β₁²+β₂²≤t 是圓，切點在曲面上幾乎不在軸。L1's |β₁|+|β₂|≤t is a diamond; contours tangent at its corners (on axes) → βj=0. L2's β₁²+β₂²≤t is a circle; tangent points almost never on axes.
次梯度視角：L1 在 βj=0 有「kink」(不可微)；KKT 條件要求 |∂loss/∂βj| ≤ λ 時 βj 可恰為 0。L2 處處可微，最小值內點解。L1 has a kink at βj=0 (non-smooth); KKT lets βj=0 whenever |∂loss/∂βj| ≤ λ. L2 is everywhere smooth → interior minimum.
爭論 3

Logistic 前需要 scaling 嗎？

數學上 MLE 不變，但實務上 sklearn 為什麼建議？

Mathematically MLE is scale-invariant, but sklearn still recommends scaling — why?

數學觀點：無正則化時 βj/scalej 不變，純預測機率相同。所以「報告 OR」時不 scaling 比較自然 (保留原始單位)。Without regularization βj/scalej is invariant; pure prediction is identical. Reporting OR is more natural without scaling (keeps native units).
實務觀點：L1/L2 懲罰對所有 β 一樣強——大尺度的特徵 (體重 kg vs 年齡 yr) 會被不公平處罰。使用任何正則化都應先 scaling。L1/L2 penalises every β equally — large-scale features (weight kg vs age yr) get unfairly penalised. Always scale when using any regularization.
收斂觀點：L-BFGS / Newton-Cholesky 在尺度差異大的特徵上條件數差，收斂慢——scaling 直接加速。L-BFGS / Newton-Cholesky have poor conditioning on mixed-scale features; scaling speeds up convergence dramatically.

實案聚光燈

Ardila 2019 Nature：CNN 擊敗放射科

Google Health 在 Nature Medicine (Ardila 2019, DOI:10.1038/s41591-019-0447-x) 提出一個端對端 3D-CNN 篩檢肺癌，AUROC=0.94，平均減少 11% 偽陽性，並在多中心 NLST 資料上勝過 6 位放射科醫師。但整個論文最關鍵的對照是一條老派的 Logistic Regression baseline——AUROC 仍有 0.91——意思是 CNN 多賺的那 0.03，要用 ×100 倍計算量、不可解釋、且須 GPU。

Google Health's 2019 Nature Medicine paper (Ardila et al., DOI:10.1038/s41591-019-0447-x) showed an end-to-end 3D-CNN screened lung cancer at AUROC=0.94, cutting false-positives 11%, beating 6 radiologists on the NLST cohort. But the most important comparison in the entire paper was an old-school Logistic Regression baseline — it still hit AUROC=0.91. The CNN's extra 0.03 came with 100× compute, zero interpretability, and a GPU requirement.

啟示：研究員的義務不是「跑出最高 AUROC」，而是「跑一條老 baseline 才知道你的新模型多賺了多少」。

Takeaway: your job as a researcher is not "highest AUROC" — it's "run the old baseline so you know how much new model actually buys you."

陷阱

六個常見陷阱

忘記 scaling 就用 KNN

收縮壓 (mmHg, 80–200) 跟年齡 (yr, 0–100) 同維歐式距離 → 血壓壓垮其他特徵。scale 永遠先做。

SBP (mmHg, 80–200) and age (yr, 0–100) on the same Euclidean axis → SBP dominates. Always scale first.

二元分類用偶數 K

K=4 時可能 2:2 平票，模型「擲硬幣」決定。二元任務 K 必須是奇數，或用 distance-weighted 投票。

K=4 can yield 2:2 ties; the model flips a coin. Use odd K in binary tasks, or distance-weighted voting.

把 ordinal 當 numeric

把「無 / 輕 / 重 = 0/1/2」當連續，KNN 計算「輕到重」的距離=1 等於「無到輕」=1，假設了等距性。用 one-hot 或設計好的 ordinal encoder。

Coding "none/mild/severe" as 0/1/2 assumes equal spacing in clinical severity — KNN reads "mild→severe"=1 same as "none→mild"=1. Use one-hot or a true ordinal encoder.

小 N 不正則化

N=50、p=20 跑無正則化 Logistic → 完美擬合訓練、驗證崩盤。小資料一律 L2 (C=0.1–1)。

N=50, p=20, unregularised logistic → perfect train fit, validation collapses. Always L2 (C=0.1–1) on small data.

不平衡資料只看 Accuracy

陽性 2% 時，全猜陰性 → 98% Accuracy、Recall=0、毫無臨床價值。用 PR-AUC、Recall@k 或 F-β (β=2)。

With 2% positive prevalence, "always negative" yields 98% Accuracy, Recall=0, zero clinical value. Use PR-AUC, Recall@k, F-β (β=2).

多元任務忘了切 multinomial

sklearn 預設 ovr (one-vs-rest)；對於互斥類別 (ICD chapter) 應切 multi_class='multinomial'，否則機率不加總到 1。

sklearn default is ovr; for mutually-exclusive classes (ICD chapter) set multi_class='multinomial' — otherwise probabilities don't sum to 1.

壞範例：KNN 直接套

KNeighborsClassifier(n_neighbors=5).fit(X_train, y_train)——沒 scaling、K 固定 5、用 accuracy 在 1:50 不平衡資料上 cross-validate，最後論文寫 "Accuracy = 97.8%"。三個錯：距離被大尺度特徵主導、K 沒做 CV、metric 撒謊。

KNeighborsClassifier(n_neighbors=5).fit(X_train, y_train) — no scaling, K hard-coded to 5, validated by accuracy on a 1:50 imbalanced set, paper reads "Accuracy = 97.8%". Three errors: distance dominated by large-scale features, K not CV-tuned, metric lies.

好範例：Pipeline + CV + Recall

Pipeline([("sc",StandardScaler()),("knn",KNeighborsClassifier(weights='distance'))]) 接 GridSearchCV 對 n_neighbors ∈ {1,3,…,25}，scoring='average_precision' 並 StratifiedKFold(5)。回報 PR-AUC、Recall@TopK、reliability diagram。

Wrap in Pipeline([("sc",StandardScaler()),("knn",KNeighborsClassifier(weights='distance'))]), run GridSearchCV over n_neighbors ∈ {1,3,…,25}, scoring='average_precision', StratifiedKFold(5). Report PR-AUC, Recall@TopK, reliability diagram.

進階研究員彩蛋

2024–2026 SOTA

Newton-Cholesky solver (sklearn 1.5+): 對多元 Logistic Regression，solver="newton-cholesky" 利用 Hessian 的 Cholesky 分解，在 n≥10k、p≤500 的中型表格資料上比 lbfgs 快 3–10×。當你的特徵矩陣是稠密實數時應為首選。

Newton-Cholesky solver (sklearn 1.5+): solver="newton-cholesky" uses a Cholesky factorisation of the Hessian; 3–10× faster than lbfgs on dense medium-size tabular data (n≥10k, p≤500). Should be your default for dense real-valued features.

🔍 Conformal Prediction

Conformal prediction (Vovk-Gammerman 2005、Angelopoulos-Bates 2023 review, arXiv:2107.07511) 是一個分布無關的不確定性量化框架。給 KNN 或 Logistic Regression 訓練好的模型加上一個 calibration set，就能保證 P(true label ∈ prediction set) ≥ 1 − α，沒有任何分布假設。

2024 sklearn-conformal 套件已提供 wrapper：from sklearn_conformal import SplitConformalClassifier; cc = SplitConformalClassifier(LogisticRegression(), confidence_level=0.9)。臨床部署日趨要求這類覆蓋保證。

Conformal prediction (Vovk-Gammerman 2005, Angelopoulos-Bates 2023 review, arXiv:2107.07511) is a distribution-free uncertainty framework. Wrap a trained KNN or Logistic with a calibration set and you get a guarantee: P(true label ∈ prediction set) ≥ 1 − α, no distribution assumption.

The 2024 sklearn-conformal package gives a one-liner: cc = SplitConformalClassifier(LogisticRegression(), confidence_level=0.9). Clinical deployments increasingly require this kind of coverage guarantee.

🔍 Hierarchical Softmax

當類別數 K 很大 (如 ICD-10 有 70k+ 細項)，標準 softmax 每次預測 O(K)。Hierarchical softmax 把類別組成樹狀結構，每次預測只需 O(log K)。2024 在 PubMed-BERT 多標籤標註上重新被引入 (Lee et al, JAMIA 2024)。

實作建議：from torch.nn import AdaptiveLogSoftmaxWithLoss 或自寫 huffman tree softmax。

For huge K (ICD-10 has 70k+ leaves), standard softmax costs O(K) per predict. Hierarchical softmax arranges classes into a tree → O(log K). Re-popularised in 2024 for PubMed-BERT multi-label tagging (Lee et al., JAMIA 2024).

Implementation: torch.nn.AdaptiveLogSoftmaxWithLoss or a hand-rolled Huffman-tree softmax.

🔍 Multiclass Brier Score

多元 Brier = (1/N) Σ Σk (p̂ik − 𝟙{yi=k})²。可分解為 reliability + resolution + uncertainty (Murphy 1973)。2025 sklearn-1.6 加入了 brier_score_loss(multioutput="raw") 支援。

對於多元 calibration，建議使用 temperature scaling (Guo et al, ICML 2017, arXiv:1706.04599)——只引入單一參數 T，softmax(z/T)，幾乎不影響 ranking 但顯著改善 ECE。

Multiclass Brier = (1/N) Σ Σk (p̂ik − 𝟙{yi=k})². Decomposes into reliability + resolution + uncertainty (Murphy 1973). sklearn 1.6 (2025) added brier_score_loss(multioutput="raw").

For multiclass calibration, prefer temperature scaling (Guo et al., ICML 2017, arXiv:1706.04599) — a single parameter T, softmax(z/T); ranking-preserving, dramatically improves ECE.

概覽

什麼是分類？

分類 (classification) 是給定特徵 X 預測離散標籤 y ∈ {0,1,…,K-1} 的監督式學習任務。二元分類（如糖尿病/非糖尿病）最常見，多元分類（如 ICD 章節）需要 softmax 或 one-vs-rest 策略。

本章聚焦兩個「入門即經典」的方法：KNN（無模型、依賴距離）與 Logistic Regression（線性參數模型、輸出機率）。它們分別示範了「記憶」與「歸納」兩種根本不同的學習哲學。

Classification is the supervised task of predicting a discrete label y ∈ {0,1,…,K-1} from features X. Binary tasks (e.g., diabetic vs not) are the most common; multiclass tasks (e.g., ICD chapter) require softmax or one-vs-rest strategies.

This chapter focuses on two "entry-level classics": KNN (non-parametric, distance-based) and Logistic Regression (parametric, probabilistic). They embody two fundamentally different learning philosophies — memorize vs generalize.

KNN

一、K-Nearest Neighbors

🧭

直覺

查詢點的類別 = 最近 K 個鄰居的多數投票。沒有「訓練」階段，模型 = 全部訓練資料。

The query label = majority vote among the K closest neighbors. No "training" phase — the model IS the training data.

🎯

K 的取捨

K 小 → 低偏差高變異（過擬合、易受雜訊影響）；K 大 → 高偏差低變異（過平滑）。常用 √n 或交叉驗證。

Small K → low bias, high variance (overfits noise); large K → high bias, low variance (oversmoothes). Use √n or CV.

⚠️

陷阱

必須先 scaling；高維時失效（curse of dimensionality）；類別不平衡時投票傾向多數類，需 weighted KNN。

Must scale first; fails in high-D (curse of dimensionality); class imbalance biases votes — use weighted KNN.

💡
距離加權：weighted KNN 將鄰居以 1/d 加權，比單純多數投票更穩健，sklearn 設 weights='distance' 即可。 Distance weighting: Weighted KNN votes by 1/d — more robust than plain majority. In sklearn, set weights='distance'.
互動模擬

KNN 決策邊界：拖動 K 觀察變化

在二維玩具資料上跑 KNN——K 小時邊界凹凸不平（過擬合），K 大時邊界平滑但會誤分稀有點。

Run KNN on a 2D toy dataset — small K gives jagged boundaries (overfit), large K oversmoothes and misclassifies rare points.

Logistic Regression

二、邏輯迴歸

線性迴歸輸出實數，但分類需要 [0,1] 機率。Logistic Regression 用 sigmoid σ(z) = 1/(1+e-z) 把線性組合 z = β₀ + β·x 壓到 [0,1]。對 odds 取對數即得線性形式：log(p/(1-p)) = β·x，因此係數 βj 的解釋是「xj 增加 1 單位，log-odds 增加 βj」或「odds 乘以 eβj」。

多元分類用 Softmax：p(y=k) = ezk / Σ ezj，等同於 K-way Logistic Regression。

Linear regression outputs real numbers, but classification needs probabilities in [0,1]. Logistic Regression uses the sigmoid σ(z) = 1/(1+e-z) to squash z = β₀ + β·x into [0,1]. Taking log of the odds gives a linear form: log(p/(1-p)) = β·x. Each βj means: "a unit increase in xj raises log-odds by βj" — i.e., multiplies the odds by eβj.

Multiclass uses Softmax: p(y=k) = ezk / Σ ezj — equivalent to K-way Logistic Regression.

正則化懲罰項效果何時用
L1 (Lasso)λΣ|βj|稀疏解高維特徵選擇
L2 (Ridge)λΣβj²縮小但不歸零多重共線
Elastic NetαL1 + (1-α)L2兼具兩者相關特徵成群
決策樹

三、KNN vs Logistic：如何選？

🌳 分類器選擇決策樹

Q1:
需要機率輸出 + 可解釋係數（臨床決策、論文 OR 表）？ → Logistic Regression
Q2:
決策邊界明顯非線性且樣本充足？ → KNN（或樹型，見 Ch 6）
Q3:
特徵高維且雜訊多（>100 維）？ → 避開 KNN；用 L1-Logistic 或樹
Q4:
類別不平衡（<5% 陽性）？ → class_weight='balanced' 或 SMOTE
Q5:
需要多元分類？ → Softmax / multinomial Logistic
Q1:
Need probability outputs + interpretable coefficients (clinical decisions, OR tables)? → Logistic Regression
Q2:
Decision boundary clearly nonlinear with enough samples? → KNN (or trees, Ch 6)
Q3:
High-D and noisy (>100 features)? → Avoid KNN; use L1-Logistic or trees
Q4:
Class imbalance (<5% positive)? → class_weight='balanced' or SMOTE
Q5:
Need multiclass? → Softmax / multinomial Logistic
⚠️
係數 ≠ 因果：Logistic 係數只是「相關」程度，未控制的混雜會讓 OR 偏移。臨床因果推論請用 Causal Inference (do-calculus, PSM, IPTW)。 Coefficient ≠ causation: Logistic coefficients are associations, not causal effects. Unmeasured confounders distort the OR. For causal claims use causal-inference frameworks (do-calculus, PSM, IPTW).
