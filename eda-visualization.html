故事化導讀

🎩 一位生物學家、四組資料、與一聲驚嘆

1973 年，劍橋大學統計學家 Francis Anscombe 在《The American Statistician》發表了一張看似不起眼的表格——四組 (x, y) 資料，每組 11 筆。表格下方列出敘述統計：四組的平均、變異數、相關係數、線性回歸線完全一致到小數點第二位。

當時許多生物學家、流行病學家正在大量發表「相關係數 r = 0.82」這類報告，預設 r 已經足以描述資料。Anscombe 沉默地附上四張散佈圖：第一張是「正常的線性關係」，第二張是「明顯的曲線」，第三張是「一個離群值拉動回歸線」，第四張是「所有 x 都等於 8，只有一個是 19」。

讀者翻到那一頁的瞬間——大概都倒抽了一口氣。Anscombe 在文末寫下名言："Make both calculations and graphs. Both sorts of output should be studied; each will contribute to understanding." 這四組資料後來被稱為 Anscombe's Quartet，是統計學家給世界的最重要禮物之一：絕對不要相信只有數字。

In 1973, Cambridge statistician Francis Anscombe published an unassuming table in The American Statistician: four datasets of 11 (x, y) pairs each. The descriptive statistics below — mean, variance, correlation, linear regression — were identical to two decimal places across all four.

At the time, biologists and epidemiologists were publishing "r = 0.82"-style reports en masse, assuming the correlation alone captured the relationship. Anscombe silently attached four scatterplots: the first showed a "normal linear" relationship, the second an obvious curve, the third a single outlier dragging the regression line, the fourth all x-values clustered at 8 except a lone 19.

The moment readers reached that page — they likely gasped. Anscombe closed with his famous line: "Make both calculations and graphs. Both sorts of output should be studied; each will contribute to understanding." Those four datasets — known forever as Anscombe's Quartet — are one of statistics' greatest gifts to the world: never trust numbers alone.

EDA 的四個目的

探索性資料分析 (Exploratory Data Analysis, EDA) 由 John Tukey 在 1977 年提出，主張資料分析的第一步應該是「看」而不是「測」。它的四個目的：① 理解資料分佈與規模 ② 找出異常值與資料品質問題 ③ 形成假設與特徵發現 ④ 為後續建模選擇合適的模型族。

下圖是著名的 Anscombe's Quartet 範例：四組資料具有完全相同的平均值、變異數、相關係數與線性迴歸線，但分佈完全不同。光看統計量會被誤導——唯有畫圖才能揭露真相。

Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA), coined by John Tukey in 1977, argues the first step of analysis should be to look, not to test. Four goals: ① understand distribution and scale ② surface outliers and quality issues ③ generate hypotheses and feature ideas ④ pre-select model families for modeling.

The famous Anscombe's Quartet shows four datasets with identical mean, variance, correlation, and regression line — yet completely different shapes. Numbers alone mislead; only plots reveal truth.

🩺
生活比喻

EDA 就像醫師的「視診 + 觸診」

沒有醫師會在病人一進診間就直接安排 MRI、CT、PET。他先看（臉色、姿勢、步態）、聽（呼吸、心音）、觸診（淋巴結、肝脾）——這些便宜、快速、無侵入性的「視覺檢查」往往就能定位 80% 的問題，貴重檢查才有指向性。EDA 就是資料的視診——便宜、快、能在 30 分鐘內告訴你 80% 該關心的問題。

No doctor orders MRI, CT, PET the moment a patient sits down. They first look (color, posture, gait), listen (breath, heart), palpate (lymph nodes, liver, spleen) — these cheap, fast, non-invasive "visual checks" already localize 80% of issues; expensive imaging is targeted. EDA is the visual exam of your data — cheap, fast, telling you 80% of what to worry about in 30 minutes.

圖型選擇

一、依資料類型選擇圖型

🔢 數值單變量

Histogram (整體分佈)、Boxplot (五數摘要 + outliers)、Violin (分佈密度 + 五數)、ECDF (累積分佈)、Q-Q plot (是否近似常態)。

Histogram (overall shape), Boxplot (5-number + outliers), Violin (density + 5-number), ECDF (cumulative), Q-Q plot (normality check).

🏷️ 類別單變量

Bar chart、Lollipop、Pareto chart。避免 3D 圓餅圖——人眼難以比較弧長。

Bar chart, Lollipop, Pareto chart. Avoid 3D pie charts — humans cannot compare arc lengths reliably.

🔗 雙變量（數值 × 數值）

Scatter (大量點時用 hexbin / 2D density)、LOESS 平滑曲線、聯合分佈圖。

Scatter (use hexbin / 2D density for many points), LOESS smoother, joint distribution plot.

🎭 雙變量（類別 × 數值）

Grouped boxplot / violin、Strip + jitter、Ridgeline plot；對小樣本顯示原始點。

Grouped boxplot/violin, strip + jitter, ridgeline plot; always show raw points for small N.

🌐 多變量

Pairplot (scatter matrix)、Correlation heatmap (色階 viridis)、Parallel coordinates、PCA biplot、UMAP/t-SNE。

Pairplot (scatter matrix), correlation heatmap (use viridis), parallel coordinates, PCA biplot, UMAP/t-SNE.

⏱️ 時序

Line + trend、Calendar heatmap、Seasonal decomposition (STL)、Spaghetti plot (多個體)。

Line + trend, calendar heatmap, seasonal decomposition (STL), spaghetti plot (multi-subject).

決策樹

二、視覺化設計的 7 大原則 (Tufte / Wilkinson)

🌳 圖表選擇決策樹

Q1:
你要呈現「比較」？ → 是 → 橫向 bar chart（避免 3D 圓餅）。
Q2:
你要呈現「分佈」？ → 是 → 小樣本 strip + violin；大樣本 histogram / density。
Q3:
你要呈現「關係」？ → 是 → 數值-數值用 scatter；分類-數值用 grouped box；分類-分類用 mosaic / heatmap。
Q4:
你要呈現「組成」？ → 是 → Stacked bar、treemap、sunburst；避免 3D。
Q5:
你要呈現「演化」？ → 是 → 時序：line + ribbon (CI)；多個體：spaghetti + median bold。
Q1:
Show "comparison"? → Yes → Horizontal bar chart (avoid 3D pies).
Q2:
Show "distribution"? → Yes → Small N: strip + violin; Large N: histogram / density.
Q3:
Show "relationship"? → Yes → Num-num: scatter; cat-num: grouped box; cat-cat: mosaic / heatmap.
Q4:
Show "composition"? → Yes → Stacked bar, treemap, sunburst; avoid 3D.
Q5:
Show "evolution"? → Yes → Time: line + CI ribbon; multi-subject: spaghetti + bold median.
⚠️
色彩規則：(1) 連續尺度用 viridis / cividis（色盲友善）；(2) 發散尺度用 RdBu；(3) 類別最多 7 色；(4) 紅綠對比避免（綠色色盲約 5%）。 Color rules: (1) Sequential — use viridis / cividis (CVD-safe); (2) Diverging — use RdBu; (3) Max 7 categorical colors; (4) Avoid red-green contrast (~5% red-green CVD).

思辨

三個值得爭論的問題

爭論 #1

相關性 (correlation) 何時重要、何時不重要？

「相關不是因果」是統計入門第一課——但相關到底有沒有用？
"Correlation is not causation" is Stats 101 — but is correlation actually useful?
觀點 A： 高 |r| 在 EDA 階段非常有用——它告訴你哪些特徵冗餘（candidate for removal）、哪些值得進一步探索。但發 paper 時不能宣稱因果。 High |r| is very useful at EDA — it flags redundant features (candidates for removal) and worth-exploring pairs. Just don't claim causation in the paper.
觀點 B： Pearson 只抓線性、Spearman 只抓單調；對非線性 (如 U 形、混沌動力) 完全失效——這時 mutual information / HSIC 比 correlation 重要得多。 Pearson only catches linear, Spearman only monotonic — both fail for nonlinear (U-shaped, chaotic). Mutual information / HSIC are far more important than correlation for these.
爭論 #2

Violin plot 真的比 boxplot 好嗎？

2020 年後 Nature/Cell 論文大量採用 SuperPlot（Lord 2020 JCB）取代傳統 boxplot——但 boxplot 並未過時。
Post-2020, Nature/Cell papers heavily adopt SuperPlots (Lord 2020 JCB) over boxplots — but boxplots aren't obsolete.
支持 Violin： 能揭露雙峰、長尾、不對稱——這些 boxplot 看不到。對大樣本特別重要。 Reveals bimodality, long tails, asymmetry — invisible in boxplots. Especially important for large samples.
支持 Boxplot： Violin 對小樣本 (N<30) 不可靠——KDE 在低資料下會「幻覺」多峰。Weissgerber 2015 PLOS Biology 主張小樣本必須 strip + jitter + boxplot 三件套。 Violin is unreliable for small N (<30) — KDE "hallucinates" peaks with little data. Weissgerber 2015 PLOS Biology argues small N must use strip + jitter + boxplot together.
爭論 #3

LLM-EDA 工具會取代資料分析師嗎？

2024–2026：ChatGPT Code Interpreter / Claude Data Analyst / Anthropic agents 都能「自動 EDA」並提出洞察。
2024–2026: ChatGPT Code Interpreter / Claude Data Analyst / Anthropic agents all do "auto-EDA" and produce insights.
支持自動化： 繁瑣的 univariate + correlation 報表完全可被自動產生；LLM 還能寫出可讀敘事，省下 50% 時間。 Tedious univariate + correlation reports can be fully automated; LLMs even produce readable narratives, saving 50% of time.
持保留態度： LLM 不懂領域語境——它不會看到「HbA1c 缺失與死亡相關」這種 MNAR 訊號；也不會質疑單位錯誤。EDA 是「資料對話」，需要人類好奇心。 LLMs lack domain context — they won't catch "HbA1c missing correlates with mortality" (MNAR signals) or question unit errors. EDA is "data dialogue" — it needs human curiosity.

經典案例

Challenger 太空梭 (1986)：糟糕的視覺化能造成災難

1986 年 1 月 28 日，Challenger 太空梭升空 73 秒後解體，7 名太空人喪生。事故根因是 O-ring 在低溫下失去彈性。發射前一晚，Morton Thiokol 工程師有完整資料——24 次先前發射的 O-ring 損傷記錄——但他們的圖表把沒有 O-ring 損傷的發射全部省略，只顯示有損傷的。結果，溫度與損傷的關係看起來像隨機。Edward Tufte 後來在《Visual Explanations》中重畫了同一份資料，但保留所有發射的點，並以溫度排序——關係立刻一目瞭然：低於 65°F 的發射全部有 O-ring 損傷。發射當天溫度 31°F——遠超出歷史經驗範圍。

On January 28, 1986, the Challenger shuttle broke apart 73 seconds after launch, killing 7 astronauts. Root cause: O-ring loss of elasticity at low temperature. The night before, Morton Thiokol engineers had complete data — 24 prior launches with O-ring damage records — but their charts omitted all launches without damage. The temperature-damage relationship looked random. Edward Tufte later, in Visual Explanations, re-drew the same data keeping every launch and sorting by temperature — the relationship was immediately obvious: every launch below 65°F suffered O-ring damage. Launch day: 31°F — far outside historical experience.

教訓：不是資料缺失導致 Challenger 災難，而是糟糕的視覺化。EDA 的責任不只是「看資料」，而是「以正確方式看資料」。

Lesson: Challenger wasn't lost to missing data — it was lost to bad visualization. EDA's responsibility isn't just "look at data" — it's "look at data the right way."

CASE STUDY

John Snow 1854：地圖打敗瘴氣論的那一夜

1854 年 8 月 31 日，倫敦 Soho 的 Broad Street 街口爆發霍亂——10 天內 500 人死亡。當時主流醫學界相信「瘴氣論」（壞空氣致病）。麻醉學家 John Snow 不接受這個假設，他做了現代資料探勘中第一個真正的「地理 EDA」：他在 Soho 街區地圖上，把每一個死亡病例畫成一個黑色長條。當圖完成時，所有人都看見了——所有死亡案例都集中在Broad Street 那口水井周圍。Snow 帶著地圖去見聖詹姆斯教區委員會，要求拆掉那口井的把手。把手拆掉後，疫情立刻止住。這張被稱為「Snow's Cholera Map」的圖表，是統計圖學史上最具影響力的單一視覺化——它不只救了命，還推動了流行病學、公共衛生工程、與現代地理資訊系統 (GIS) 的誕生。

On 31 August 1854, cholera erupted around Broad Street in London's Soho — 500 dead in 10 days. Mainstream medicine believed in miasma theory (bad air). Anaesthetist John Snow refused to accept it, and performed what may be the first real "geographic EDA" in data-mining history: on a Soho street map he marked every death as a black bar. When the chart was complete, everyone could see it — every death clustered around the Broad Street pump. Snow took the map to the St James parish council and demanded the pump handle be removed. Once removed, the outbreak stopped. Snow's Cholera Map is the single most influential visualization in the history of statistical graphics — it didn't just save lives, it gave birth to epidemiology, public-health engineering, and modern GIS.

啟示：Snow 沒有 p-value、沒有迴歸——只有一張正確的圖。EDA 不是「看圖找美感」，而是「找一張能讓所有人立刻同意的圖」。

Implication: Snow had no p-values and no regression — just the right chart. EDA isn't about finding a pretty picture; it's about finding the one chart everyone can agree on, at a glance.

陷阱

視覺化六大陷阱

① 3D 長條圖

PowerPoint 預設的「立體效果」會扭曲長度感知——同樣高度的柱看起來不一樣高。解：純 2D，使用 horizontal bar 對抗 cherry-picking。

PowerPoint's default "3D effect" distorts length perception — equal-height bars look unequal. Fix: stick to 2D; use horizontal bars to resist cherry-picking.

② 雙 y 軸濫用

把兩條趨勢「看起來相關」——靠調整 y 軸比例。實際統計可能毫不相關。解：用 small multiples 或標準化後同軸。

Making two trends "look correlated" by tweaking y-axis scales — actually unrelated statistics. Fix: small multiples or standardize and overlay on the same axis.

③ 長條圖省零點

長條圖必須從零開始——不從零會誇大差異。例：65% vs 68% 從 60 開始畫差三倍，從 0 開始差 5%。解：長條一律從 0；line chart 才可省。

Bar charts must start at zero — truncating exaggerates differences. e.g., 65% vs 68% looks 3× when starting at 60, 5% from 0. Fix: bars from 0 always; line charts may truncate.

④ Rainbow / Jet 色階

非感知均勻——同等差異在不同顏色段被視為不同大小。色盲也看不清。解：連續用 viridis；發散用 RdBu。

Not perceptually uniform — equal differences look unequal across color segments. Colorblind users fail. Fix: viridis for sequential; RdBu for diverging.

⑤ 沒做色盲檢測

紅綠色盲約佔男性 8%、女性 0.5%。紅綠對比根本看不見。解：Color Oracle / sim_daltonism 工具預檢；或直接用 Okabe-Ito palette。

~8% of men, 0.5% of women have red-green CVD. Red-green contrast is invisible to them. Fix: Color Oracle / sim_daltonism for preview; or use Okabe-Ito palette.

⑥ 切片過度 / p-hacking

看到資料時「先做 20 個子集」找有效應的那個——multiple comparison 之後 p 值就不可信。解：pre-register 你的子組分析；報告所有嘗試。

Slicing data into 20 subsets and finding the "significant" one — p-values become meaningless after multiple comparisons. Fix: pre-register subgroup analyses; report all attempts.

2024–2026 EDA 工具前沿

marimo + DuckDB (2024–2025)：marimo notebook 採用 reactive 模型——改一個 cell 自動傳遞下游；併入 DuckDB 後可以即時對 100 GB CSV 做 SQL EDA。取代了 Jupyter 的「重新跑全部」痛點。marimo.io。

marimo + DuckDB (2024–2025): marimo notebook is reactive — changing one cell auto-propagates downstream; embedded DuckDB enables instant SQL EDA on 100 GB CSVs. Replaces Jupyter's "re-run all" pain.

great_tables (Posit 2024)：R + Python 通用 publication-grade table 工具；支援 LaTeX、HTML、Word 輸出；與 Quarto 整合。終結了「貼到 Word 又跑版」的時代。

great_tables (Posit 2024): publication-grade table tool for R + Python; LaTeX/HTML/Word output; Quarto integration. Ends the "paste-to-Word and watch it explode" era.

lonboard for big-data (2024)：WebGL-加速地理空間 + 大規模散佈視覺化；可在瀏覽器內顯示 10M+ 點。datashader 的 GPU 版本。

lonboard for big-data (2024): WebGL-accelerated geospatial + large scatter viz; renders 10M+ points in-browser. The GPU counterpart of datashader.

Datasaurus Dozen (Matejka & Fitzmaurice 2017)：用模擬退火造出 13 組同 stats 但不同形狀的資料集，包含一隻恐龍。後續 2024 Cairo 出版《How Charts Lie》紀念延伸。原始 paper。

Datasaurus Dozen (Matejka & Fitzmaurice 2017): simulated annealing crafts 13 datasets with identical stats and wildly different shapes — including a dinosaur. Cairo's 2024 "How Charts Lie" honors this lineage.

2025 不確定性視覺化 (Uncertainty Viz)：Bayesian posterior 顯示在主圖上而非另開圖；技術：HOPs (Hypothetical Outcome Plots, Hullman 2015)、gradient interval、quantile dotplot。VAST 2024 多個 paper 強調「don't hide the uncertainty」。

2025 Uncertainty Viz: Bayesian posteriors embedded in the main chart, not in a separate panel. Techniques: HOPs (Hullman 2015), gradient intervals, quantile dotplots. Multiple VAST 2024 papers emphasize "don't hide the uncertainty."
