CHAPTER 10 / 20 · MODULE C

密度型與機率型分群

當群非球狀、密度不均、有雜訊，K-Means 就失效。本章介紹 DBSCAN、OPTICS、HDBSCAN、Mean-Shift、GMM、Spectral——形狀無偏見的分群家族。

When clusters are non-spherical, vary in density, or contain noise, K-Means breaks down. This chapter covers DBSCAN, OPTICS, HDBSCAN, Mean-Shift, GMM, and Spectral — the shape-agnostic clustering family.

故事開場

場景：城市犯罪熱點

SCENE · LAPD 2017

街道是線性的，K-Means 卻畫圓

洛杉磯警察分析部的 Officer Jiménez 接到任務：找出市區的犯罪「熱區」以重新部署巡邏。她拿到 18,000 個過去半年的搶劫地點 (lat, lon)，先試 K-Means K=10——產生 10 個圓形熱區，每個都跨越多條街、包含大量低犯罪格子。指揮官搖頭：「巡警不會在圓裡跑，會沿街跑。」

她改用 DBSCAN：ε = 100 m、minPts = 8。結果驚人——找到 7 條沿街道分布的「線狀」熱區，與夜市、地鐵站出口、停車場入口完全吻合。剩下 2,300 個點被標為 noise，正是真正的孤立事件。

但問題沒結束。市中心有些區段是「擁擠熱區」（minPts 應該高一點），郊區又有「稀疏但確實是熱區」的點——同一個 ε 無法兩全。她升級到 HDBSCAN：不用固定 ε，自動找不同密度的群。最後 14 個熱區、其中 3 個是「巢狀」（大熱區內含小熱區）。指揮官這次點頭了。

Officer Jiménez at LAPD analytics gets a brief: find crime "hotspots" downtown to redeploy patrols. She has 18,000 robbery locations (lat, lon) from the past six months. K-Means K=10 first — gives 10 round blobs, each crossing many streets and including lots of low-crime cells. The commander shakes his head: "Cops don't patrol in circles. They patrol along streets."

She switches to DBSCAN: ε = 100 m, minPts = 8. Stunning — 7 linear hotspots aligned with night markets, subway exits, parking-garage entrances. 2,300 points end up as noise — true isolated incidents.

But it's not done. Downtown has "packed hotspots" (higher minPts needed); the suburbs have "sparse but real hotspots". One ε can't fit both. She upgrades to HDBSCAN: no fixed ε, varying-density clusters. Final result: 14 hotspots, 3 of them nested (big hotspot contains small ones). This time the commander nods.

核心詞彙

12 個關鍵術語

Density-based clustering
密度型分群
把擠在一起的點視為一群，散散的點當「沒朋友」。
Treat packed points as a cluster, lonely points as "no friends".
群定義為高密度連通分量；密度可由 kernel density estimate 或 ε-鄰域點數量度。代表：DBSCAN、OPTICS、HDBSCAN、Mean-Shift。
A cluster = a connected high-density region; density via kernel density estimate or ε-neighborhood count. Examples: DBSCAN, OPTICS, HDBSCAN, Mean-Shift.
Core point
核心點
在自己周圍 ε 內有很多朋友（≥ minPts）的點——他是「群的種子」。
A point with lots of friends (≥ minPts) within ε — the cluster seed.
|N_ε(p)| ≥ minPts；DBSCAN 從 core 出發，藉 density-reachable 擴張群。
|N_ε(p)| ≥ minPts; DBSCAN expands clusters from cores via density-reachability.
Border point
邊界點
朋友不夠多，但住在某個 core 附近——被拉進群但不能繼續介紹新朋友。
Not enough friends, but lives near a core — joins the cluster but can't recruit further.
|N_ε(p)| < minPts 但存在 core q 使 p ∈ N_ε(q)；被吸入群但不再擴張。
|N_ε(p)| < minPts but a core q exists with p ∈ N_ε(q); absorbed without extending.
Noise point
雜訊點
既沒朋友、又不靠近任何熱鬧地——獨來獨往。標為 −1。
No friends, doesn't live near any busy spot — solo. Labeled −1.
既非 core 也非任何 core 的 ε 鄰域成員；DBSCAN 的 unique 功能——其他演算法（K-Means、GMM）強制每點入群。
Neither core nor in any core's ε-neighborhood; DBSCAN's unique feature — K-Means and GMM force every point into a cluster.
ε (eps)
鄰域半徑
畫圓時用的半徑——多大算「附近」。
The radius of the circle — how close counts as "nearby".
用 k-distance plot 找肘部選擇；k = minPts，將 k-NN 距離降序排序，肘部即建議 ε（Ester et al. 1996 KDD）。
Choose by k-distance plot knee; with k = minPts, sort k-NN distances descending, knee is suggested ε (Ester et al. 1996 KDD).
MinPts
最少鄰居數
要算「熱鬧」至少幾個朋友？通常 2 × 維度。
How many friends count as "busy"? Usually 2 × dimensionality.
經驗法則 minPts ≥ 2·dim；越大越保守、抑制雜訊但可能漏小群。可由 OPTICS 一次掃過多個值。
Rule of thumb minPts ≥ 2·dim; larger = more conservative, suppresses noise but may miss small clusters. OPTICS can sweep multiple values at once.
Density-reachability
密度可達
如果可以一步一步從一個 core 跳到你（每步都不超過 ε），你就「可達」。
If we can hop core-to-core to you with each step ≤ ε, you're "reachable".
直接密度可達：p ∈ N_ε(q) 且 q is core。密度可達：直接可達鏈。DBSCAN 的群 = 密度可達閉包。
Directly density-reachable: p ∈ N_ε(q) and q is core. Density-reachable: chain of direct steps. A DBSCAN cluster = closure under density-reachability.
OPTICS
OPTICS
DBSCAN 的「多解析度」版本——不固定 ε，給你一張可達距離圖，自己挑高度。
DBSCAN's "multi-resolution" cousin — no fixed ε, gives a reachability plot to cut at any height.
Ankerst et al. 1999；不直接分群，輸出 reachability plot（順序 vs 可達距離），由 ξ 或手動切山谷得到群。
Ankerst et al. 1999; doesn't cluster directly — outputs a reachability plot (order vs reachability distance); valleys cut by ξ or manually give clusters.
HDBSCAN
HDBSCAN
DBSCAN 升級版：自動處理「不同密度」的群，還會給你每點的成員機率。
DBSCAN's upgrade: handles varying density automatically, plus gives a membership probability per point.
Campello, Moulavi, Sander 2013：mutual reachability distance + condensed cluster tree；以 cluster stability (excess of mass) 自動選最佳分割。
Campello, Moulavi, Sander 2013: mutual reachability distance + condensed cluster tree; selects the optimal cut by cluster stability (excess of mass).
Mutual reachability
互可達距離
兩點的距離，但要 max(原距離, 各自的 k-NN 距離)——讓稀疏區的點變「遠」。
A pair's distance, but using max(actual distance, each point's k-NN distance) — points in sparse regions look farther apart.
d_mreach(a,b) = max{core_k(a), core_k(b), d(a,b)}；讓稀疏邊界被自動「拉開」，使 HDBSCAN 對密度變化不敏感。
d_mreach(a,b) = max{core_k(a), core_k(b), d(a,b)}; pulls sparse borders apart so HDBSCAN becomes density-invariant.
GMM
高斯混合模型
把資料當成 K 個高斯山疊起來；每點來自哪座山是「機率」。
Treat data as a sum of K Gaussian bumps; each point's membership is a probability.
p(x) = Σ π_k 𝒩(x|μ_k, Σ_k)；用 EM 演算法迭代估參。covariance type: full/diag/tied/spherical。以 BIC 選 K。
p(x) = Σ π_k 𝒩(x|μ_k, Σ_k); estimated by EM. Covariance types: full/diag/tied/spherical. Choose K by BIC.
Spectral clustering
譜分群
把點連成圖，看圖的「振動模式」（特徵向量），就能切出非凸的群。
Connect points into a graph, look at its "vibration modes" (eigenvectors) — non-convex clusters fall out.
Ng-Jordan-Weiss 2002；取 normalized Laplacian L = I − D^{-1/2}AD^{-1/2} 的前 K 個小特徵向量，再 K-Means。處理流形結構。
Ng-Jordan-Weiss 2002; take the K smallest eigenvectors of the normalized Laplacian L = I − D^{-1/2}AD^{-1/2}, then K-Means. Handles manifold structure.

🏘️
CITY-MAP ANALOGY

DBSCAN：在城市地圖上標出「鄰里」

DBSCAN 像是拿出城市地圖，挑一個半徑（ε，例如 100 公尺）和最少房子數（minPts，例如 8）。每棟房子畫個半徑圓，圓內房子夠多——這就是「核心房」(core)。從 core 出發、半徑內可達的房子組成一個「鄰里」(cluster)。孤立的房子標為「郊野」(noise)。
HDBSCAN 是升級版：城市裡市區與郊區密度不同，HDBSCAN 不用固定半徑，自動找各種大小的鄰里。

DBSCAN is like pulling out a city map, picking a radius (ε, say 100 m) and a minimum house count (minPts, say 8). Draw a circle around each house; if enough houses fit inside, it's a "core house". Houses reachable from cores form a "neighborhood" (cluster). Isolated houses are "wilderness" (noise).
HDBSCAN is the upgrade: downtown and suburbs differ in density, so it doesn't fix a radius — it finds neighborhoods of all sizes.

點開看：DBSCAN 在高維為何失靈？
維度詛咒：在 d 維單位立方體中，隨機兩點的距離幾乎都集中在 √(d/6)。當 d > 20，所有點距離都差不多——ε 無法區分「附近」與「遠處」。對策：(1) 先 PCA / UMAP 降維（但這違反 DBSCAN 的「無監督」精神）、(2) 用 cosine 距離（高維分隔較佳）、(3) 改用 HDBSCAN（mutual reachability 緩解了，但沒完全解決）、(4) 直接放棄密度型，用 GMM。
Curse of dimensionality: in a d-dim unit hypercube, pairwise distances all concentrate around √(d/6). When d > 20, all distances look the same — ε can't distinguish "near" from "far". Fixes: (1) reduce with PCA / UMAP first (but this dilutes DBSCAN's unsupervised spirit), (2) use cosine distance (better separation in high-d), (3) HDBSCAN's mutual reachability helps but doesn't fully solve it, (4) abandon density-based and use GMM.

概覽

為什麼需要密度型分群？

K-Means 三大限制：(1) 必須事先指定 k，(2) 假設群為球狀，(3) 把每個點都強制指派到群（包含 outlier）。在許多生物醫學情境裡，群數未知、形狀為彎月或環、且含明顯離群——這時 密度型 (density-based) 方法閃耀登場。

密度型方法的核心觀念：群 = 高密度區域的連通元件，noise = 低密度區域的孤立點。代表算法為 1996 年 KDD 上提出的 DBSCAN，至今仍是 sklearn / R 的標準工具。

K-Means has three big limits: (1) k must be set, (2) clusters assumed spherical, (3) every point is forced into a cluster (including outliers). In many biomedical settings clusters are crescent- or ring-shaped, k is unknown, and there are real outliers — this is where density-based methods shine.

Core idea: clusters are connected components of high density, while noise sits in low-density regions. The flagship algorithm is DBSCAN (KDD 1996), still the standard in sklearn / R.

DBSCAN

一、DBSCAN：三類點與兩個參數

Core 核心點

在半徑 ε 內至少有 minPts 個鄰居。是群的「種子」。

Has at least minPts neighbors within radius ε. The cluster seed.

Border 邊界點

鄰居數 < minPts，但落在某 core 的 ε 內。被吸入該群但不再擴張。

Has < minPts neighbors, but lies within ε of some core. Absorbed but does not extend further.

Noise 雜訊點

既不是 core 也不在任何 core 的 ε 鄰域內。標記為 -1（不屬於任何群）。

Neither core nor within any core's ε. Labelled -1 (no cluster).

💡
選 ε 的經驗法則：畫 k-distance plot（k = minPts，遞減排序），找肘部。選 minPts：通常取 2×維度（如 2D 用 minPts=4）。 Choosing ε: plot the k-distance graph (k = minPts, sorted descending), find the knee. minPts: rule of thumb is 2 × dim (4 for 2D).

⚠️
HDBSCAN 改進 DBSCAN 的關鍵：引入 mutual reachability distance 與 condensed cluster tree，使其能處理「密度不均」的群——例如同時存在 1000 個與 30 個成員的群，DBSCAN 必須妥協單一 ε，HDBSCAN 不必。 HDBSCAN improves DBSCAN by: using mutual reachability distance and a condensed cluster tree, letting it handle varying-density clusters — e.g. one with 1000 members and another with 30 — without a single global ε.
GMM 與 EM

三、GMM 與軟分群

GMM 假設資料來自 k 個多元高斯的混合：p(x)=Σ π_k · 𝒩(x | μ_k, Σ_k)。以 EM 演算法交替更新：(E-step) 算每點屬於每群的機率 (responsibility)；(M-step) 用 responsibility-weighted 更新 π, μ, Σ。

k 的選擇用 BIC（越小越好，內建懲罰複雜度）。共變異形式有 full（橢圓任意角度）、diagonal（橢圓但軸對齊）、spherical（圓形——退化為 K-Means）、tied（共用 Σ）。

GMM models data as a mixture of k Gaussians: p(x) = Σ π_k · 𝒩(x | μ_k, Σ_k). The EM algorithm alternates: (E-step) compute each point's responsibility for each component; (M-step) update π, μ, Σ using those weights.

Pick k by BIC (lower is better, penalizes complexity). Covariance types: full (any-angle ellipses), diagonal (axis-aligned ellipses), spherical (circles — reduces to K-Means), tied (shared Σ).

from sklearn.cluster import DBSCAN, OPTICS, SpectralClustering, MeanShift
from sklearn.mixture import GaussianMixture
from sklearn.neighbors import NearestNeighbors
import hdbscan, numpy as np

# 1) DBSCAN — k-distance heuristic for eps
nn = NearestNeighbors(n_neighbors=4).fit(X)
d, _ = nn.kneighbors(X); d = np.sort(d[:, -1])
# 肘部就是建議 eps / knee gives suggested eps

# 2) OPTICS

bics = []
for k in range(1, 10):

best_k = min(bics, key=lambda kb: kb[1])[0]

# 5) Spectral Clustering for non-convex shapes / 非凸群

最終結果。C1 = {P1, P2, P3, P4} (4 cores)；C2 = {P5, P6, P7} (3 cores)；noise = {P8}。2 群 + 1 個雜訊點。若改 ε = 5.0、minPts = 3：P8 仍無鄰居（最近 P5 距離 ≈ 4.24）——仍是 noise。但 ε = 6.0 時，P8 變 border 加入 C2，凸顯參數的敏感性。 Final result. 思辨

三個常被爭辯的問題

Q · UPGRADE

HDBSCAN 真的比 DBSCAN 嚴格更好嗎？

官方倡導者（McInnes 2017）說「是」——HDBSCAN 不需 ε、處理密度變化、給軟成員機率。但實務不一定。

The official advocate (McInnes 2017) says "yes" — no ε, varying-density, soft membership. But practice is more nuanced.

正方： 「在 OpenStreetMap、Twitter 地理資料、scRNA-seq 上，HDBSCAN 都贏。Seurat v5 也預設用它。」 "On OpenStreetMap, Twitter geo, scRNA-seq — HDBSCAN wins. Seurat v5 uses it by default."
反方： 「HDBSCAN 的 min_cluster_size 比 ε 更難解釋，新使用者完全猜不準。在密度均勻的資料上，DBSCAN 反而結果更可控。」 "HDBSCAN's min_cluster_size is harder to interpret than ε; newcomers can't guess it. On uniform-density data, DBSCAN gives more controllable results."
折衷： 「兩個都跑——若結果一致，DBSCAN 更簡單；若 HDBSCAN 找出更細的群、且該細粒度符合領域知識，就用 HDBSCAN。」 "Run both — if they agree, DBSCAN is simpler; if HDBSCAN finds finer clusters that match domain knowledge, use HDBSCAN."
Q · SPECTRAL

Spectral clustering 何時打敗 K-Means？

當群為「流形結構」——非凸、彎曲、糾纏。經典範例：瑞士卷、兩個交織的環。Spectral 在 normalized Laplacian 的特徵空間中，這些群變成球狀，K-Means 就可以正確分。但代價：O(N³) 特徵分解，N > 10,000 就吃不消。

When clusters are manifolds — non-convex, curved, intertwined. Classic: Swiss roll, two interlocked rings. In the normalized Laplacian's eigenspace, those clusters become spherical and K-Means works. Cost: O(N³) eigendecomp; N > 10,000 chokes.

Q · GMM

GMM 用 BIC 選 K 在高維可靠嗎？

公式 BIC = k·log(N) − 2·log L，當 d 大時 full covariance 每多一群多 d(d+1)/2 個參數——BIC 重重懲罰。實務上：d > 50 時用 diagonal Σ；或先 PCA 降到 ~20 維再 GMM。AIC 比 BIC 寬鬆，但常選 K 太多。正規化版：variational Bayesian GMM（sklearn BayesianGaussianMixture），自動「關掉」不需要的群。

BIC = k·log(N) − 2·log L. In high-D, full covariance adds d(d+1)/2 params per cluster — BIC penalizes hard. In practice: d > 50 → diagonal Σ; or PCA to ~20 first. AIC is more lenient but often over-fits K. Regularized: variational Bayesian GMM (sklearn BayesianGaussianMixture) automatically prunes unneeded components.

🗺️
SPOTLIGHT · 2019

OpenStreetMap 的 1.2M 個錯置 POI

2019 年 OpenStreetMap 社群用 HDBSCAN 對全球 2.5 億個 POI（興趣點）跑空間分群——找出「不該獨立存在」的離群 POI（如標在海中央的餐廳）。方法：以 (lat, lon) 計算 haversine 距離、min_cluster_size = 5，HDBSCAN 標出 1.2M 個 noise 點，再由志願者人工確認約 60% 確實是貼錯位置，自動修正流程節省了估計 8,000 人時。關鍵：DBSCAN 一個全域 ε 無法兼顧東京（極密）與蒙古（極疏）；HDBSCAN 自動處理。

In 2019 OpenStreetMap volunteers ran HDBSCAN over 250M global POIs to spot "shouldn't-be-alone" points (e.g., a restaurant in the middle of the ocean). Method: haversine distance on (lat, lon), min_cluster_size = 5. HDBSCAN flagged 1.2M noise points; volunteers manually confirmed ~60% were genuine misplacements. Estimated 8,000 person-hours saved. Key: a single global ε can't handle both Tokyo (ultra-dense) and Mongolia (ultra-sparse); HDBSCAN handles both.

陷阱

密度型 / 機率型分群 五大常見錯誤

ε 隨便挑

「ε=0.5 因為例子裡看過」——不同尺度的特徵需要完全不同的 ε。對策：畫 k-distance plot（k = minPts），找 knee。

"ε=0.5 because the example used it" — different feature scales need very different ε. Fix: k-distance plot (k = minPts), find the knee.

高維直上 DBSCAN

d > 20 時 ε 失去意義（維度詛咒）。對策：先 PCA / UMAP；或改用 cosine 距離；或乾脆 GMM。

d > 20: ε loses meaning (curse of dimensionality). Fix: PCA / UMAP first; use cosine; or switch to GMM.

GMM 群數太多

不掃 BIC 就 K=20，得到一堆 1–2 點的「群」。對策：掃 K=1–10，看 BIC 拐點；或用 Variational Bayes GMM 自動 prune。

K=20 without scanning BIC gives tiny 1–2-point "clusters". Fix: sweep K=1–10 with BIC; or use Variational Bayes GMM that auto-prunes.

Mean-Shift bandwidth 錯

bandwidth 太小 → 太多群；太大 → 全併一群。對策：sklearn 的 estimate_bandwidth 或從 k-distance plot 估。

Too small → too many clusters; too large → one giant cluster. Fix: sklearn's estimate_bandwidth or k-distance heuristic.

Spectral 對 disconnected 圖

若 KNN 圖斷成多個元件，Laplacian 第 2 個特徵值 = 0，spectral 結果不穩。對策：確保連通（增加 n_neighbors），或先 connected_components。

If the KNN graph splits into components, the Laplacian's second eigenvalue = 0 and spectral becomes unstable. Fix: ensure connectivity (increase n_neighbors) or pre-process with connected_components.

把雜訊強制歸群

某些報告把 DBSCAN noise (−1) 當第 K+1 群——錯！它們不是同類，只是「都是異類」。對策：分開分析 noise 點（可能是 outlier、罕見群或錯誤）。

Some reports treat DBSCAN noise (−1) as the K+1th cluster — wrong! They're not similar, just "all dissimilar". Fix: analyze noise points separately (could be outliers, rare clusters, or errors).

不好的做法

DBSCAN(eps=0.5, min_samples=5).fit(raw_geno_matrix)——50000 個 SNP 特徵直接灌入，ε 用預設。結果：1 個巨大群 + 5000 個 noise。

DBSCAN(eps=0.5, min_samples=5).fit(raw_geno_matrix) — 50000-SNP matrix straight in, default ε. Result: 1 giant cluster + 5000 noise.

好的做法

(1) PCA 降到 20 維；(2) StandardScaler；(3) kNNdistplot(k=2·dim) 找 ε；(4) HDBSCAN min_cluster_size=15, min_samples=5；(5) 檢視 cluster persistence；(6) 用 hdbscan.approximate_predict 給軟成員機率。

(1) PCA to 20D; (2) StandardScaler; (3) k-distance plot (k=2·dim) for ε; (4) HDBSCAN min_cluster_size=15, min_samples=5; (5) inspect cluster persistence; (6) use hdbscan.approximate_predict for soft probabilities.

進階

2024–2026 最前沿

SOTA · 2024–2026

從 HDBSCAN 到 deep clustering 與對比學習

HDBSCAN 軟分群（2024）。除了 hard labels，hdbscan.all_points_membership_vectors() 給每點對每群的機率向量。配合 approximate_predict 可在新資料上 zero-shot 推論——這是 Seurat v5 接受 HDBSCAN 為預設的原因。

HDBSCAN soft clustering (2024). Beyond hard labels, hdbscan.all_points_membership_vectors() gives per-point probability vectors over clusters. Combined with approximate_predict you get zero-shot inference on new data — the reason Seurat v5 adopted HDBSCAN by default.

Deep clustering 路線。DEC (Xie et al. 2016)、IDEC (Guo 2017)、DCEC——用 autoencoder 學表徵、同時優化分群目標。2024 SCDeepCluster (Cell Sys.) 為 scRNA-seq 客製化，整合 ZINB 損失。但「比 HDBSCAN 好多少」仍有爭議，且需大量訓練。

Deep clustering line. DEC (Xie et al. 2016), IDEC (Guo 2017), DCEC — learn representations via autoencoder + cluster loss jointly. 2024 SCDeepCluster (Cell Sys.) customized for scRNA-seq with ZINB loss. But "how much better than HDBSCAN" remains debated, and training is expensive.

對比分群（2025）。受 SimCLR / DINO 啟發，先用對比學習 (contrastive) 把資料映到「同類點靠近、異類遠離」的嵌入空間，再 K-Means 或 HDBSCAN。SCAN (Van Gansbeke 2020 ECCV) 在 CIFAR-10 上達 89% 準確（無監督）——但生醫資料還在驗證中。

Contrastive clustering (2025). Inspired by SimCLR / DINO, first learn an embedding where same-class points are pulled together (contrastively), then K-Means or HDBSCAN. SCAN (Van Gansbeke 2020 ECCV) hits 89% unsupervised on CIFAR-10 — but biomedical validation is still ongoing.

變分 GMM（VBGMM）。不挑 K、自動 prune 不需要的群（sklearn BayesianGaussianMixture）。weight_concentration_prior 控制 sparsity。原理：Dirichlet process prior，理論上支援無限混合 (Blei & Jordan 2006)。

Variational GMM (VBGMM). No K to pick; auto-prunes unneeded components (sklearn BayesianGaussianMixture). weight_concentration_prior controls sparsity. Theory: Dirichlet process prior, infinite mixture in principle (Blei & Jordan 2006).

論文路徑。Ester 1996 (KDD); Ankerst 1999 (SIGMOD); Ng-Jordan-Weiss 2002 (NIPS); Campello 2013 (PAKDD); McInnes 2017 (JOSS); Xie 2016 (ICML, DEC); Van Gansbeke 2020 (ECCV, SCAN); Blei & Jordan 2006 (Bayes Anal); Stuart 2019 (Cell, Seurat v3)。

Paper trail. Ester 1996 (KDD); Ankerst 1999 (SIGMOD); Ng-Jordan-Weiss 2002 (NIPS); Campello 2013 (PAKDD); McInnes 2017 (JOSS); Xie 2016 (ICML, DEC); Van Gansbeke 2020 (ECCV, SCAN); Blei & Jordan 2006 (Bayes Anal); Stuart 2019 (Cell, Seurat v3).
