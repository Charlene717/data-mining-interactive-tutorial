開場故事
案例

📚 5 萬篇 COVID-19 摘要 vs 3 個人類

2021 年初，某學術中心的疫苗安全小組接到任務：兩週內完成「mRNA 疫苗副作用相關文獻」回顧。PubMed 撈出來 50,000 篇。三位 PI 看了一眼直接放棄：人類每篇 5 分鐘看完，10 個人連續工作 8 小時也要 21 天——根本來不及。

研究助理 K 拿出 PubMedBERT + TF-IDF + LDA 流水線：先用 PubMedBERT 把每篇摘要轉成 768 維向量，TF-IDF 過濾「無關內容」（如純細胞實驗），LDA 找出 20 個主題，再對「心肌炎」「血栓」「過敏反應」等關鍵字做精確 NER。72 小時內，K 已經把 50,000 篇縮到 380 篇高關聯文獻，並繪出主題-時間趨勢圖。最重要的——他在 第 113 篇找到一條極罕見的「免疫性血小板減少 + 大腦靜脈竇血栓」訊號，比 EMA 官方公告早了 9 天提交給中心臨床藥學部。

文字探勘並非取代專家，而是把專家從「先看 50,000 篇」解放出來，專注於 380 篇——和那 1 個罕見訊號。

Early 2021. A vaccine-safety team gets two weeks to review the mRNA-vaccine adverse-event literature. PubMed returns 50,000 hits. Three PIs glance at it and give up: 5 min/abstract × 10 people × 8 h = 21 days. Impossible.

Research assistant K spins up a PubMedBERT + TF-IDF + LDA pipeline: PubMedBERT embeds each abstract to 768-D; TF-IDF filters off-topic (pure cell experiments); LDA surfaces 20 latent topics; precision NER for "myocarditis", "thrombosis", "anaphylaxis". In 72 hours, K narrows 50,000 to 380 high-relevance abstracts and plots topic-vs-time. On paper #113, K finds an ultra-rare "immune thrombocytopenia + cerebral venous sinus thrombosis" co-occurrence signal — 9 days before EMA's official notice.

Text mining doesn't replace the expert; it frees the expert from "first read all 50,000" to focus on 380 — and the 1 rare signal.

概覽

為什麼要做文字探勘？

醫療資料約 80% 為非結構化文字：病歷記錄、影像報告、出院摘要、藥品仿單、文獻。要將這些轉成可學習向量，需經過 (1) 前處理（tokenize → 停用字 → stem/lemma）、(2) 表徵（BoW → TF-IDF → 詞向量 → 句向量）、(3) 建模（分類、主題、摘要、命名實體 NER）。

2018 BERT 之後，NLP 由「特徵工程」進入「預訓練 + 微調」時代。在生醫領域有專門的領域預訓練模型：BioBERT (2019, PubMed/PMC)、PubMedBERT (2020, 全 PubMed scratch)、SciBERT (科學文獻)、BlueBERT (PubMed+MIMIC-III)，常於 BC5CDR、NCBI-disease、ChemProt 等 benchmark 拿到 SOTA。

About 80% of medical data is unstructured text: clinical notes, radiology reports, discharge summaries, drug labels, literature. Turning text into learnable vectors needs (1) preprocessing (tokenize → stopwords → stem/lemma), (2) representation (BoW → TF-IDF → word/sentence vectors), (3) modeling (classification, topics, summarization, NER).

After BERT (2018), NLP shifted from "feature engineering" to "pretrain + fine-tune". Biomedical domain-pretrained models include BioBERT (2019, PubMed/PMC), PubMedBERT (2020, scratch on PubMed), SciBERT (sci. literature), BlueBERT (PubMed+MIMIC-III) — often SOTA on BC5CDR, NCBI-disease, ChemProt.

📚
類比

文字探勘像「圖書館員 + 偵探」

TF-IDF 是圖書館員整理「索引」：每篇文件最具代表性的詞是什麼？常見詞 "the"、"病人" 不算。Word2Vec / GloVe 像給每個詞畫人格側寫——每個詞在「活潑度／正式度／情緒」等維度上各有座標，「醫生」「醫師」很近，「醫生」和「警察」較遠。BERT 是會看上下文的偵探——"bank" 在 "river bank" 和 "bank account" 知道是不同意思。BioBERT / PubMedBERT 是讀過 PubMed 整套的偵探，懂得 "PD-L1" 是免疫檢查點不是「個人助理」。

TF-IDF is the librarian's index: which words are distinctive for this document? "the" / "patient" don't count. Word2Vec / GloVe sketch a personality profile per word — "doctor" and "physician" sit close; "doctor" and "police" far. BERT is the detective with context — "bank" near "river" ≠ "bank" near "account". BioBERT / PubMedBERT is the detective who has read all of PubMed — knows "PD-L1" is an immune checkpoint, not "personal assistant".

Captures short-range collocations; high n explodes dim; TF-IDF often uses (1,2).

停用字
"the"、"的"、"和" 這種「沒什麼意義但出現超多」的字。
"the", "and", "of" — common but uninformative.

High frequency, low info; but medical text MUST keep "no" / "not".

BoW / TF / IDF / TF-IDF
詞袋 / 詞頻 / 逆文件頻率
把文件當成裝詞的「袋子」；常見詞 IDF 低，獨特詞 IDF 高。
A document is a "bag of words"; common terms get low IDF, unique terms high.
TF=count / |doc|；IDF=log(N/df)；TF-IDF=TF·IDF。Sparse、interpretable。
TF=count/|doc|; IDF=log(N/df); TF-IDF=TF·IDF. Sparse and interpretable.
Cosine similarity
餘弦相似度
看兩支箭頭夾的「角度」——同向＝1，正交＝0。
Angle between two arrows — same dir = 1, perpendicular = 0.
cos(θ) = u·v / (‖u‖·‖v‖)；無視長度只看方向，TF-IDF 標配。
cos(θ) = u·v / (‖u‖·‖v‖); length-invariant; the standard for TF-IDF.
Word2Vec (CBOW, Skip-gram)
Word2Vec
透過「上下文猜中間字」學出每個詞的座標。
"Predict middle word from context" learns each word's coordinates.
Mikolov 2013；CBOW 用上下文預測中心，Skip-gram 反之；負採樣加速。
Mikolov 2013; CBOW context→centre, Skip-gram centre→context; negative sampling for speed.
GloVe
GloVe 全域向量
用「兩字共同出現次數」推每個詞的座標。
Coordinates from "how often each pair co-occurs".
Pennington 2014；矩陣分解觀點，cost = Σ f(X_ij)·(wᵢᵀwⱼ + b − log X_ij)²。
Pennington 2014; matrix-factorisation view, cost = Σ f(X_ij)·(wᵢᵀwⱼ + b − log X_ij)².
FastText (subword)
FastText 子詞
把每個詞拆成「字母片段」，沒見過的新詞也能拼出向量。
Split each word into character n-grams; works for unseen words too.
Bojanowski 2017；vec(word) = Σ vec(char n-gram)；OOV-robust。
Bojanowski 2017; vec(word) = Σ vec(char n-gram); robust to OOV.
Transformer / Attention
Transformer 自注意力
每個字「同時看」句子裡所有其他字，重要的多看一點。
Every word "looks at" all others simultaneously, weighting the important ones.
Vaswani 2017；Attention(Q,K,V) = softmax(QKᵀ/√d)·V；O(n²) 為瓶頸。
Vaswani 2017; Attention(Q,K,V) = softmax(QKᵀ/√d)·V; O(n²) bottleneck.

BERT 系列

Bidirectional Transformer; BioBERT/PubMedBERT trained on biomedical text.

Devlin 2018; MLM + NSP; BioBERT (Lee 2019) continues from BERT on PubMed; PubMedBERT (Gu 2020) trains from scratch on PubMed.

潛在 Dirichlet 配置

時間線

從 1957 Luhn TF 到 2026 PubMed RAG

1957 Luhn 引入 TF 概念IBM Journal；用詞頻為文件「自動摘要」打分，現代資訊檢索的奠基。IBM Journal; word-frequency scoring for auto-summary — foundation of modern IR.
1972 Salton 引入 IDFSMART 系統把「在多少文件出現」加入加權，誕生 TF-IDF；至今仍是基準。SMART system adds "how many docs contain it" — TF-IDF is born; still a baseline today.
1990 LSA (Deerwester)SVD-based 潛在語意分析，首度把「同義詞」放進向量空間。SVD-based latent semantic analysis — first to place synonyms close in vector space.
2003 Blei LDAJMLR；Dirichlet 先驗的機率主題模型，主導 2003-2018 主題建模。JMLR; Dirichlet-prior probabilistic topic model — dominated topic modelling 2003-2018.
2013 Mikolov Word2Vec"king − man + woman = queen" 震驚學界；分布語意向量化的革命起點。"king − man + woman = queen" stuns the field; distributional semantics goes vector.
2014 Pennington GloVe用全域共現矩陣分解，向量同樣優雅、訓練更穩定。Global co-occurrence matrix factorisation; elegant vectors, more stable training.
2018 Devlin BERTNAACL；雙向 Transformer + MLM 預訓練——NLP 進入「預訓練 + 微調」時代。NAACL; bidirectional Transformer + MLM — "pretrain + fine-tune" era begins.

2020 Gu PubMedBERTACM HEALTH；從零開始在 PubMed 預訓——專屬詞表，多項 SOTA。ACM HEALTH; pre-trained from scratch on PubMed — dedicated vocab, multiple SOTA.

前處理

一、五大前處理步驟

✂️

① Tokenization

切詞。英文以空白為主；中文/醫學詞需 jieba / ckiptagger / scispaCy。新一代用 BPE / WordPiece (子詞)。

Splitting. English by whitespace; Chinese/medical needs jieba / ckiptagger / scispaCy. Modern models use BPE / WordPiece (sub-word).

🚫

② Stopwords

移除高頻無意義字（the, a, 是, 的）。但醫學情境下保留否定詞 "no", "not"！

Remove high-frequency low-info words (the, a). But in medical text, keep negations like "no", "not"!

🌱

③ Stemming

規則切尾 (Porter, Snowball)：running → run。快但粗糙：「universal → univers」。

Rule-based suffix stripping (Porter, Snowball): running → run. Fast but crude: "universal → univers".

📖

④ Lemmatization

查詞典還原原形：was → be。準但慢；spaCy / WordNetLemmatizer 是常見實作。

Dictionary-based root recovery: was → be. Slower but accurate; spaCy / WordNetLemmatizer are common.

🔢

⑤ Vectorization

BoW (count)、TF-IDF、n-gram、Word2Vec、GloVe、FastText、BERT embedding。

BoW (count), TF-IDF, n-gram, Word2Vec, GloVe, FastText, BERT embedding.

🏷️

⑥ POS / NER

詞性標註 + 命名實體（疾病、藥品、基因）。scispaCy + UMLS linker 為生醫 NER 主流。

POS tagging + named entities (disease, drug, gene). scispaCy + UMLS linker is the biomed NER standard.

💡
TF-IDF 直覺：TF = 詞在當文件出現次數；IDF = log(N / 含該詞的文件數)。在所有文件都出現的詞（the, 病人）IDF≈0 → 整體權重低。在少數文件高頻出現的詞 → 高權重。 TF-IDF intuition: TF = term count in this doc; IDF = log(N / docs containing term). Words in every document (the, patient) have IDF ≈ 0 → low weight. Words frequent in few documents → high weight.
主題模型

三、LDA 主題模型

Latent Dirichlet Allocation (LDA, Blei 2003) 假設：每篇文件是「主題的混合」，每個主題是「字詞的混合」，兩者皆服從 Dirichlet 先驗。產出兩個矩陣：document-topic θ 與 topic-word φ，可同時用於降維、視覺化、主題追蹤。

變體：HDP（自動決定 K）、BTM（Biterm，短文）、CTM / BERTopic（用 BERT embedding + UMAP + HDBSCAN，2022 後常用於 PubMed/COVID 文獻洞察）。

Latent Dirichlet Allocation (LDA, Blei 2003) assumes each document is a mixture of topics and each topic is a mixture of words, with Dirichlet priors. It yields two matrices: document-topic θ and topic-word φ — used for dim-reduction, visualization, topic tracking.

Variants: HDP (auto-K), BTM (short text), CTM / BERTopic (BERT embedding + UMAP + HDBSCAN, popular for PubMed/COVID literature mining post-2022).

程式碼

實作：TF-IDF → Word2Vec → LDA → BioBERT

library(tm); library(tidytext); library(text2vec); library(topicmodels)

# 1) TF-IDF（tidytext）
docs <- tibble(id = 1:3, text = c("patient with sepsis fever",
                                  "diabetes glucose insulin patient",
                                  "sepsis shock icu admission"))
tf_idf <- docs |>
  unnest_tokens(word, text) |>
  count(id, word) |>
  bind_tf_idf(word, id, n)

# 2) Word2Vec（text2vec, GloVe-style）
it    <- itoken(tolower(docs$text), tokenizer = word_tokenizer)
vocab <- create_vocabulary(it)
v_pr  <- prune_vocabulary(vocab, term_count_min = 2)
tcm   <- create_tcm(it, vocab_vectorizer(v_pr), skip_grams_window = 5)
glove <- GlobalVectors$new(rank = 50, x_max = 10)
wv    <- glove$fit_transform(tcm, n_iter = 10)

# 3) LDA 主題模型
dtm <- cast_dtm(count(unnest_tokens(docs, word, text), id, word), id, word, n)
lda <- topicmodels::LDA(dtm, k = 2, control = list(seed = 42))
tidy(lda, matrix = "beta")         # topic-word
from sklearn.feature_extraction.text import TfidfVectorizer, CountVectorizer
from gensim.models import Word2Vec, LdaModel
from gensim.corpora import Dictionary
from transformers   import AutoTokenizer, AutoModel
import torch

docs = ["patient with sepsis fever",
        "diabetes glucose insulin patient",
        "sepsis shock icu admission"]

# 1) TF-IDF
tfidf = TfidfVectorizer(stop_words="english", ngram_range=(1,2))
X = tfidf.fit_transform(docs)        # sparse matrix

# 2) Word2Vec（Skip-gram, sg=1; CBOW, sg=0）
sents = [d.split() for d in docs]
w2v = Word2Vec(sents, vector_size=100, window=5,
               min_count=1, sg=1, epochs=50)
vec_sepsis = w2v.wv["sepsis"]

# 3) LDA
dct  = Dictionary(sents)
bow  = [dct.doc2bow(s) for s in sents]
lda  = LdaModel(corpus=bow, id2word=dct, num_topics=2, passes=10)
print(lda.show_topics())

# 4) BioBERT 句向量（biomedical）
tok = AutoTokenizer.from_pretrained("dmis-lab/biobert-v1.1")
mod = AutoModel.from_pretrained("dmis-lab/biobert-v1.1")
inp = tok(docs, padding=True, return_tensors="pt")
with torch.no_grad():
    emb = mod(**inp).last_hidden_state.mean(dim=1)   # [N,768]
⚠️
領域偏移：用 BERT (general) 處理 PubMed 表現往往遜於 BioBERT/PubMedBERT。在病歷 (clinical) 還需用 ClinicalBERT 或 Bio_ClinicalBERT (Alsentzer 2019)。「英文 ≠ 醫學英文」。 Domain shift: BERT (general) on PubMed often loses to BioBERT/PubMedBERT. For clinical notes you also need ClinicalBERT or Bio_ClinicalBERT (Alsentzer 2019). "English ≠ medical English".

TF-IDF(t, d) = TF(t,d) × log(N / df(t)) · cos(u, v) = u·v / (‖u‖·‖v‖)N = 文件總數，df(t) = 含 t 的文件數。TF-IDF 把「全部都有的詞」壓低、把「少數高頻的詞」推高。N = total docs, df(t) = docs containing t. TF-IDF down-weights ubiquitous terms and lifts terms frequent in few docs.
🔬 為什麼用 log，而不是 1/df？

原始 IDF = N/df 在低 df 端會劇烈放大（df=1 vs df=2 差 50%），把超罕見詞權重炸高。log 變形讓 IDF 在 df 增大時逐漸飽和，避免噪音詞主導。理論上對應「資訊量」(Shannon)：詞 t 出現的「資訊」 = −log P(t)，而 P(t) ≈ df/N。Robertson 2004 給出 BM25 / IDF 變體更完整的機率化推導。

Raw IDF = N/df explodes at low df (df=1 vs df=2 differs by 50%), making ultra-rare words dominate. log-IDF saturates as df grows, suppressing noise. Theoretically it matches Shannon information: I(t) = −log P(t) with P(t) ≈ df/N. Robertson 2004 derives BM25 / IDF variants more rigorously.

研究討論

三個值得思考的真問題

Q1

LLM 會徹底取代 TF-IDF 嗎？

2024 年起 dense embedding 在 MTEB benchmark 大殺四方——但 BM25 (TF-IDF 的進化版) 仍在某些任務硬撐 SOTA。

Dense embeddings dominate MTEB since 2024 — yet BM25 (TF-IDF's grown-up cousin) still tops some tasks.

支持取代：BGE / Voyage-3 / E5 在 MTEB 平均超越 BM25 6-12 分；長文件、跨語言、需要語意對齊時 dense 完勝。
Replacement camp: BGE / Voyage-3 / E5 beat BM25 by 6-12 points on MTEB; dense wins on long docs, cross-lingual, semantic alignment.
反方：BEIR 18 個檢索任務上 BM25 在 6 個仍是冠軍；對短查詢、長尾詞、低資源語言、可解釋性要求高的場景，TF-IDF / BM25 不死。Thakur 2021 NeurIPS。實務做 hybrid (dense + sparse) 通常最穩。
Counter: Across 18 BEIR retrieval tasks, BM25 wins 6 of them; for short queries, long-tail terms, low-resource languages, and explainability, TF-IDF/BM25 stays alive (Thakur 2021 NeurIPS). In practice, hybrid (dense + sparse) beats either alone.
Q2

GPT-4 懂醫學文本，還需要 BioBERT 微調嗎？

GPT-4 USMLE Step 1 過 90 分，醫學論文摘要寫得似模似樣。BioBERT 的時代結束了嗎？

GPT-4 hits >90 on USMLE Step 1 and drafts medical-sounding abstracts. Is BioBERT's era over?

Pro-LLM：少樣本 in-context learning、prompt engineering 就能達到 90% BioBERT 微調效能（Singhal 2023, Med-PaLM 2）；不需要訓練資料、不需要 GPU 部署。
Pro-LLM: Few-shot in-context learning + prompts hits 90% of fine-tuned BioBERT (Singhal 2023, Med-PaLM 2) — no training data, no on-prem GPU.
BioBERT 仍重要：(1) 隱私敏感資料無法傳給 OpenAI；(2) 結構化 NER（精確 span）BioBERT 仍勝；(3) 推論成本 BioBERT 110M params 比 GPT-4 便宜千倍；(4) 可重現性、可審計性高得多。
BioBERT still matters: (1) privacy-sensitive data cannot leave the hospital; (2) span-exact NER still favours BioBERT; (3) BioBERT-110M inference is ~1000× cheaper than GPT-4; (4) far more reproducible and auditable.
Q3

開源 vs 專有 LLM 處理臨床文字？

Llama 3 70B、Mistral Large 2、Qwen 2.5 都在快速追趕 GPT-4；醫院該選哪邊？

Llama 3 70B, Mistral Large 2, Qwen 2.5 close in on GPT-4 fast. Which should a hospital choose?

專有 (GPT-4o / Claude)：能力天花板高、整合好、Azure HIPAA-compliant；缺點是 token 成本、資料離開機構、API 政策變動。
Proprietary (GPT-4o / Claude): Highest ceiling, smooth integration, Azure HIPAA-compliant; cons: token cost, data leaves the institution, vendor lock-in.
開源 (Llama 3 / Mistral)：本機部署、零外傳、可微調；缺點是運維成本、能力略遜、需自行驗證。2026 共識：敏感資料用開源（本地 LoRA 微調），非敏感前端用專有 API。
Open (Llama 3 / Mistral): On-prem, no leakage, fine-tunable; cons: ops cost, slightly weaker, validation on you. 2026 consensus: sensitive data → open with LoRA; non-sensitive front-end → proprietary API.
實戰案例
🦠
案例

CORD-19 挑戰賽 (2020)：兩週內把 20 萬篇 COVID-19 文獻分類完

2020 年 3 月，White House 聯合 Allen Institute、Microsoft、Kaggle 發起 COVID-19 Open Research Dataset (CORD-19)：200,000+ 篇 COVID-19 / SARS / coronavirus 文獻，要求社群在兩週內建立可用的檢索與摘要系統。

March 2020. The White House + Allen Institute + Microsoft + Kaggle launch CORD-19: 200,000+ papers on COVID-19/SARS/coronavirus, with the community asked to build retrieval + summarisation systems in two weeks.

獲勝方案的核心是 PubMedBERT + Elasticsearch + BM25 hybrid：用 PubMedBERT 做語意檢索、BM25 做關鍵字檢索，re-rank 取最佳結果。50 多支隊伍將管線開源——這是定義「pandemic NLP」的時刻。直到今日，BioASQ、TREC-COVID 仍用 CORD-19 為基準。

The winning recipe: PubMedBERT + Elasticsearch + BM25 hybrid — PubMedBERT for semantic retrieval, BM25 for keyword, re-rank for best results. 50+ teams open-sourced pipelines — the moment that defined "pandemic NLP". BioASQ and TREC-COVID still use CORD-19 as a benchmark.

教訓：(1) 危機驅動的 open dataset 比五年規劃的 benchmark 更能催化研究；(2) dense + sparse hybrid 是高效檢索的關鍵；(3) 領域預訓練模型（PubMedBERT）在 zero-shot 與微調都比通用 BERT 強。

Lessons: (1) crisis-driven open datasets catalyse research more than 5-year-planned benchmarks; (2) dense + sparse hybrid is the retrieval workhorse; (3) domain-pretrained models (PubMedBERT) beat general BERT in zero-shot and fine-tuned.

常見陷阱

六個地雷與修正

① LDA 沒移除停用字

"the / and / of" 會主導每個主題 → 看不到真正內容詞。修正：用 sklearn 的 ENGLISH_STOP_WORDS + 自訂醫學停用字 (patient, the, etc.)；也可用 max_df=0.95 過濾。

"the / and / of" dominate every topic → real terms hidden. Fix: sklearn ENGLISH_STOP_WORDS + custom medical stopwords; or max_df=0.95.

② Word2Vec 訓練在小語料

3,000 篇病歷訓 Word2Vec 結果一片噪音；word2vec 需要 > 1M tokens 才穩定。修正：用 PubMedBERT 預訓向量；或 FastText subword 在小語料表現較好。

Training Word2Vec on 3,000 notes is mostly noise; Word2Vec needs > 1M tokens. Fix: use PubMedBERT pretrained vectors; or FastText subword (more sample-efficient).

③ cosine 用在原始 count

兩篇都有很多 "the"，cosine 會錯誤地給高相似度。修正：cosine 應該在 TF-IDF 或 embedding 上計算，不是 raw count。

Both docs contain many "the" → cosine wrongly high. Fix: compute cosine on TF-IDF or embeddings — not raw counts.

④ LDA 主題數 K 拍腦袋

K 太小 → 主題糊；K 太大 → 重複碎裂。修正：用 perplexity / topic coherence (Cv, UMass)；或改用 BERTopic / HDP 自動定 K。

K too small → blurry topics; too large → redundant fragments. Fix: use perplexity / topic coherence (Cv, UMass); or switch to BERTopic / HDP for auto-K.

⑤ LLM 幻覺引用

GPT-4 可能編造 "PMID 12345678 — Smith 2019" 完全不存在的論文。修正：所有引用必須走 RAG 驗證真實 PubMed PMID；MedRAG 框架可降低 80% 幻覺。

GPT-4 may invent "PMID 12345678 — Smith 2019" that doesn't exist. Fix: every citation routed through RAG against real PubMed PMIDs; MedRAG cuts hallucination by 80%.

⑥ BioBERT 直接用，沒驗證

BioBERT NER 可能把 "Cold" 誤標為疾病而非「感冒」；領域漂移（病歷 vs PubMed）。修正：用自己領域 500 筆人工標註驗證；必要時做 domain adaptation 或 ClinicalBERT。

BioBERT may mistag "Cold" as a disease vs the common cold; domain shift (notes vs PubMed). Fix: validate on 500 own-domain manual labels; consider domain adaptation or ClinicalBERT.

壞做法 vs 好做法

壞做法

直接餵 GPT-4 100 個病歷段落，用 prompt "幫我找心肌炎相關"，輸出貼進論文當證據——沒有驗證引用、沒有 ground truth 對照、沒有領域專家複檢。

Pipe 100 clinical paragraphs into GPT-4 with "find me myocarditis evidence", paste the output into a paper — no citation validation, no ground truth, no expert review.

好做法

三層 pipeline：(1) TF-IDF + BM25 初步過濾；(2) PubMedBERT embedding + 向量檢索；(3) LLM 摘要必經 MedRAG 驗證 PMID；最後請臨床專家在 380 篇隨機抽 50 篇人工檢核 precision/recall，並記錄在 Methods。

Three-stage pipeline: (1) TF-IDF + BM25 first-pass filter; (2) PubMedBERT embeddings + vector retrieval; (3) LLM summarisation via MedRAG with PMID verification; clinician spot-checks 50 of 380 for precision/recall, all logged in Methods.
